Colin here. Like many red blooded American kids, I had a baseball card collection. Here’s what I remember most from this phase of my life: The battle to find super rare cards (like the Michael Jordan one), and also, the product differentiation of the actual cards from brand to brand. I seem to remember Upper Deck feeling super premium—glossier and sharper than some of the other versions, like Fleer or Topps. But there’s one baseball card rarity that I’d forgotten, until I was recently reminded of it in a group chat. It’s both a hilarious and playful anomaly.
