Instead of playing some dumb game or doomscrolling, it’s a nice five-minute distraction when needed. (NRB)

Apple News+
There are some parts of Apple News that still deeply bother me (mainly the fact you can’t share links to actual news websites, only deep links into the app itself). However, I recently realized that between the different subscriptions I had to various Apple and iCloud products, I could upgrade my Apple One subscription [ https://email.mg2.substack.com/c/eJwlkMGOhSAMRb_msRsDCKgLFrOZ3yBV-pSMooE6xr8feCYE2pTb9twJCOc93fbYM7F6OboPtBGvvCIRJnZmTC54K5Q2bSsG5q3yotc9C9m9E-IGYbWUTmTHOa5hAgp7rIqOc84W64Xw2I8AXJiuUyN_eyONGqTySgowz1g4fcA4ocU_TPcekU37tmGk2omtdiE68qv9fsmfcq7rauA4VmzKr5J_4q-iKjELVnIpuRFKlA2EbtpGcG36N4dRdehB8Jfi2yybfI6ZYPqtXViyIS8Qm8pT6nN9P4WC5eoyZwx0O4wwrugfYnqM-3jgZoyYiqHeAVlhtJac94PWRj2IBUQPrRrMYFiZ7PeiivZa7pBpCTnEYjdmCnH-B0H6iYw ] to the one that includes News+. So I did. 
What I discovered, and have been really enjoying, is the audio tab. It’s full of articles from various high-quality publications read by real people. It’s got speed options (I prefer 1.5x) and makes for a nice break from podcasts and a great way to consume some of my favorite pubs in a new way. (NRB)
