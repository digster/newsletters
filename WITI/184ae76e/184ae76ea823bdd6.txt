Colin here. A quick rundown of interesting things as you plan your holiday shopping. A more detailed WITI gift guide will follow later in December.
Michael Williams from ACL [ https://substack.com/redirect/90bf3fb5-d4d9-4d27-90b3-c9b2ef8296ef?r=8o54n ] highlights this interesting City Radio [ https://substack.com/redirect/4e41a560-384a-4cb6-9a27-7348ebc98abb?r=8o54n ], that allows you to tune into global radio stations. You should pair it with this WITI [ https://substack.com/redirect/970db37e-e588-4865-83a7-f1ee0cab70d7?r=8o54n ] on great stations to check out from around the world. 
The Bar Burger from Raoul’s. This is a hit gift. At Raoul’s they only offer up a limited amount per night. But this is a pretty damn good version [ https://substack.com/redirect/cc8141e4-a80e-4841-963f-8d44cbf5dfa6?r=8o54n ], to be made at home via Goldbelly. Read more on it in our WITI, the mail order sandwich edition.  [ https://substack.com/redirect/4434004b-6184-4b23-b4a5-e1e35b492774?r=8o54n ]
Patina Modern: a guide to designing warm, timeless interiors [ https://substack.com/redirect/c1b60332-8d77-4a57-9921-241c5dd92242?r=8o54n ]
Australian Food by Bill Granger [ https://substack.com/redirect/d53c0d13-eb2e-48ba-933e-952311703348?r=8o54n ]. I love all of his restaurants (especially Bills in Tokyo) and this cookbook has some gems. The chickpea kale and paneer curry is excellent. 
Big Ranch, Big City cookbook [ https://substack.com/redirect/c011146f-88c1-44ba-b5c2-4d66d7fc4aad?r=8o54n ] by Lou Lambert. Lou is the brother of Liz, a great hotelier, and a friend of WITI. 
Kindle Paperwhite [ https://substack.com/redirect/e8205b9e-eb64-4df4-8336-70bfba618a25?r=8o54n ]: it turns out that reading without notifications and distractions is amazing. Since they first launched, Kindles allow you to get into a flow state. 
An overkill  bottle opener.  [ https://substack.com/redirect/4f424893-1bf5-436e-a71a-44845b877aca?r=8o54n ]
A cult Swiss peeler [ https://substack.com/redirect/36c50346-c5ab-4f96-bb1f-7079a9f4e278?r=8o54n ], recommended by Brooks Reitz. [ https://substack.com/redirect/4933574e-86ca-4527-a026-e792b642ed17?r=8o54n ]
An embassy pen [ https://substack.com/redirect/a9aa2d97-fcec-4d9a-8992-aa531b376aa6?r=8o54n ]. Ikyyk. Just don’t take it on the plane. 
A nice titanium money clip [ https://substack.com/redirect/9224c5d5-133a-41df-9304-1ea6c9c4af0d?r=8o54n ] that I have gifted several times.

Royal Braun connects cutting edge companies with key creative talent. Meet Royal Braun [ https://substack.com/redirect/84acf285-927e-43c0-b293-645b2670e052?r=8o54n ]
Nudge, get customer insights with ease. Try Nudge [ https://substack.com/redirect/d721e195-55d7-44e3-b9e5-59e8a38f58d3?r=8o54n ]
