Chris Papasadero is professional dilettante [ https://substack.com/redirect/747c0f5a-4a4f-479c-b61e-ad5bacc7815c?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], former Green Beret [ https://substack.com/redirect/cb53895d-0649-4ccf-bfc4-0bc1d12d495d?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], and co-author of one of WITI’s most popular article, The Go Bag Edition [ https://substack.com/redirect/d7f69d4d-c1fa-4d78-88c4-90816ada0f1e?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
Chris here. Just because you’re prepping for the apocalypse, doesn’t mean you can’t exercise a little taste. Given that there’s a strong risk you might still die within the first week of a major catastrophe, you may as well enjoy it. To help, I’ve prepared this companion list to my original Go Bag [ https://substack.com/redirect/d7f69d4d-c1fa-4d78-88c4-90816ada0f1e?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] — TP and bottled water not included.
Once you’ve converted your parking strip into a micro wheat field, you’ll need some way to process it into flour. Behold, the manual grain mill [ https://substack.com/redirect/c06948ef-881d-4be5-b72a-81f39632d923?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. If you have a generator, consider an attachment [ https://substack.com/redirect/9c420fb0-fab7-45e9-8237-ae5dc2a93ff4?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] for your stand mixer.
Outdoor wood ovens [ https://substack.com/redirect/4b3f2662-104d-471a-9936-2b3c732de1e5?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] are great, but why not take some inspiration from Francis Mallmann and learn to cook on an open fire [ https://substack.com/redirect/a44cec6e-e2e0-4254-9317-d160bd22e135?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]? You can construct a Calder-style wire mobile like him [ https://substack.com/redirect/b7a43a87-e8d1-4c37-8e90-a7211802de47?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], buy an adjustable-height grill [ https://substack.com/redirect/c8595e8d-2014-4282-b23b-717d6d383636?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], or just use chicken wire and proper fire-zone management [ https://substack.com/redirect/26a78b1d-f511-43f8-ae6d-295d9843dbe7?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
You’ll drink or trade your booze away within the first 72 hours, so you’ll want to replenish with a home brewing kit [ https://substack.com/redirect/7937ecd9-2710-43cc-a24f-2b60c36bca94?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. (Mead is even easier.) Here are some important [ https://substack.com/redirect/e09f11a8-150e-4701-a603-ef1f91a9589f?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] books [ https://substack.com/redirect/d395157a-40e4-4b95-b85a-0a058d4534c2?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] on [ https://substack.com/redirect/ac4c83ee-85c8-45fa-be93-98abe00e7170?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] home [ https://substack.com/redirect/5bf663d0-1a77-4ad1-8cca-46436d5e14c1?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] brewing [ https://substack.com/redirect/231e87ed-bbdb-446c-95bb-0d0fe65aaa7f?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. Note: I never bought a full kit, just built one out of stuff from Webstraunt Store [ https://substack.com/redirect/06f93646-2c20-4dbf-92fc-8c73cdf35654?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] and Home Depot.
Get a Nanopresso [ https://substack.com/redirect/e4fabd72-4c29-401b-ac0d-64cb23b3739b?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] or Flair [ https://substack.com/redirect/0e15e6b8-13bf-4d8f-bc85-1e9c504d369b?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] manual espresso maker and burr grinder [ https://substack.com/redirect/1e11e0e8-3d5b-4bc4-b5a5-05429295406d?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] – assuming you’ve still got a guy who’s smuggling arabica up from Brazil.
A wine cave [ https://substack.com/redirect/c0d266cf-f5a8-44e5-b209-4b3021550cfa?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] is good for protecting your magnums and your magnums. Also good for dry [ https://substack.com/redirect/60f9ed10-33c2-41db-b309-74edc7d58c94?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] curing [ https://substack.com/redirect/88c8a637-6929-4673-9089-e8a9258ce2a0?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] at home [ https://substack.com/redirect/ce40c945-0f6c-4806-8c4b-f3c89af44c30?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. Build one [ https://substack.com/redirect/20610880-87e8-42a8-8fdb-9516f6db936c?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] while you can, or at least get yourself a nice locking storage rack [ https://substack.com/redirect/c3395944-0c6b-47b1-ae11-768d5fc3fba3?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. Worst case, you can always get on your ‘Root Cellaring’ and dig a hole [ https://substack.com/redirect/2a3540f1-62c9-4555-8781-8e8c75149751?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
Plant a victory garden [ https://substack.com/redirect/3b75527f-83dd-4944-a2e5-225d9800cd56?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], or at least a herb garden. Don’t forget something to keep away the rabbits and raiders [ https://substack.com/redirect/83bec6c0-e360-4a0f-a2b0-97db4bd49089?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
Buy a bunch of meat [ https://substack.com/redirect/46f03615-f8bc-4f15-a087-4dc0239bb865?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], store it, and dry it. (I’m still looking for a halfway decent recipe for pemmican [ https://substack.com/redirect/472c7715-1ab8-449c-bebf-2d30b8e44df5?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], if anyone has any pointers).
Learn to hunt and fish [ https://substack.com/redirect/35066121-fb34-45fe-b950-9a6cbcfdb682?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] by asking your local groundskeeper or yeoman to teach you. After you’ve felled your first stag, learn to butcher it [ https://substack.com/redirect/76502ee1-33f1-41c7-aecc-482f45f28f6f?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
Speaking of learning, there’s a whole host of prepper books out there, and most of them are useless. I don’t have any recommendations other than Where There Is No Doctor [ https://substack.com/redirect/7626b78f-3f6b-4889-ac9c-fafb3ba37eb1?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] and the entire Foxfire series [ https://substack.com/redirect/3ebeacbd-3ca1-426b-98d9-d58e13b39c74?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. While you’re at it, get a copy of the Iliad in Greek and the Liddel and Scott Lexicon [ https://substack.com/redirect/8f3cd342-be04-472f-b9e5-e564cc630b5d?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] and work your way through the translation on your own. Also, consider downloading Kiwix [ https://substack.com/redirect/f04dc6e2-4ec1-4ad9-a222-0146a48b1b26?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] and keeping the whole of Wikipedia on a local drive — just be sure to store it in a waterproof, fireproof, EMP-proof faraday bag [ https://substack.com/redirect/4c558e3c-7531-45d8-8fc0-252a52c29ef9?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. Other resources for restoring a civilized post-collapse civilization include forums like r/datahoarder [ https://substack.com/redirect/9322f993-8070-4585-b0ce-18008ff24dfc?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] and the offline version of Project Gutenberg. [ https://substack.com/redirect/1f491cfd-125f-40fe-98e8-30f3e5315d61?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]
Get a printed map of your area, ideally a Rand McNally Road Atlas [ https://substack.com/redirect/ff5bd402-1203-42d9-b13c-55f631794fc9?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. Mark all the best places for foraging truffles and trading ammo for cheese [ https://substack.com/redirect/ddc07650-cee2-4054-bd12-7a1514400f95?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
Speaking of cheese, this is the best, most scientific book [ https://substack.com/redirect/90211419-79a7-4165-80ca-76b06cbeb2db?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] on making it. Befriend your local dairy farmer.
