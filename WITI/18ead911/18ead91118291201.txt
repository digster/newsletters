Colin here. I’m experimenting with a lighter weight Friday format since we give you a lot to read every week. A recommended Substacks for your time and attention:
Emily Sundberg writes Feed me [ https://substack.com/redirect/be910de8-a77f-454e-9e22-770d05656780?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], and it is superb. I get a lot of morning emails but this is now a can’t miss for me. She gives a fun roundup of business, pop culture, and startups with a very sharp eye and POV. She knows what to notice.
FOR SCALE  by David Michon "celebrates the supreme potential of domestic space.” It is a super smart and tasteful read on design and interiors. 
Jason Stewart  of How Long Gone fame has (re) started a ‘stack. I like his quick sense of humor and sniper shot observations on the pod and I think his food writing is a also a real talent. Start with some of his pieces in Taste for an amuse-bouche.  [ https://substack.com/redirect/134b7ced-25f0-4c95-a1bd-6cbe0bafcb39?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]

Sky high taste levels, 10/10 would recommend.
