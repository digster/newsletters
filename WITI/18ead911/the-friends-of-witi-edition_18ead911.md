Colin here. I’m experimenting with a lighter weight Friday format since we give you a lot to read every week. A recommended Substacks for your time and attention:
[Emily](https://open.substack.com/users/9237884-emily?utm_source=mentions)Sundberg writes [Feed me](https://substack.com/redirect/be910de8-a77f-454e-9e22-770d05656780?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o), and it is superb. I get a lot of morning emails but this is now a can’t miss for me. She gives a fun roundup of business, pop culture, and startups with a very sharp eye and POV. She knows what to notice.
[FOR SCALE](https://substack.com/redirect/d130fad9-1198-4634-bc9c-6d4988715510?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by David Michon "celebrates the supreme potential of domestic space.” It is a super smart and tasteful read on design and interiors.
[Jason Stewart](https://open.substack.com/users/3376256-jason-stewart?utm_source=mentions) of How Long Gone fame has (re) started a ‘stack. I like his quick sense of humor and sniper shot observations on the pod and I think his food writing is a also a real talent. Start with some of his pieces in [Taste for an amuse-bouche. ](https://substack.com/redirect/134b7ced-25f0-4c95-a1bd-6cbe0bafcb39?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o)
[Brooks Reitz](https://substack.com/redirect/fea57fcc-ddd9-4617-9c91-0ef5a9aed75e?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) writes [A Small and Simple Thing](https://substack.com/redirect/7c74fa5d-a45c-4627-ae87-b9ae0fa9c8c3?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o), which is a constant read for me. Sky high taste levels, 10/10 would recommend. His MMD was [also great](https://substack.com/redirect/2c758992-b03f-4311-9951-318ff2dc3bdb?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o).
Also a shout-out to the great [Louis Cheslaw](https://substack.com/redirect/3e60e683-1ab0-43b1-931d-639f1f6ee575?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) who has been helping us elevate the standard of WITI recently. He is the guy to call for editorial projects/consulting/general taste and excellence. Find him [here](https://substack.com/redirect/8fb23c78-b3fd-448c-b8d9-e0f213849a49?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o).
