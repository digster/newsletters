America’s Biggest Bottleneck of the Decade

While news cycles focus on the latest AI model weights and GPU availability, not enough attention has gone to the physical machine that makes everything possible...

Chamath Palihapitiya
Feb 27
 
READ IN APP
 

Energy is the fundamental variable of civilization. It powers our shelters, drives emergency services, and supports every major technological leap.

While news cycles focus on the latest AI model weights and GPU availability, not enough attention has gone to the physical machine that makes everything possible.

The U.S. electric grid is the largest interconnected machine in the world, but engineers rate it a D+, and now it stands as America’s biggest technological bottleneck.

In addition to our Deep Dive in December, today, my team and I created two new exciting resources to help you learn with me.

I released a video explaining our critical grid problem and what the U.S. must do to secure one of our biggest national security risks:

The video is only 8 mins, but it will give you 80-90% of what you need to know about the energy/grid problem. Watch and let me know what you think.

Alongside our new video, we’re excited to introduce a brand-new way to experience our Deep Dive:

The U.S. Grid Web Experience (Beta): grid.socialcapital.com

We’ve transformed our slide deck into a dynamic web experience, complete with interactive charts, clickable data toggles, and integrated media playback.

While I think our weekly 60-150 page Deep Dives are some of the most comprehensive yet accessible decks you can find, this web experience could be our “Raptor 2 engine” moment for Learn With Me.

I think this web experience makes LwM 10x more engaging and accessible.

Because I think it can be a game-changer, I decided to make it free for everyone: try it for yourself here and let me know what you think.

If the community is as excited about this as I am, we will do our best integrate this experience into future Deep Dives.
