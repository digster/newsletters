Teaching my son Finance and Investing – Part 1: Joys of Compounding

by Jana Vembunarayanan

Time and tide waits for none.

Eighteen joyful and memorable years have passed since my elder son Vibhu was born.

Vibhu graduated from 12th grade yesterday, and he turned 18 today.

On his birthday, I started teaching Vibhu whatever little I know about finance and investing.

We thought it's a good idea to record my teachings and share them. I will be delighted even if one kid gets benefited from these presentations. In part 1, I teach him the importance of compounding and how to use it to our advantage. We will do 2-3 more recordings, and I will share them in the next week or two.
