Everyone knows that saving more and eating healthy is beneficial in the long term. But why do we fail to follow it?

In this presentation, Vibhu and I discuss how temptations, envy, and procrastination prevent us from reaching our goals. We talk about techniques to minimize these obstacles and reach our goals.
