Everyone says they hate the question: What do you do? It’s cliché and reductive. But nobody stops asking it, and nobody stops answering. Because the answer is never just about what you do. It’s about what people think you’re worth, and what you want people to think you’re worth. A few years ago, as far as the tech world is concerned, the winning answer sounded like ‘I’m starting something’ or ‘We just closed a $1M seed round’ or ‘I’m all in on crypto.’ The unifying brag: I take risks. I don’t need a safety net. Now the winning answer sounds a lot more like ‘I’m at OpenAI’ or ‘I’m building something new at Stripe’ or ‘We just raised a $20M seed round.’ The brag used to be the bet; now the brag is the safety net — the best one you can get. |
