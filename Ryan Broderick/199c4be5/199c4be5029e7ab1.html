Hi again, Managing Editor Cates here,

We promised not to bug you, our dear free readers, too much, but we recently announced some big changes to Garbage Day and we also have some exciting announcements for you. So forgive the email!

First, and most importantly, for folks who may have missed the news, we’ve shifted from two free issues a week to one, the Monday issue. We’re not going to flood your inbox about this too often, but we thought a reminder would be good, considering the switch happened earlier this month.

We’re putting more behind the paywall. So consider upgrading if you want more Garbage Day in your inbox and access to the Discord where a fun group of people hang out and share, well, a lot. It’s a great community that I’ve gotten to know well over the last few months, even if there are far too many requests to reveal Ryan’s shoe size. We recently held a Power Point Party and it was a blast, so we’re going to do it again soon!
