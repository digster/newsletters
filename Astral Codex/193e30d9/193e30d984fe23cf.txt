Each year, I post a reader survey. This helps me learn who’s reading this blog. But it also helps me try to replicate a bunch of psych findings, and investigate interesting hypotheses. Some highlights from past years include birth order effects [ https://substack.com/redirect/262759a8-9601-45ca-8402-a75595078f07?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], mathematical interests vs. corn-eating style [ https://substack.com/redirect/e0e05f73-d16f-490d-aec6-5cc5c6a9777b?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], sexual harassment victimization rates in different fields [ https://substack.com/redirect/723195ae-3781-420d-a0d4-4c3629419c92?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], whether all our kids are going to have autism [ https://substack.com/redirect/c6b27c4d-60fc-4390-8530-16d7ba3ae89f?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], wisdom of inner crowds [ https://substack.com/redirect/c0fa94eb-1b30-4f5f-ab18-7d791a941b77?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], failed replication of anti-vax polls [ https://substack.com/redirect/25c66a96-008b-4af2-83a8-a51608dd5f74?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], and Internet addiction [ https://substack.com/redirect/d5fdfe6b-3c44-4188-8529-bb4e0acdd62d?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
