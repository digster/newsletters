1: Some good pushback to my post [Most Technologies Aren’t Races](https://substack.com/redirect/03c6542b-bf6e-4c20-8905-80cdc05e3ad5?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o), arguing that even my examples of historical non-race technologies were races at the time. See especially [Erusian](https://substack.com/redirect/abc10f96-84f3-40dd-a6b8-59d9373bf2bf?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o), [Tatu Ahponen](https://substack.com/redirect/5d338d93-2b48-4990-b153-a9cca802a4da?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o), [Jumpingjacksplash](https://substack.com/redirect/3742fbe4-c816-4acf-b4da-885533d6efd5?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o), and (thanks to anonymous emailer) [Ding & Dafoe (2023)](https://substack.com/redirect/70b7c822-7ec3-41d7-9265-e015da56f369?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) (Twitter summary [here](https://substack.com/redirect/909cff92-4b02-422d-a1da-7df0c60c74b0?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o)). I agree any technology with military applications is more than zero a race; I still think under most assumptions it’s only a race in the same sense that stealth aircraft are a race, rather than the existential threat you would get if you imported singularitarian scenarios and took out the singularitarian bits.
