A New Way to Think About Your Life

So, what is The 5 Types of Wealth all about?

It's about rejecting the default and living life by design.

It's about realizing that your wealthy life may involve money, but in the end, it will be defined by everything else.

In this book, I offer a new way for you to think about your life centered around five types of wealth:

  • Time Wealth
  • Social Wealth
  • Mental Wealth
  • Physical Wealth
  • Financial Wealth

A new way to measure what matters, make better decisions, and design your life around the pillars that truly create lasting joy and fulfillment.

In each section of the book—one for each type of wealth—you'll find:

  • The Big Question: A question to help frame the discussion and spark dialogue amongst you and your loved ones as you consider your own priorities and vision.
  • History & Context: How humans recognized and interacted with each type of wealth—from ancient times to modern times—and how that through line informs our present struggle with it.
  • Pillars: The three core pillars of each type of wealth.
  • Action Guide: The specific, actionable, high-leverage systems to help you build that type of wealth in your life. Science-backed, proven strategies you can start using today to take action and create growth.

Throughout the book, you'll find powerful stories from real people (many of them readers of this newsletter!) who I had a chance to spend time with over the course of the last few years. They have faced all variety of challenges, heartbreak, and happiness on their journey—but their wisdom to define life on their own terms shines a light for all of us on our path.

Importantly, this book will not give you the answers. It will give you the right questions so that you can uncover and act on them.

While the lens through which you view them will be individual, the stories, questions, ideas, and tools contained in this book are universal.

No matter who you are, or where you are on your journey, this book is for you.

It will help you design and build a life of truly comprehensive wealth:

  • Control over how you spend your time
  • Depth and breadth of connection with those around you
  • Clarity of purpose, presence, and growth
  • Improved health and vitality
  • Financial independence of a state of "enough"

You’re going to find immense value in this book. I guarantee it.
