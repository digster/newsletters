Friday: The Crab Mentality: A Hidden Psychological Trap

If you place a single crab in a bucket, it’ll claw its way up the side and escape.

But if you place five crabs in a bucket, something different happens: When one crab starts clawing its way up the side to escape, the others reach out and pull it back down. None of the crabs escape the bucket.

As you’ve probably realized, this isn’t just about crabs…it’s a metaphor for an all-too-common experience in our lives:

“If I can’t have it, neither can you.”

I call it the Crab Mentality...
