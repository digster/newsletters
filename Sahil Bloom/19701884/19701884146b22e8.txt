Friday: The Dunning-Kruger Effect
---------------------------------

Ever notice how the people who know the least often seem to be
the most confident in their knowledge?

You're observing the Dunning-Kruger Effect.

Incompetence doesn’t just lead to poor decisions—it also blocks
the awareness needed to recognize them.

It’s a dangerous double whammy—and understanding it may allow you
to avoid its wrath.
