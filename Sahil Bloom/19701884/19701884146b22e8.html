Monday: A Question to Reset Your Life

Here’s a question I like to ask myself when I feel stuck:

If an outside observer watched me for a week, how serious would they say I am about achieving my goals?

It’s easy to convince yourself that you're doing what’s necessary—that you're showing up with discipline, energy, and focus.

But what would an unbiased observer see?

Friday: The Dunning-Kruger Effect

Ever notice how the people who know the least often seem to be the most confident in their knowledge?

You're observing the Dunning-Kruger Effect.

Incompetence doesn’t just lead to poor decisions—it also blocks the awareness needed to recognize them.

It’s a dangerous double whammy—and understanding it may allow you to avoid its wrath.
