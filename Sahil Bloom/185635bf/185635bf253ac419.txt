5 Life Lessons:
---------------

Your entire life can change with one year of focused daily
effort. Most people overestimate what they can do in a day and
underestimate what they can do in a year. If you show up every
single day, there are no limits to what you can achieve.

If you think something nice about someone, always tell them right
then. It's a shame that we often wait until a person's funeral to

you have a positive thought about someoneтАФno matter how small or
insignificant it may beтАФtell them right then.

You never know when it will be.

Regret is way more painful than failure. When in doubt, act.

Never let the quest for MORE distract from the beauty of ENOUGH.
Pursue growth, but never forget the beauty in simplicity.

Write down three things youтАЩre grateful for every single night
