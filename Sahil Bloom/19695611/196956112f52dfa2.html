Monday: The Hidden Tax Holding You Back

There are certain taxes of life—like loneliness, hard conversations, and imposter syndrome—that you must happily pay to achieve the things you want. But there is one tax that must be rejected: The Ignorance Tax.

The Ignorance Tax is the hidden cost of what you haven’t learned or what you’re choosing to ignore. It’s the gap between where you are and where you could be if you gained the knowledge, skills, perspective, or awareness you’re currently lacking.

Where are you paying the Ignorance Tax in your life right now?

Identify it. Address it. Eliminate it.
