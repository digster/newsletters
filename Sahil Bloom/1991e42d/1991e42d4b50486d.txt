Monday: What Will Your Next Note Be?
------------------------------------

I recently read a quote from legendary musician Miles Davis that
stopped me in my tracks…

“It’s not the note you play that’s the wrong note—it’s the note
you play afterwards that makes it right or wrong.”
This isn’t just about music. This is about life.

No matter how much you’ve prepared for the moment, wrong notes
happen. The mistake. The failure. The setback.

But the quote reminds us of an important truth:

The misstep doesn’t define you. Your next move does...

Wednesday: The Victim Mentality: A Silent Killer of Growth
----------------------------------------------------------

I think of Victim Mentality as a mindset in which a person
consistently views themselves as the target of external forces,
believing that their challenges and failures are primarily a
byproduct of circumstances outside their control.

Victim Mentality is anti-agency. It’s a belief that you are not
in control. That you are a passenger in your own journey.

It’s a belief that life happens to you.

In this piece, I wrote about this mindset and shared three simple
strategies to eliminate it forever...

Friday: The Snake Chasing Effect
--------------------------------

In life, you’re going to get bitten by a snake every now and
then.

Someone betrays you. A partner lies. A family member mistreats
you. A friend lets you down. A coworker takes credit for your
work.

The initial wound hurts—but it’s survivable. The real damage is
not from the bite itself, but from your reaction to it.

The real damage comes when you chase the snake.

This piece offers a reminder that the wisest response to harm
isn’t retaliation or obsession, but restoration...
