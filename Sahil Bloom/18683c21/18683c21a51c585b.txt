String of Pearls Theory
-----------------------

Stutz is a 2022 Netflix documentary directed by actor Jonah Hill
that takes a deep dive into the life and teachings of his
therapist, Dr. Phil Stutz.

‚Äã
The film has a very rare 100% rating on Rotten Tomatoes for good
reason‚Äîit is exceptional in its exploration of a variety of tools
and frameworks on life and career.

One very interesting theory covered: the String of Pearls.

Stutz draws a string of pearls on a piece of paper.

Each "pearl" is an action taken, with each action having equal
value. No action is more or less valuable than any other‚Äîall
actions are to be celebrated as an important part of creating the
string of pearls.

The important point here is just creating an identity around
taking the action:

"I am the person that puts the next pearl on the string.‚Äù
Just as we learned from the Parable of the Farmer and the Horse,
we don't need to place judgement on whether any action is "good"
or "bad"‚Äîwe just need to take the action and let it be.

No action is completely perfect‚Äîno action is completely
imperfect. Every action has the same value. Every action is a
pearl on your long, ever-expanding string of life.

Lesson: Each and every single day, add one perfectly imperfect
pearl to your string.
