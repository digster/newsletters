Wisdom Worth Sharing:

A lesson I wish I learned earlier:

Risk ages you, but so does regret.

The difference is that risk leaves the marks of meaningful struggle, while regret leaves the marks of unlived potential.

Choose wisely.

Monday: The Question That May Change Your Life

At 12 years old, Steve Jobs cold-called Bill Hewlett and landed a life-changing opportunity. How? Simply because he had the courage to ask.

What would you ask for if you weren't afraid of hearing no?

The life you want is on the other side of the fear you’re avoiding.

Friday: The Hidden Bias Keeping You Stuck

We all do it—we hold on to jobs, relationships, identities, and goals long after they’ve stopped serving us. Why? Because of the Endowment Effect: Our tendency to overvalue what we already have.
