Friday: A Life-Changing Metaphor: Rocks, Pebbles & Sand
-------------------------------------------------------

Every year around the holidays, I take some time to set my
intentions for the year ahead.

There's a simple story I came across years ago that has proven
helpful in this exercise year after year.

A professor fills a glass jar—first with rocks, then pebbles,
then sand—to illustrate a powerful truth: The jar represents your
life.

The rocks are your most important things—your family, your
health, your purpose, your core values. The pebbles are
meaningful but secondary. The sand is everything else—the noise,
the busywork, the distractions.

The lesson is clear: If you pour the sand in first, you'll never
have room for the rocks.
