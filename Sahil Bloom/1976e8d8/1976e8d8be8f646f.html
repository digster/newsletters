Monday: The Hidden Cost of Certainty

Tolerance for uncertainty is the most valuable human trait.

The real rewards in life go to those who can show up when the rewards are uncertain—who take the next step even when they can’t see where their foot is going to land.

Where are you accepting good because you're afraid to tolerate the uncertainty necessary to reach great?

Your answer might change everything.
