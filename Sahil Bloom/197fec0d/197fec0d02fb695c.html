Monday: A Simple Reframe to Improve Your Life

We’ve all felt it—that familiar sensation of feeling behind.

But it’s not comparison itself that’s the problem—it’s the obsessive focus on where you are right now. The snapshot. The point in time.

That focus blinds you to something far more important: Your trend.

Stop self-inflicting misery with position-thinking. Return to your craft. Stack tiny wins until the graph can’t hide them.

Trust in the trend.

Wednesday: 3 Steps to Stop Running From Your True Potential

Sometimes you don't give your full energy to something because you're afraid of what will happen if it works out.

You're afraid of seeing what you're truly capable of. Afraid of realizing your full potential goes far beyond the limiting stories you've been telling yourself. Afraid of never being able to go back to the safety of those stories.

Psychologist Abraham Maslow called it the Jonah Complex: the fear of our own light.

But to fear your own light is to live in the dark. And that’s not where you belong.

You're meant for more. Start acting like it.

Friday: How to Live an Artful Life

This is a beautiful Latin phrase:

Ars longa; vita brevis.

Translation: Art is long, life is short.

It isn't simply about art, but about living an artful life.

To live artfully is to live differently. To wake up early. To walk slowly. To listen intently. To stand proudly. To focus deeply.

To live artfully is to treat the ordinary with the sort of reverence typically reserved for the extraordinary.

So, live artfully.
