An investor I admire recently listed three reasons to sell a stock:
- You were wrong
- You’ve already been paid too well
- The opportunity cost is too high
This insight goes far beyond investing. These are the same reasons to “sell” anything in life—a job, relationship, or an identity. It's good to know when to buy, but knowing when to sell is how you get the highest Return on Life.
