Monday: The Cheshire Cat Principle: How to Create Clarity
---------------------------------------------------------

In Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland, there’s a scene in which
Alice reaches a fork in the road and has a simple exchange with
the Cheshire Cat:

“Would you tell me, please, which way I ought to go from here?”
​
“That depends a good deal on where you want to get to,” said the
Cat.
​
“I don’t much care where—” said Alice.
​
“Then it doesn’t matter which way you go,” said the Cat.

It’s a clever exchange, but holds a deep truth:

If you aren’t clear on where you want to go, any road will take
you there.
