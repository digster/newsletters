Because the next step after cryptocurrencies is cryptocommunities.
From Cryptocurrencies to Cryptocommunities
Network states are becoming real.
We wrote our book [ https://substack.com/redirect/452a7152-cdf4-4df2-8cb8-0cd4aa8750ba?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] in 2022, held our first conference [ https://substack.com/redirect/a562763e-1e0d-4dcc-9131-8d845ac6bf2d?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] in 2023, and opened our school [ https://substack.com/redirect/fe395af8-d8cc-4cd0-9aeb-4d69416b2ae8?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] in 2024. Now, we have a movement. From tiny startups to global organizations, many different actors are now opening up physical popups, starting startup societies, materializing internet communities, and negotiating special economic zones.
This year’s conference documents that movement. We have Vitalik Buterin and Ethereum cofounder Mihai Aislie on network-state inspired [ https://substack.com/redirect/dd449b53-a87c-47b5-9c1f-ae1abb19b3b0?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] popups like Zuzalu [ https://substack.com/redirect/3442f685-05e8-4273-8c8f-c52559955892?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] and Etherlaken [ https://substack.com/redirect/50a6d2be-15dd-480b-8193-85a96054bedf?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. We have Brian Armstrong and Xen Baynham-Herd of Coinbase on Basecamp [ https://substack.com/redirect/8b320efc-4dc5-4404-8db4-f040380a59ed?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] and the Base Network State [ https://substack.com/redirect/e05fa90d-6165-49ee-887b-f94d2d98484b?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. We have Akshay BD and Farhaj Mahan from Solana on Forma and the new Solana Economic Zones [ https://substack.com/redirect/b3c2d39e-27f2-463f-aca2-e9b40d099c55?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. We have Veronika Kapustina, CEO of Telegram’s new TON Strategy Co, on the path to the Telegram Network State [ https://substack.com/redirect/3913b8a6-3920-4c5c-a085-80168d1fe56b?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. And we have Bryan Johnson speaking about longevity and the Don’t Die Network State [ https://substack.com/redirect/8c5d616f-a9bc-4583-978c-d3ed1f54f682?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
So: Ethereum, Coinbase, Solana, Telegram, and Don’t Die — all important online movements with billions of dollars and millions of followers — are materializing their cloud communities into the physical world. And they’ll be speaking about it at the Network State Conference [ https://substack.com/redirect/8fd5654d-e668-4b1b-abf1-b8c8b6977e06?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
Nation States + Network States
But wait, there’s more. Because the concept of the network state is also about win/win cooperation between nation states and digital networks. After all, many countries (particularly small states, and those from the ascending world [ https://substack.com/redirect/95279b9d-32e3-42ac-abcc-182110caf0d7?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]) want global technologists to bring capital, talent, and economic development to their jurisdictions.
Towards that end, we have talks by representatives of the governments of El Salvador, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Singapore. Stacy Herbert [ https://substack.com/redirect/dbd28d7f-ddc4-4bb4-86b6-02652ee45135?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] will speak about El Salvador’s Bitcoin office, its AI law, and its digital nomad programs. Rashid Mohammed [ https://substack.com/redirect/b1ef0446-380e-4254-a37d-84845634b1ef?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] will talk about Expo City, a startup city built in Dubai, while Wai-Lum Kwok [ https://substack.com/redirect/faaeeb3a-db80-43f2-9939-a418dec41d70?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] will discuss Abu Dhabi Global Markets (ADGM)’s pro-crypto regulation. And Jacqueline Poh [ https://substack.com/redirect/2eb7e778-1cbc-480c-b08c-ffbf7dcf8a25?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], chief of Singapore’s $40B JTC development arm, will speak about building the physical infrastructure and innovation parks that enable Singapore’s rapid economic growth.

The point is that many of the most successful founders, creators, and investors in the world are now interested in the idea of bringing online communities into the physical world. Moreover, writers, policymakers, and intellectuals from around the world (and across the ideological spectrum!) are aligning around the concept of fusing networks with states, with ideas ranging from pro-tech regulations to special innovation zones. And all this capital and talent going towards the area means that you too can join a startup society that suits you — or found one yourself.
Because that’s the next step. We’ve started internet companies, we’ve started internet currencies, and now we’re starting internet communities.
