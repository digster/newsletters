AMAZON IS GUTTING ITS VOICE-ASSISTANT ALEXA (10 MINUTE READ)
[https://archive.ph/16osE?utm_source=tldrnewsletter] 

Mounting losses and massive job cuts are leading to the downfall of
Amazon's Alexa voice assistant. Amazon's Worldwide Digital unit, which
includes Echo smart speakers and Alexa voice technology, had an
operating loss of over $3 billion during the first quarter of this
year. A vast majority of the losses were tied to Alexa and other
devices. The Alexa division is now the prime target for layoffs at the
company. Amazon's Senior Vice President for Devices and Services
claimed in a statement that the company remains committed to Alexa and
will continue to invest in the technology.
