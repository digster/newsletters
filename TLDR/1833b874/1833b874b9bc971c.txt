Chinese researchers are testing modified cars that can magnetically

Harvard's Office of Technology Development has granted an exclusive
technology license to Adden Energy to scale a solid-state
lithium-metal battery for commercial deployment. The battery prototype
can charge in three minutes and last over 10,000 charge cycles. Adden
aims to create a full-scale vehicle battery within the next five
years. Enabling electric vehicles to charge within minutes with
batteries that last for many years will make EV technology accessible
to many more people.
