BLOOM is a Large Language Model (LLM) that is available for free to
anyone. LLMs are prohibitively expensive to create and run. They are
rarely open-sourced, especially when they provide competitive
advantages. BLOOM's 176-billion-parameter model is larger than
OpenAI's GPT-3 and MetaAI's OPT. It will give researchers a unique
chance to explore the risks and benefits of LLMs. BLOOM can generate
text in 46 languages and dialects and 13 programming languages.
