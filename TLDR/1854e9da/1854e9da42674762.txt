MARKETS & BUSINESS

JUDGE KEPT COOPERATION OF ALAMEDA CEO, FTX CO-FOUNDER A SECRET SO SAM
BANKMAN-FRIED WOULDN’T GET SPOOKED AND FIGHT EXTRADITION (4 MINUTE
READ)
[https://fortune.com/2022/12/23/judge-alameda-ceo-ellison-ftx-cofounder-wang-sam-bankman-fried-extradition/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter]


US prosecutors waited until Sam Bankman-Fried was in FBI custody
before revealing that his business partners were cooperating with the
investigation. Carolyn Ellison and Gary Wang pleaded guilty to fraud
charges, possibly earning them leniency at sentencing. Prosecutors
were concerned that Bankman-Fried might have tried to fight
extradition from the Bahamas had he known that his friends were
cooperating. Bankman-Fried appeared in court in New York on Thursday
and was released on bail on the condition that he lives under house
arrest with his parents. 

CRYPTO IS DOWN BAD, BUT VCS KEEP POURING MONEY IN (8 MINUTE READ)
[https://decrypt.co/117900/crypto-is-down-bad-but-vcs-keep-pouring-money-in?utm_source=tldrnewsletter]


Venture capital investment in 2022 outweighs that of both fintech and
biotech. $6.5 billion has been invested into crypto projects over the
last 12 months, with $879 million of that investment in the last
quarter. VC firms have become far more cautious after this year's
events. Many are now focusing on decentralized technologies. There is
still plenty of money being invested into the industry, especially for
infrastructure projects.

INNOVATION & LAUNCHES

METAVERSE-FIRST BLOCKCHAIN LAMINA1 LAUNCHES ROLLING FUND FOR WEB3
BUILDERS (2 MINUTE READ)
[https://www.coindesk.com/web3/2022/12/21/metaverse-first-blockchain-lamina1-launches-layer-1-ecosystem-rolling-fund/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter]


Lamina1 is launching the Lamina1 Ecosystem Fund (L1EF) to help web3
builders raise capital for metaverse ventures and offer investors a
way to invest in projects at the ground level. The publicly accessible
ecosystem fund provides broad economic access to global accredited
investors. L1EF currently offers a subscription on AngelList starting
at $2,500 quarterly over a minimum period of four quarters. Lamina1
will soon launch a testnet to showcase future use cases. 

RIPPLE INSIDER SAYS SMART CONTRACTS COMING TO THE XRP LEDGER (2 MINUTE
READ)
[https://dailyhodl.com/2022/12/24/ripple-insider-says-smart-contracts-coming-to-the-xrp-ledger/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter]


According to a Ripple insider, XRP is being used daily for
international payments, NFTs, and web monetization. Its On-Demand
Liquidity settlement solution is being supported in countries
including Africa, Israel, Australia, Brazil, Argentina, Belgium,
Singapore, the UAE, and the UK. Ripple is making a push to bring smart
contracts to its ledger, XRPL. An EVM-compatible sidechain for XRPL is
currently being tested.

MISCELLANEOUS

BINANCE FAILING TO GET US EXCHANGE LISTINGS FOR BNB IS YELLOW FLAG FOR
CRYPTO ANALYSTS (5 MINUTE READ)
[https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2022/12/23/crypto-analysts-see-yellow-flag-in-us-exchanges-refusal-to-list-binances-bnb-token/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter]


Binance's BNB token has failed to win a listing on major US crypto
exchanges except on Binance.US. Some analysts speculate that exchanges
are avoiding listing BNB due to concerns with regulators. The token
may be seen as a security by regulators due to the centralization of
its network. Regulatory issues could represent a risk for BNB token
holders. 

THE END OF CRYPTO TWITTER AS WE KNOW IT? (6 MINUTE READ)
[https://www.coindesk.com/layer2/2022/12/23/the-end-of-crypto-twitter-as-we-know-it/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter]


Some sources estimate that Twitter will lose up to 10% of users in the
next two years. This could have an impact on engagement on the
platform. While Twitter has previously played a large role in crypto
discourse, the platform may end up being an echo chamber as those who
disagree with Twitter's policies leave. Other alternative platforms
may end up facilitating the debate and development of ideas in crypto.
