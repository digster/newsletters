Alibaba Releases AI Glasses in Rare Consumer Gadget Foray (3 minute read)

The glasses feature built-in translucent displays that superimpose contextual information on the wearer's view of their surroundings. NASA Rover Makes a Shocking Discovery: Lightning on Mars (6 minute read)
](http://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/hhttps:%2F%2Flinks.tldrnewsletter.com%2FTvC8tT/1/0100019aca346600-df28cc1b-a6a3-4448-aa70-f7f8c6971f5e-000000/ACmHgfAEh5EPc_kkeje61PvQB0Usclqw9CeOcAeUAjk=433)
Scientists have observed centimeter-scale electrical discharges on Mars. The sparks could play an important role in chemical reactions in the Martian atmosphere. They do not pose any danger of electrocution for humans. However, they could disrupt or damage electronic devices over time. Lightning has also previously been observed in the atmospheres of Jupiter and Saturn.

Study claims to provide first direct evidence of dark matter (3 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.theguardian.com%2Fscience%2F2025%2Fnov%2F25%2Fstudy-claims-to-provide-first-direct-evidence-of-dark-matter%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100019aca346600-df28cc1b-a6a3-4448-aa70-f7f8c6971f5e-000000/xGwvVYdAnfV6DG4IuRmcQAyg10Ea82QhrCtKRtP1Bb4=433)
The first evidence of dark matter may have been discovered. Scientists have spotted a pattern of gamma rays that appears to match the shape of the dark matter halo that spreads out in a sphere from the heart of the galaxy. It closely matches the properties of gamma-ray radiation predicted to be emitted by dark matter. More work is needed to rule out other astrophysical processes and background emissions that could explain the signals.

More Articles Are Now Created by AI Than Humans (6 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fgraphite.io%2Ffive-percent%2Fmore-articles-are-now-created-by-ai-than-humans%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100019aca346600-df28cc1b-a6a3-4448-aa70-f7f8c6971f5e-000000/57_zwWiN_szWjm33rJunQqQjjsq9GW7-hLD2GO8gL-Y=433)
