OpenAI upgrades Codex with a new version of GPT-5 (2 minute read)

Sam Altman-Backed Retro Biosciences Starts Alzheimer's Reversal Trial (4 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Flinks.tldrnewsletter.com%2FQ22O7N/1/01000199522e753a-814c2fdf-fef8-47b2-b322-4d9168525f98-000000/LrY5q0C6A_oxn4bIszLdmbJKGhikp0wY6UZt1BRnc2k=422)
Retro Biosciences is gearing up for a clinical trial of a drug aimed at reversing the effects of Alzheimer's disease. The drug could potentially restore cognitive function in ways that could redefine longevity treatments. Retro's approach leverages cellular reprogramming techniques. The upcoming trial will test the pill's efficacy in human subjects.

New Ultrasound Helmet Reaches Deep Inside The Brain Without Surgery (4 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.sciencealert.com%2Fnew-ultrasound-helmet-reaches-deep-inside-the-brain-without-surgery%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000199522e753a-814c2fdf-fef8-47b2-b322-4d9168525f98-000000/P06fbQRGFyXr2trl9eiT2woLObXy1tbcBmsu6FbJd8I=422)
Researchers have created an ultrasound helmet that could offer an alternative to invasive procedures. It can modulate deep-brain circuits without surgery with unrivaled precision. The helmet offers a safe, reversible, and repeatable method for both understanding brain function and developing targeted therapies. It has potential clinical applications for neurological disorders like Parkinson's disease, where deep-brain regions are especially affected.

The Last Days Of Social Media (32 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.noemamag.com%2Fthe-last-days-of-social-media%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000199522e753a-814c2fdf-fef8-47b2-b322-4d9168525f98-000000/PN1Map-rRMjGObHxH8YrSOr0l0OycXb9mmhCmaxOFzc=422)
