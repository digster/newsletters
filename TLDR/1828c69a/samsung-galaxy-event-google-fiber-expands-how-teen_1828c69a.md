[Samsung’s Galaxy Unpacked August 2022: the five biggest announcements (3 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.theverge.com%2F2022%2F8%2F10%2F23299070%2Fsamsung-galaxy-unpacked-august-2022-announcements-z-flip-fold-4-watch-5-pro-buds-2%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/010001828c69a4f7-7733de7e-4291-4b4b-9b94-55b0b23ce632-000000/vFdn3NyaT4wIDA88Qnuz3DB37-KyIdSx_i-vS9ApV7Y=261)
Samsung announced two foldables, new smart watches, and an update to its Galaxy Buds Pro line at its Galaxy Unpacked event. The Galaxy Z Flip 4 looks a lot like its predecessor. It comes with a few new software tricks and fast charging. The Galaxy Z Fold 4 is slightly lighter than its previous iteration and it features big changes to its camera array, including a new 50-megapixel main sensor. The Galaxy Watch 5 smartwatches have bigger batteries and the Galaxy Buds 2 Pro have a new design and support hi-fi audio.

[LG’s 97-inch vibrating OLED TV claims to offer 5.1 audio without speakers (3 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Farstechnica.com%2Fgadgets%2F2022%2F08%2Flgs-97-inch-vibrating-oled-tv-claims-to-offer-5-1-audio-without-speakers%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/010001828c69a4f7-7733de7e-4291-4b4b-9b94-55b0b23ce632-000000/pSL1lX6B9yWr9YpoIU-eFN2rFYlTwwUJDTY7YOCk0UU=261)
LG Display's 97-inch OLED EX display provides up to 30% brighter screens than standard OLED displays and features LG's Film Cinematic Sound OLED (CSO) technology. Film CSO allows displays to generate sound by vibrating. The new display will have a 5.1-channel sound system that offers a cinematic level of immersion. It is currently being demoed at the K-Display 2022 conference in South Korea.

[Microsoft open sources its 3D emoji to let creators remix and customize them (4 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.theverge.com%2F2022%2F8%2F10%2F23299527%2Fmicrosoft-emoji-open-source-creators%3Fscrolla=5eb6d68b7fedc32c19ef33b4%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/010001828c69a4f7-7733de7e-4291-4b4b-9b94-55b0b23ce632-000000/5muHtM43SDJRWBwGx2EnebMQIdOuch9zRSibipp3kkM=261)
Microsoft has open sourced more than 1,500 of its 3D emoji. Anyone is free to remix and build upon the designs. The collection includes emojis that span across different people, religions, and countries. Part of the reason Microsoft open sourced its work is because the company observed a change in how emojis were used in the workplace during the pandemic. A link to the emoji repository is available.

[Billionaires are funding a massive treasure hunt in Greenland as ice vanishes (3 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.cnn.com%2F2022%2F08%2F08%2Fworld%2Fgreenland-melting-mineral-mining-climate%2Findex.html%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/010001828c69a4f7-7733de7e-4291-4b4b-9b94-55b0b23ce632-000000/cJ22A-JguWTYAwltwq9ipNitDsTeQkq4_VV-Fuk688Q=261)
A group of billionaires, including Jeff Bezos, Michael Bloomberg, and Bill Gates, is funding a massive treasure hunt underneath Greenland. Greenland could be the site of one of the most significant nickel and cobalt deposits in the world. The Arctic's disappearing ice has made exploration and mining in Greenland easier and more accessible. The deposits could help power the green energy transition.

[Teens, Social Media and Technology 2022 (14 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.pewresearch.org%2Finternet%2F2022%2F08%2F10%2Fteens-social-media-and-technology-2022%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/010001828c69a4f7-7733de7e-4291-4b4b-9b94-55b0b23ce632-000000/WSn8_sLHt8YO-OapNHIcZrVi6POU2oy6zQLHRap137A=261)

The top five online platforms were YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Snapchat, and Facebook.
