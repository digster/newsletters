TikTok signs agreement to create new US joint venture, memo says (3 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.cnbc.com%2F2025%2F12%2F18%2Ftik-tok-us-sale-china.html%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100019b36596a51-ba4eaefc-fea5-4a30-a17e-b2c4c1027baf-000000/BytLxjLi69qZAql6Xtu4Fc3hbOMti1pAqHgMnUDP93k=436)
TikTok's US operations will soon be housed in a new joint venture named TikTok USDS Joint Venture LLC. The company has signed agreements with Oracle, Silver Lake, and Abu Dhabi-based MGX. The deal's closing date is on January 22. Oracle will serve as the 'trusted security partner' in charge of auditing and validating that TikTok complies with agreed-upon national security terms.

Warner Demands Larry Ellison's Personal Guarantee in Paramount Bid (6 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Flinks.tldrnewsletter.com%2FE1P1x7/1/0100019b36596a51-ba4eaefc-fea5-4a30-a17e-b2c4c1027baf-000000/MoehjNn_bqhScUcmTlzF4F_ie_qUff6oPWp-V3oScdk=436)
Warner has urged shareholders to reject Paramount's hostile takeover bid due to concerns about the financing. Paramount is using an Ellison family trust to backstop the deal - Warner wants Larry Ellison to give a stronger personal guarantee that he is fully committed to the bid. Paramount isn't likely to raise its bid. Instead, it is engaging with shareholders and gathering feedback on its tender about what it needs to do.

Cloudflare's latest outage proves dangers of global configuration changes (8 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fblog.pragmaticengineer.com%2Fthe-pulse-cloudflares-latest-outage%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100019b36596a51-ba4eaefc-fea5-4a30-a17e-b2c4c1027baf-000000/c0T6q94PTwxLi_5vJMeWRGFz4w5k2ybPlu1L4bMh-7Y=436)
Cloudflare's most recent outage was caused by a seemingly innocent but global configuration change. The incident shows why the largest systems in the world will have to implement safer ways to roll out configs. Implementing gradual rollouts for all configuration files is a lot of work, so it doesn't make sense unless the stability of systems is more important than fast iteration. Smaller companies and products probably won't need to implement staged configuration rollouts because the infrastructure work slows down product development.

Let a thousand societies bloom (50 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fvitalik.eth.limo%2Fgeneral%2F2025%2F12%2F17%2Fsocieties.html%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100019b36596a51-ba4eaefc-fea5-4a30-a17e-b2c4c1027baf-000000/o1lM2Hq2svrTcQX6zeDEUlUIcyg9wjS8JCR4eHaLbqg=436)
One of the recurring ideological themes of the last few decades has been the idea of creating entirely new communities, cultures, cities, and even countries. In this world of a thousand nations, people would be able to choose the communities that best fit their values. These ideas inspired Vitalik Buterin to create Zuzalu, an experimental pop-up city in Montenegro, in 2023. This post provides an updated picture of the space, detailing what Buterin has learned since 2023, and sketches out a world that the movement could be driving towards.
