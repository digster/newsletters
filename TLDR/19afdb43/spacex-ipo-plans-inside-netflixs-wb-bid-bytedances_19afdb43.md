After Neuralink, Max Hodak is building something even wilder (15 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Ftechcrunch.com%2F2025%2F12%2F05%2Fafter-neuralink-max-hodak-is-building-something-stranger%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100019afdb42f6c-6a1e6bcf-4233-47a9-a2fd-1b3598f1ce19-000000/0Ho-MgFIMac2TcRm7SKnmFiFxbisWjbKSJfg0fwFgQU=434)
Max Hodak co-founded Neuralink with Elon Musk in 2016. He served as its president and essentially ran day-to-day operations. In 2021, he left and co-founded Science Corp., a brain-computer interface company that aims to build technologies that can reshape human consciousness. Its first product is a procedure that restores 'form vision' to people with advanced macular degeneration through a rice grain-sized computer chip implanted directly in the retina.

The Big Blind (1 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Favc.xyz%2Fthe-big-blind%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100019afdb42f6c-6a1e6bcf-4233-47a9-a2fd-1b3598f1ce19-000000/b_QTmKLGm78UnEZuBfGzMEmBAW8uope36IP-VqZ5RY8=434)

This is the third such finding in Zanskar's short life. AI prospecting appears to be a game-changer for geothermal energy, a market that many had previously written off because finding heat sources was so challenging.

Estimates – a necessary evil? (11 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fthorsell.io%2F2025%2F12%2F07%2Festimates.html%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100019afdb42f6c-6a1e6bcf-4233-47a9-a2fd-1b3598f1ce19-000000/id_ZLcMIBjklAKHaeviVg5Qkm8BGweBj9E9RSVMcjeg=434)
Developers find it difficult to estimate. A lot of the time, it can be difficult to see why the estimate is even important. Product owners see estimates as a crucial piece of the puzzle that helps in planning the short and long-term life cycle of a product. It is best to work in a way that allows developers to be transparent and continuously communicate updated estimates as development progresses.

Another DeepSeek moment (4 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fthreadreaderapp.com%2Fthread%2F1996538308697137277.html%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100019afdb42f6c-6a1e6bcf-4233-47a9-a2fd-1b3598f1ce19-000000/xYInrQjUVLphYb3iYBHvGzmSEpnEeYoBei81dZsGMNA=434)
ZTE's Nubia M153 is a smartphone that runs ByteDance's DoubaoAI agent at the OS level. The agent has complete control over the phone. It can see the UI, choose/download apps, tap/type, make calls, and run multi-step task chains. The Doubao model is a massive, sparse Mixture of Experts model with full text and vision support. This thread contains several videos that show what the phone is capable of.
