It has already raised nearly $135 million from four investors and has a binding and enforceable agreement for the purchase of the remaining shares. NASA says SpaceX’s next Starship flight could test refueling tech (9 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Farstechnica.com%2Fspace%2F2023%2F12%2Fnasa-wants-to-see-gas-stations-in-space-but-so-far-its-tanks-are-empty%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100018c3ed9c11c-e31d89de-83d5-4705-b00c-a7f8320c57a9-000000/6XbTvC-J66ykS0hFawzmuXpCVwc-my0A4HTNJDkBUvI=330)
SpaceX and NASA may be demonstrating orbital refueling technology on the next Starship test flight. NASA has yet to make a decision on when to start testing the capability. Cryogenic propellants must be kept at super-low temperatures or they turn into a gas and boil off. Rockets using these types of propellants typically need to complete their missions within hours - NASA wants to extend this lifetime to months and enable the transfer of the propellants from one vehicle to another.

](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.axios.com%2F2023%2F12%2F05%2Fhumanoid-robot-factory-agility-bipedal-amazon%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100018c3ed9c11c-e31d89de-83d5-4705-b00c-a7f8320c57a9-000000/xvfRIjctYWGxFPpLSbgA9dtr1sUrDEEXWliQ447_TgQ=330)

‘Animate Anyone’ heralds the approach of full-motion deepfakes (4 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Ftechcrunch.com%2F2023%2F12%2F04%2Fanimate-anyone-heralds-the-approach-of-full-motion-deepfakes%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100018c3ed9c11c-e31d89de-83d5-4705-b00c-a7f8320c57a9-000000/chIYjZTP7i2JZKsoRjd1dG8WTWMlew8Ocu5u4Dgl3UI=330)

Introducing StyleX (7 minute read)
](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fstylexjs.com%2Fblog%2Fintroducing-stylex%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100018c3ed9c11c-e31d89de-83d5-4705-b00c-a7f8320c57a9-000000/ZmRbgeduW4Hj8sxwIuAn2F420PR-qHvKqlH0UBOwPtU=330)
StyleX is an expressive, deterministic, reliable, and scalable styling system. It was carefully designed to meet the requirements of large application, reusable component libraries and statically typed codebases. StyleX is fully typed and supports an expressive subset of CSS. It takes the developer experience of CSS-in-JS libraries and uses compile-time tooling to bridge it with the performance and scalability of static CSS.
