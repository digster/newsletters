[‘Maximum amazing’: here’s what we think we know about Elon Musk’s plan for Twitter (9 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.theverge.com%2F2022%2F10%2F5%2F23389159%2Felon-musk-twitter-future%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000183acd814b2-1fd531bd-f0d7-49cf-b6bd-afcfad08fdc9-000000/34XbRNoBLHTVwDBd7V4QK-d2Oxun_-YK3xLgIn93oGk=269)
Elon Musk is planning to integrate Twitter into an all-in-one service app modeled after China's WeChat. WeChat makes money by taking a part of all payments made through the platform. This article discusses the many changes that Musk has suggested over the last few months. The changes include improvements to direct messages and videos, blockchain integration, stronger authentication to prevent bots, and a reduction in content moderation.

[Google’s newest AI generator creates HD video from text prompts (2 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Farstechnica.com%2Finformation-technology%2F2022%2F10%2Fgoogles-newest-ai-generator-creates-hd-video-from-text-prompts%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000183acd814b2-1fd531bd-f0d7-49cf-b6bd-afcfad08fdc9-000000/mLsAopanB-Y7adtaekjLtWq_13lby_tMKxDHJcNFTfE=269)
Imagen Video is Google's new text-to-video AI. It can produce 5.3 second long videos at 1280x768 and 24 frames per second from written prompts. Google Imagen is trained on data from the publicly available LAION-400M image-text dataset, which contains sexually explicit and violent content. The model is not yet available to the public. Examples of videos created with Imagen Video are available in the article.

[Why Applying Machine Learning to Biology is Hard – But Worth It (11 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Ffuture.com%2Fwhy-applying-machine-learning-to-biology-is-hard-but-worth-it%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000183acd814b2-1fd531bd-f0d7-49cf-b6bd-afcfad08fdc9-000000/i2pOWuapzRSQyy8-r62YTvGnNXoWnPzqLPnwH1nldlA=269)

[DeepMind unveils first AI to discover faster matrix multiplication algorithms (5 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fventurebeat.com%2Fai%2Fdeepmind-unveils-first-ai-to-discover-faster-matrix-multiplication-algorithms%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000183acd814b2-1fd531bd-f0d7-49cf-b6bd-afcfad08fdc9-000000/oXWD0_oMSXNj0_UDzXz_TI6_tn58z3Lraa3tZqKKzWk=269)
AlphaTensor is an artificial intelligence system for discovering novel, efficient, and provably correct algorithms. It is built upon AlphaZero, an AI that has shown superhuman performance in some games. It was recently used to discover matrix multiplication algorithms that are more efficient than the state-of-the-art for many matrix sizes and outperform human-designed ones. The discovery could inspire other scientists to use AI to guide algorithmic discovery for other fundamental computation tasks.
