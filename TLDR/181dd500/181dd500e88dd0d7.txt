VISION-BASED AI DRONES OUTFLY WORLD-CLASS HUMAN PILOTS (4 MINUTE READ)
[https://spectrum.ieee.org/zurich-autonomous-drone-race?utm_source=tldrnewsletter]


A trio of champion drone racers recently faced off against
vision-based AI drones in a race and lost. The drones used onboard
vision systems and their own computers to fly. They did not have the
aid of motion-capture systems or off-board computers. Motion capture

between humans and AI completely unfair. Several drone racing clips

ELECTRON WHIRLPOOLS: SCIENTISTS WITNESS ELECTRICITY BEHAVING LIKE
WATER (2 MINUTE READ)
[https://newatlas.com/physics/electron-whirlpools-fluid-flow-electricity/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter]


Researchers at MIT have observed electron whirlpools for the first
time in crystals of tungsten ditelluride. Electricity is made up of
discrete particles, and it can flow with the viscosity of honey under
the right conditions. Tungsten ditelluride is a new quantum material
that allows electrons to strongly interact and behave as quantum waves
rather than particles. The study confirmed a long-standing prediction
and it could lead to more efficient electronics designs.
