Scientists from the Chinese Academy of Sciences have developed a new
gene-editing approach using CRISPR technology. The Cas13d-N2V8 system
uses the Cas13 enzyme, which targets RNA. RNAs are transient molecules

editing system is reversible and safer and more controllable compared
to CRISPR-Cas9.
