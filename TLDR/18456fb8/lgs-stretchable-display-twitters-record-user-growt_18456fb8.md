[LG Display develops stretchable display that elongates by 20% (2 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.zdnet.com%2Farticle%2Flg-display-develops-stretchable-display-that-extends-by-20%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100018456fb8c7d-ab2b39be-f5fa-450a-a1fc-46836d405aea-000000/q8J-5plmsSvbxcI9p5Li6dDRjHyZ8X2AztJsGqVBq6o=274)
LG has developed a stretchable display that can be elongated from 12-inches in size to 14-inches. Stretchable displays are considered to be the next step of evolution for displays after rollable and foldable displays. The full-color display could find many applications within wearable technologies. LG has been developing the display since 2020 in collaboration with 20 South Korean research institutes.

[Lab-grown blood given to people in world-first clinical trial (3 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.bbc.com%2Fnews%2Fhealth-63513330%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100018456fb8c7d-ab2b39be-f5fa-450a-a1fc-46836d405aea-000000/z8EIrAXujfYC3i3AreyoTRDonEJtbxbA_8e2TS4TYrM=274)
A team of researchers from the UK has developed lab-grown blood and started human testing. Small amounts of the blood are being tested to see how it performs inside the body. The blood is created from a pool of stem cells, with the process taking about three weeks. Lab-grown blood is currently significantly more expensive than the cost of an average blood donation.

[New Go-playing trick defeats world-class Go AI—but loses to human amateurs (3 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Farstechnica.com%2Finformation-technology%2F2022%2F11%2Fnew-go-playing-trick-defeats-world-class-go-ai-but-loses-to-human-amateurs%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100018456fb8c7d-ab2b39be-f5fa-450a-a1fc-46836d405aea-000000/9DLmzqDo7mFIEY8vOyU1x3OeveyeS9vccDvSp7u-YFI=274)
A group of AI researchers recently published a method of defeating KataGo, an AI Go-playing program, that involves playing unexpected moves outside of its training set. KataGo learned how to play Go by playing millions of games against itself. Despite the large number of games it was trained on, it still didn't experience every possible scenario. The research highlights the need for better automated testing of AI systems.

[Musk-led Twitter laid off some employees by mistake, asks them to come back (3 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Farstechnica.com%2Ftech-policy%2F2022%2F11%2Fmusk-led-twitter-laid-off-some-employees-by-mistake-asks-them-to-come-back%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100018456fb8c7d-ab2b39be-f5fa-450a-a1fc-46836d405aea-000000/sA0Bbe64J-yS33OruROA14JiMWYggmmMa6dTKqqibsY=274)

[Signal is the latest app to roll out a Stories feature (3 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Ftechcrunch.com%2F2022%2F11%2F07%2Fsignal-the-latest-platform-roll-out-stories-feature%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/0100018456fb8c7d-ab2b39be-f5fa-450a-a1fc-46836d405aea-000000/LxeYvpSRYqWDt9UeLZQsXSG6-_1GgE8UxIYgZBr5lmU=274)
