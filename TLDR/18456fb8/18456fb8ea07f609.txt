A group of AI researchers recently published a method of defeating
KataGo, an AI Go-playing program, that involves playing unexpected
moves outside of its training set. KataGo learned how to play Go by
playing millions of games against itself. Despite the large number of
games it was trained on, it still didn't experience every possible
scenario. The research highlights the need for better automated
testing of AI systems.
