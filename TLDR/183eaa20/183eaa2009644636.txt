THE PRESENT (17 MINUTE READ)
[https://archive.ph/Irsg3?utm_source=tldrnewsletter] 

Effective altruism aims to figure out how those with means can most
impact the world in a quantifiable way. The belief that unlikely but
existential threats are humanity's most pressing problems is integral
to EA. Millions are being poured into funds that focus on theoretical
menaces. EA has been criticized for avoiding structural problems in
favor of abstract math. This article discusses EA, its background,
issues, the billionaires pushing towards its cause, and the future of
the movement.
