China's 1st reusable rocket explodes in dramatic fireball during landing after reaching orbit on debut flight (3 minute read)

Chinese company Landspace's Zhuque-3 rocket exploded after successfully reaching orbit on Tuesday. The 216-foot stainless steel rocket successfully placed its expendable second stage in orbit, but the first stage booster appeared to lose an engine during its landing burn. No one was harmed in the incident. The anomaly is currently under investigation to discover its root cause. A video of the explosion is available in the article.
DragonFire laser weapon takes down high-speed drones (2 minute read)

Britain's DragonFire laser weapon shot down drones flying at 404 mph on November 20 at the Ministry of Defence's Hebrides Range in Scotland. The high-powered, solid-state laser will be installed in the Navy's Type 45 frigates by 2027. It can hit a target the size of a US quarter over a distance of 0.62 miles. The laser weapon can lock onto and fire at hostiles that are flying at a distance and at an altitude where they are above the curvature of the Earth.

Critical Security Vulnerability in React Server Components (4 minute read)

There is a security vulnerability in React Server Components that allows unauthenticated remote code execution. It exploits a flaw in how React decodes payloads sent to React Server Function endpoints. Apps may still be vulnerable even if they don't implement any React Server Function endpoints. The vulnerability is present in versions 19.0, 19.1.0, 19.1.1, and 19.2.0 of react-server-dom-webpack, react-server-dom-parcel, and react-server-dom-turbopack.
Failed software projects are strategic failures (27 minute read)

You need to know how to formulate a strategy to avoid strategic failure. Decide what your basic goals are, consider your positioning in your environment, and look at your strengths and weaknesses as a strategic entity. As a business, your first sweeping goal is continued survival, followed by maximizing total profitability. Consider different things that you can do in your circumstances to achieve your strategic goals. Organizations can usually only pursue a few goals at once, so set objectives that provide the maximum amount of impact with the available resources.

Everyone in Seattle Hates AI (6 minute read)

Tech workers in Seattle tend to react negatively when the topic of AI is brought up. This is due to several reasons, like layoffs due to AI, the proliferation of AI in software, and being forced to use AI tools whether they work or not. The result is that engineers are no longer trying because they think they can't, and companies aren't empowering them because they assume they shouldn't. Bad products are only reinforcing the belief that AI is doomed.
