NEW CHIP EXPANDS THE POSSIBILITIES FOR AI (10 MINUTE READ)
[https://www.quantamagazine.org/a-brain-inspired-chip-can-run-ai-with-far-less-energy-20221110/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter]


NeuRRAM is a type of neuromorphic chip that features millions of
memory cells and thousands of neurons built into its hardware to run
algorithms. It uses a type of memory called resistive RAM which uses
analog memory cells that can store multiple values along a fully
continuous range. NeuRRAM chips can store more information from
algorithms in the same amount of chip space as traditional chips. They
can perform as well as digital computers on complex AI tasks while
being up to 1,000 times more energy efficient.

This article breaks down the story between two major cryptocurrency
exchanges, Binance and FTX. A recent report into one of FTX's sister
companies, Alameda Research, led to a loss of trust in FTX and its
