THE FIRST-EVER COMPLETE MAP OF IMMUNE SYSTEM CONNECTIONS OFFERS NEW
PATHWAYS TO THERAPIES (2 MINUTE READ)
[https://interestingengineering.com/health/first-map-immune-system-connections-therapies?utm_source=tldrnewsletter]


Researchers from the UK and Switzerland have developed a comprehensive
map of the network of connections that make up the human immune
system. The map highlights the individual cell types, messengers, and
relative speed of each conversation taking place between immune cells.
It could revolutionize how researchers approach drug development and
immunotherapies. The map includes many previously unknown interactions
and provides a new understanding of the cell-to-cell signals happening
in the immune system.
