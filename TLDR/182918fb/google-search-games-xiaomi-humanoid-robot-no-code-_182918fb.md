[Ethereum network sets a target date for the big proof-of-stake switch (3 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.theverge.com%2F2022%2F8%2F11%2F23301638%2Fethereum-crypto-blockchain-proof-of-stake-environment%3Fscrolla=5eb6d68b7fedc32c19ef33b4%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000182918fb0ed-06707f53-9136-4903-baf9-ba91ba4afee3-000000/qUAtRtPLGy295trVZEjHumjH7MvxSmdujbGSGwAdE3w=261)
Ethereum's transition to Proof-of-Stake will occur on September 15 or 16. A pull request proposing when the change will occur was submitted during a call with Ethereum developers streamed on the Ethereum Foundation's YouTube page. Previous test runs of the Merge have been successful. Ethereum will use significantly less energy to secure its network after the Merge.

[Meet Xiaomi’s new humanoid robot, CyberOne (2 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Ftechcrunch.com%2F2022%2F08%2F11%2Fmeet-xiaomis-new-humanoid-robot-cyberone%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000182918fb0ed-06707f53-9136-4903-baf9-ba91ba4afee3-000000/HhYOEdFbyneyi6OQR3N-LYY8IOotztURX0lPa5WpalE=261)
Xiaomi introduced a bipedal humanoid robot during a launch event for its foldable devices. The CyberOne is capable of perceiving 3D space and recognizing individuals, gestures, and expressions. It can recognize 45 classifications of human emotion and comfort users in times of sadness. Xiaomi promises a range of real-world applications for the robot, including manufacturing assistance and human companionship.

[Researchers find way to shrink a VR headset down to normal glasses size (3 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.pcgamer.com%2Fresearchers-find-way-to-shrink-a-vr-headset-down-to-normal-glasses-size%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000182918fb0ed-06707f53-9136-4903-baf9-ba91ba4afee3-000000/F8FXVHfVVnOEj9PFxoVfi5MaSH5mr8Y9_tjuagOvsz4=261)
Researchers from Stanford University and Nvidia have teamed up to develop VR glasses that weigh just 60 grams. The Holographic Glasses use pancake lenses, which allow for a smaller profile and unlimited resolution. They currently only offer a field-of-view of 22.8 degrees. The glasses require a very accurate measurement of the users' pupils to work. A video explaining the technology behind the Holographic Glasses is available.

[Will low and no code tools ever truly disrupt tech development? (6 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fstackoverflow.blog%2F2022%2F08%2F10%2Fwill-low-and-no-code-tools-ever-truly-disrupt-tech-development%2F%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000182918fb0ed-06707f53-9136-4903-baf9-ba91ba4afee3-000000/gDM2IO5FzQlSs8kZs4Mm4aUVfN68k9Kc9gz7yH906dI=261)
Low and no code tools are making building tech more accessible. These tools aren't able to replace developers as there will always be issues that only a developer can fix. Low and no code tools will prompt more people to learn coding languages. Those who choose to learn more and become developers will always be able to find work.

[System.css (GitHub Repo)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsakofchit%2Fsystem.css%3Fref=producthunt%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000182918fb0ed-06707f53-9136-4903-baf9-ba91ba4afee3-000000/EA3PXxj-7wMRXUi6ylKaB-NShiGPMx0g_B9wYjpR6Ak=261)
System.css is a design system for building retro Apple interfaces. It is based on Apple's System OS 6, the final monochrome version of macOS. The library doesn't use any JavaScript and it is compatible with all frontend frameworks.

[Why thinking hard makes us feel tired (4 minute read)](https://tracking.tldrnewsletter.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fwww.nature.com%2Farticles%2Fd41586-022-02161-5%3Futm_source=tldrnewsletter/1/01000182918fb0ed-06707f53-9136-4903-baf9-ba91ba4afee3-000000/tsVqjRSbCwno_YEQktbcBwxWojUn-iCRLSOgd7COUl8=261)
A study on mental fatigue found that working on tedious and mentally taxing assignments increases glutamate levels in the brain. Glutamate is an important signaling molecule. Too much of the molecule can disrupt brain function. Future research will focus on finding out how to restore glutamate levels.
