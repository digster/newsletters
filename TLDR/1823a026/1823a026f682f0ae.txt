Google, Microsoft, Meta, and Amazon have launched a public effort to
scrap the leap second. The leap second is an occasional extra second
that keeps clocks in sync with the Earth's actual rotation.
Researchers predict that it could be removed without much effect for
at least 2,000 years. US and French time-keeping authorities agree
with the removal of the leap second.
