AI IS CHANGING SCIENTISTS’ UNDERSTANDING OF LANGUAGE LEARNING (4
MINUTE READ)
[https://arstechnica.com/information-technology/2022/10/ai-is-changing-scientists-understanding-of-language-learning/?comments=1?utm_source=tldrnewsletter]


Many language scientists believe that language learners need to learn
grammar to generate proper sentences, but AI is proving that it is
possible to output meaningful text after being exposed to vast amounts
of language input without the help of grammar. While sometimes their
choice of words is strange, the overwhelming majority of AI language
outputs are grammatically correct. It is possible that linguistic
experience is more important than grammar for becoming a competent
language user.
