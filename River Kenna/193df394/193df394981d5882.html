You already know the answer

River Kenna
Dec 19
 
READ IN APP
 

“We have what we seek, it is there all the time, and if we give it time, it will make itself known to us.”

— Thomas Merton

Most people live inside tight loops — problem-solving, overthinking, endless analyzing — and miss out on the slower, wider rhythms where clarity emerges.

What if you could open a door to that rhythm? What if you could ask the big questions, and directly feel the answers stirring?

This is what Latent Insight Practice is for.

By taking time to deepen into the body and open into spacious awareness, we find a different way of perceiving questions and answers. When we find that, clarity shows up as a natural byproduct.

Bring your big questions: the stuck points, the lingering curiosities, the things you can’t quite resolve. In this mini-intensive, you’ll learn not just how to approach these questions but how to let them move you.

Meetings will take place on Zoom January 10, 11, 12 & 17 at 9pm EST (6pm PST).

Why It Matters

Because life is too short to stay fragmented and stuck.

You’ve felt it before — that sharp, rational part of your mind, endlessly circling but never landing. It’s not enough. Real insight doesn’t come from effort alone. It comes from tapping into the part of you that knows more than it can explain.

This isn’t woo, it’s a practical skill, and it’s one you already have — buried under the noise, maybe, but it’s there. One of our first steps in this workshop is demonstrating all the simple, ways you already use this skill day to day — before demonstrating just how much further that skill can go.

What to Expect

Think of this as an experiment with yourself:

  • Day One: We’ll guide you into your body’s intelligence, step by step, until you can sense the quiet “yes” and “no” within.

  • Day Two: We’ll expand your awareness — away from overthinking and problem-addiction, into the wide-open field where the knots simplify.

  • Day Three: Then we’ll connect it all — body, awareness, intuition — and apply them to a new way of approaching your own burning questions.

  • Days Four through Seven: Deepen into the practice at home, experiment, see what works.

  • Day Eight: We come back together for a final check-in and Q&A.

You don’t need to prepare much. Just bring a question or two — things you’ve been chewing on lately but can’t quite crack. Even something vague is fine. The clarity will surprise you.

Who This Is For

Anyone who’s tired of spinning their wheels, tired of feeling stuck.

Beginners will find the practice approachable and grounding. Experienced meditators will find a new layers to explore. Anyone who sticks with the practice over time will notice their life opening up into more simplicity and depth.

Join Us

If you’ve been looking for clarity, for groundedness, for a way to meet life’s questions with steadiness and grace — this is your invitation.
