Most people live inside tight loops — problem-solving, overthinking, endless analyzing — and miss out on the slower, wider rhythms where clarity emerges. What if you could open a door to that rhythm? What if you could ask the big questions, and directly feel the answers stirring? This is what Latent Insight Practice is for. By taking time to deepen into the body and open into spacious awareness, we find a different way of perceiving questions and answers. When we find that, clarity shows up as a natural byproduct. Bring your big questions: the stuck points, the lingering curiosities, the things you can’t quite resolve. What to ExpectThink of this as an experiment with yourself: Day One: We’ll guide you into your body’s intelligence, step by step, until you can sense the quiet “yes” and “no” within.
