From September 25 to October 9, I'll be in Barcelona, running a pop-up mystery school: Mysterium. Almost none of you should come. In fact, almost none of you can come — we decided on a maximum of 14 attendees, and we'd rather keep it on the lower side. 
I'd love for you to come, to join us in this little journey into meditation, mysticism, soul-making, and weirdness [ https://substack.com/redirect/cd17cf11-a594-41e8-87df-2b594219d14e?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. But there are some reasons you might not want to apply [ https://substack.com/redirect/05e4a0bf-9ac4-4e7b-954a-830c73f4000e?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
You can find more information on Mysterium here, and you can apply here.
There are two ways a successful journey goes: either you go into the unknown and you bring something back — or you go into the unknown and you bring others with you.
Both are necessary. But the second is underused and undervalued. Every time a group journeys into the unknown, finds something there, and sets up an outpost, the map is expanded. The world gets bigger. The next people who venture out that way will find a settlement waiting for them, find people and knowledge and new possibilities waiting for them.
At Mysterium, we're here to go together into places we didn't know we could go together.
Last year, I wrote a piece [ https://substack.com/redirect/d68e3818-a006-4031-832d-27b613f5fda0?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] from a place I've touched in meditation, in embodiment, in journeying — a realm I'd touched into alone, and wanted to describe to others because it meant so much to me.
A couple months later, I had an intense meeting with someone where we dropped into a lot of strange meditative states together. Afterward, they read that piece and texted me "This is where we were! We just spent two days in this place together, didn't we?"
That was the first time I had any real idea that pairs and groups could venture into these spaces together. It's happened more often since — and I want to open and nurture a space where more of us can take each other on journeys into these realms [ https://substack.com/redirect/e8c79048-e4a4-4062-a195-a1da85b8648a?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]. Into other flavors of existence [ https://substack.com/redirect/a395d6bc-bf92-42bb-9d18-ccf3de3d0864?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], other layers of existence, other settings of reality, where inner physics are different.
There are many paths into the unknown, into flavors of existence you haven't tasted yet. The breath, the body, intense focus; the eyes of the man across from you, the voice in your dreams; the fury with which you resist your own sadness; the sadness with which you resist your own fear. The psychedelic shimmer of a forest lit into flickering life by a breeze. The silence that enfolds your heart and the heart next to you on the couch; the fire of the yagna pit; the sacrifice of who you were to the spirit of who you will become. Devotion to the muses trying to pour art through you. Service to the world that wants to be. Making food to nourish the beings in your care.
There are many paths into the Mystery. If you can't walk them with others, don't come to Mysterium.
If you won't let your soul expand past the property line and feel where it mixes with the world and the souls in it, don't come to Mysterium.
There's a trust in yourself and a trust [ https://substack.com/redirect/31e6d17c-dcc0-41a6-989b-fbb578dc08a6?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] in the Process of Reality that opens up new paths. I don't think there's any real limit to how much this trust can open up. The only limit is how deeply you can surrender into trust.
If you don't have a desire to let go into deeper trust in yourself and Reality, don't come to Mysterium.
When you're in a room with someone who knows a realm you don't, who carries a flavor of existence you've never tasted, you always have the option of following their voice and their presence into that realm. You can let yourself be guided to the places they lead you. To the places where they stand and beckon you to follow.
You always have the option of letting go into the flavors of existence that are inside of you, the specific realms you can rise and fall into. If you aren't interested in becoming more yourself, in deepening into the flavor of your Being [ https://substack.com/redirect/50df5759-bf89-44e3-b11a-dc693f061d88?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], don't come to Mysterium.
The first week of Mysterium will have more intentional practice — some mixture of spacious somatic awareness, meditation in nature, circling, attention-crafting, imaginal journeying, ritual, poetic attunement... other guidance, depending on who shows up and what they have to share.
The second week is more optional, more freeform — a further playground to continue in that space.
If you want to be a student, rather than a curious peer, don't come to Mysterium. 
The paths into mystery vary person by person. As everyone shares their own odd paths and homebrew prompts into divine presence, it can only bring more and more beauty to recognize the signature of their being in everything about the process. The strangeness, the ritual, the idiosyncratic language and approach [ https://substack.com/redirect/198b125d-37a9-48ff-a6f3-3d3af8a5cd17?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] — if you can't find the beauty in it, don't come to Mysterium.
If you want one-size-fits-all instructions with polished edges, don't come to Mysterium
"A lineage of friends," or "a lineage of peers." These phrases have illuminated my past couple years. I can't tell you how much I've learned from coming into connection with experienced friends and peers who know things I don't, who see things I don't. And I can't tell you how much some of them have learned from the things I knew that they didn't, things I saw that they couldn't.
Pairs and groups know things that the individuals in them don't. The group field lets things move between us that we couldn't have shared consciously, in words or images. This has happened over and over again. I don't know why this is but I know that it is. 
If you've meditated alone in a room, and then meditated in a group of skillful meditators, you may have felt this.
If you've danced alone in a room, and then danced in a group of soulful dancers, you've definitely felt this.
Group fields are more skillful and powerful than the sum of the members of the group.
If you won't open up to giving and receiving with yourself, you won’t want to come Mysterium. If you won't open up to giving and receiving in the group field, you shouldn’t come to Mysterium.
Every time some new amazing possibility opens up, you have to accept that new challenging possibilities can move through that same opening. If you open to new streams of embodied bliss, you are also opening to new quivers of all-pervading fear and anger.
If you have no patience for the depths that open when you open to new heights, don't come to Mysterium.
The group field can be made up of humans, of course. It can also open up to include trees, sky, sun, moon, God, ancestors, owls.
If you cannot be supported by trees and owls, don't come to Mysterium.
Think of Mysterium as a ship, sailing off to explore new lands. The crew is made up of people who've seen and walked on those lands, and are trying to share them with the rest of us. We'll be navigating together, and when we get lost, we'll get lost together — which also means we'll be able to find our Way again together. 
It's not a cruise ship, you aren't a passenger, you're one of us. The ship has a captain, to take on responsibility, and a couple of first and second mates with the experience and power to keep things on track; and it also has you, with your experience, your hunger, your wisdom and direction to share in the navigational process.
If that doesn't sound like a good time to you, you shouldn't come to Mysterium.
I feel like I shouldn’t have to say this, but just to be explicit: If you don’t love Mystery, don’t come to Mysterium.
You Should Come to Mysterium
I don’t want to be foreboding or push you away. I think we’re going to have a really beautiful time in Barcelona.
A handful of my favorite mystics and practitioners will be there, or will be stopping through. We’ll be following an intuitive range of practices, and I trust everyone there to bring their own unique flavor and depth to the shared practice and inquiry.
At core, Mysterium is a gathering to pull together people, intentions, and energies that I trust deeply, and allow them to make an imprint that ripples out through the world and our lives. We’re coming together to find and tend to the openings in reality that want our care — and to allow our lives to be fed by the sacredness that emerges from those openings.
We’re here to feed and be fed by Mystery.
We’re here to deepen our own flavor and self-trust and specificity of Being — to let our individual Soulfulness emerge more fully [ https://substack.com/redirect/99e42e09-d176-4e23-8d38-932cf28fe86c?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] from contact with Mystery. 
Everything else comes from that. Any schedules, practices, conversations, openings that occur, they come out of following those threads.
If you want to follow those threads, check out Mysterium’s info page [ https://substack.com/redirect/8b01ae2f-66ed-4ee4-b056-fa1cc05ea6fe?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] and apply [ https://substack.com/redirect/05e4a0bf-9ac4-4e7b-954a-830c73f4000e?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
