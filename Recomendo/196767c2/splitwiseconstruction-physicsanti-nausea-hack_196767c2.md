On a recent wedding party trip, a group of six of us easily split the costs of our stay, food, drinks, Ubers, and party favors using [Splitwise](https://substack.com/redirect/51a33176-69c6-41c8-9087-bf955c32afde?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o). We created a group that allowed each of us to add our expenses individually, and the app handled all the calculations and cost-splitting for us. All that was left to do was for each person to settle up at the end of our trip. The app is free to use on iPhone, Android, and the web, and we managed just fine with the free version. — CD
A blog I am really digging is Brian Potter’s [Construction Physics](https://substack.com/redirect/fdbcd563-6998-4859-8752-df45dcdeeaa5?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o), which is self-described as “Essays about buildings, infrastructure, and industrial technology.” Sounds boring, but is thrilling, as Potter deep dives into our built environment. The territory is similar to the podcast 99%Invisible, but far more quantitative. I’ve found his investigations so interesting I am systematically reading through his back issues. — KK
A [study](https://substack.com/redirect/f4612b9a-93fc-46e8-af98-155c21226015?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) found that smelling isopropyl alcohol pads was more effective than a common anti-nausea drug at reducing nausea in emergency department patients, with peak effect in just four minutes. I bought a box of [100 alcohol prep pads](https://substack.com/redirect/b81db6e8-f4fa-4b47-98a8-7c8e2d51dc2d?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) ($6) to keep in my wallet for emergencies. Just hold near your nose and inhale gently if needed. They're so slim I forget they're there, but I'll be glad to have them if nausea strikes. — MF

Found on Reddit, this [guide to rewiring your brain](https://substack.com/redirect/2fe19f47-fca4-4013-82bc-d2680f94cd97?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) emphasizes interrupting negative thought patterns, reframing perspectives, and, of course, mindfulness as techniques for mental transformation. All it takes is attention and intention when it comes to developing better habits. —CD
