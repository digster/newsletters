I was getting an education when I read today’s story. I’ve now learnt that AI can play a significant role in eyecare. There aren’t that many ophthalmologists and retina specialists, so the doctor to patient ratio is actually 1:10,000 in India. So if you have an algorithm which can take a retina image and diagnose those images, it can really help with early detection of blindness-related conditions. Google. LebenCare in Singapore. IBM Watson - all of them are trying to make this work. 
 
Google, for instance, is working with Aravind Eye Care and Sankara Nethralaya. Both these hospitals are working with Google to detect diabetic retinopathy—an eye condition that is the most common cause of vision loss among diabetic people. This is our story for today.
