Every great game has its share of nail-biting moments, and you can count on your all-rounder to bail you out.

Publicly-listed fintech Paytm* too had one such allrounder—its personal loans product—one of its biggest growth drivers. It doubled the issue of personal loans, saw a 58% growth in disbursements, and a 64% jump in financial services revenue. And, importantly, a turnaround in its financial performance, and the lifting of its share price.

Except in January when a curveball came at Paytm, like sidelining a star player mid-season, the fintech had to cut back on issuing small ticket-size loans due to a jump in bad loans in the segment.

Such a crisis is not new to the company. This time, alongside Paytm’s founder Vijay Shekhar Sharma is the formidable duo of Madhur Deora and Bhavesh Gupta as the new strategists. And they are banking on a new product to drive growth. But is it a worthy substitute?

Rounak, the author of today’s story finds out: https://the-ken.com/story/paytm-cant-count-on-what-brought-on-its-turnaround/ (8-minute read)
