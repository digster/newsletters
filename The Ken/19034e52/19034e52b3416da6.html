Announcing the launch of a special new website for The Ken’s learning vertical
 
Good Afternoon Ishan, 
 
Today is a special day for all of us at The Ken. We’ve been conducting narrative thinking and storytelling programmes for corporate leaders for over two years now, and all this while, we’ve been laser-focused on making sure that our workshops and masterclasses are the best that we can offer. We’ve iterated and improved and honed our product with every session. 
 
We now believe it’s time to take things to the next level. 
 
I’m super excited to announce the launch of a special new website for The Ken’s learning vertical—a single dedicated location where you can find all the information about our learning programmes, the trainers who conduct them, and case studies that document the impact of the programme. 
 
So get yourself a cup of coffee or tea, and do take some time to browse our offerings and achievements. 
 
https://the-ken.com/learning/
 

 

 

As one of the trainers and the head of The Ken’s learning vertical, I’ve constantly been told by leaders who’ve participated in our workshops that their greatest professional need is being able to earn the attention, understanding, and trust of their teams, customers, and investors. For leaders who work with diverse teams in a rapidly evolving cultural landscape, this need is only turning ever more gnawing. 

The key to successful business communication has never been straightforward. But now, the costs of distrust, disconnection, and disengagement are higher than ever. How do you communicate well-researched, nuanced truth in a way that’s both interesting and compelling—like a Game of Thrones screenplay?

The answer, we’ve found, is journalistic storytelling—and that’s at the heart of our curriculum. It is that magic recipe that The Ken has been perfecting for seven years now. The kind that, if applied well, has the power to persuade even the most ardent nay-sayers, hardened sceptics, and even the most objective truth-seekers.
