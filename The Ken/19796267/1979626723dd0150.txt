Sun, 22 Jun 25

Dear Ishan, there is no edition of First Principles today.

Yesterday morning, I spent over three captivating hours at the Bangalore International Centre co-moderating The Ken’s third live event. Along with Praveen Gopal Krishnan, my co-moderator (who also couldn’t write his newsletter, The Nutgraf, yesterday), we discussed with three wonderful guests what it means to be healthy and fit today.

Nisha Millet spoke as an elite athlete, a 40+ woman, an entrepreneur, a coach, and a mom. Viren Shetty spoke as a healthcare executive, (fit) Crossfit retiree, dad, and son. And Dr. Cyriac “Abby” Philips spoke as a doctor, a father, a scientist, and an everyman.

We were joined by over 150 wonderful people, their ages spanning from teenage to the seventies and eighties.
