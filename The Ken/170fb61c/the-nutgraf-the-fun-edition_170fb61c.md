Telcos are the ones who carry the burden and also reap the rewards of getting India’s massive population online—primarily by offering affordable data plans. Never before in India’s history has its future rested on so few companies—Airtel, Vodafone Idea, Reliance Jio, and BSNL.
Last week, with India shutting down many of its offices and pushing employees to work from home, India’s telcos were needed more than ever.
Last week, one came closer to vanishing.

"We do not see how they can meet the demands of the court and are, therefore, in threat of insolvency," Bernstein analysts said in a research report.
