Every day for the last 365 days, we’ve seen you in your inbox at 8 AM sharp. But we didn’t just write to you about our stories this year.
2021 has been a big year for newsletters at The Ken. We launched seven new newsletters to cover areas like edtech, personal finance, crypto, and climate change. You may have missed some of them, so we’ve collated a list of the top ten newsletter editions we’ve put out this year.
Pour yourself a steaming hot cup of your favourite beverage and get started!
[How India defeated the evil of recurring payments](https://the-ken.com/the-nutgraf/how-india-defeated-the-evil-of-recurring-payments/): Why are the cash flows of subscription businesses all over India crunched?
(The Nutgraf, our business newsletter about macro events in India)
[Why is Paytm more valuable than Zomato?](https://the-ken.com/kaching/why-is-paytm-more-valuable-than-zomato/) Or why does Aswath Damodaran value Paytm more than Zomato?
(Ka-ching!, our fintech newsletter)
[It’s not game over yet for Dream11 and Co in Karnataka](https://the-ken.com/moneyball_edition/its-not-game-over-yet-for-dream11-and-co-in-karnataka/): But for now, the companies have no choice but to abide by the law and challenge bans wherever they can—and hope that more states don’t get similar ideas.
(Moneyball, our newsletter about business of sports)
[Web3—change of guard or just the uniform?](https://the-ken.com/tokenised-edition/web3-change-of-guard-or-just-the-uniform/) Enthusiasts want to build a new iteration of the internet using blockchains, but fixing Big Tech’s monopolies using crypto-assets might just end up as a pipe dream
(Tokenised, our crypto newsletter)
[What Mckinsey can learn from Microsoft](https://the-ken.com/greenmargins/what-mckinsey-can-learn-from-microsoft/): A consultancy is only as good as its clients, yet McKinsey could learn a thing or two from Microsoft, a hyper communicative company when it comes to decarbonisation
(Green Margins, our climate change newsletter)
[India is embracing the Omicron uncertainty](https://the-ken.com/incitingincident/india-is-embracing-the-omicron-uncertainty/): The lack of clarity is frustrating, but it gives us time to listen to and consider different perspectives
(Inciting Incident, our business narratives newsletter)
[Will Byju’s have a spac-tacular debut? ](https://the-ken.com/edsetgo/will-byjus-have-a-spac-tacular-public-debut/)The edtech is making all the right noises for a US listing; how successful will it be?
(Ed Set Go, our edtech newsletter)
[How Netflix is making Formula 1 great again:](https://the-ken.com/moneyball_edition/how-netflix-is-making-formula-1-great-again/) The streaming giant’s docuseries ‘Formula 1: Drive to Survive’ is almost single-handedly boosting the sport’s popularity after years of stagnancy
(Moneyball, our newsletter about business of sports)
