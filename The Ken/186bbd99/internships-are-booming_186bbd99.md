Did you know that the consumption of fish in India outstrips that of poultry by nearly a factor of three?
And unlike smartphones or consumer products, fish offers 40-50% gross margins.
Shan Kadavil—co-founder and CEO at FreshToHome, one of India's largest online fish and meat sellers—reveals this and much more about the fascinating fish industry in Episode 15 of our leadership podcast, First Principles.
In 2016, FreshToHome made roughly $2 million in sales. This year, it will do around $130 million—a 65X growth.
From obsessing over CURR, NURR and RURR metrics to encouraging employees to look for ways to disrupt their own business, Shan Kadavil speaks with host Rohin Dharmakumar on his multiple stints as an entrepreneur.
