Featuring Nithin Kamath, Deepak Shenoy, and Avinash Luthria on rethinking stock market investments
 

 

Good Morning Ishan,

 

I want you to think back to the financial advice that you received when you first started investing. Surely, many people have offered you guidance on how to turn a rupee into two.

 

Were you urged to build a 60/40 portfolio, with 60% in stocks? Perhaps you were told about the 5% rule of diversification, or attempted to buy what you saw as undervalued stocks and waited for the rest of the market to catch up. Were you asked to rebalance your portfolio every month, quarter, or year? 

 

The fact is, the nature of India’s stock markets has changed. I’m sure you’ve sensed it. There are fewer undiscovered bargains; value traps have taken their place. Markets are more efficient than ever, so many patterns are arbitraged away. There’s immense volatility, as we saw with the rout months ago that sparked worries of a sustained bear market.

 

If the investment principles that worked in the past are no longer valid, where does that leave you as a retail investor?

 

What are some new ways of thinking about investing that will ensure you are in a good position 10, 20, or even 30 years from now?

 

The Ken’s next event in Bengaluru will tackle those questions. And every ticket holder will help us shape the discussion.

 

My colleague Praveen Gopal Krishnan and I will chat with three guests who even now make it possible for you to invest in different ways.

 

 

Here’s what we won’t do: We will not try to predict highs and lows. We will not show you which stocks to invest in. This event will not give you a trading strategy that you can use the following day.

 

Instead, we want you to leave the event feeling more confident and better prepared to chart your own way with stock investments. We want you to be equipped to ask the foundational questions that challenge conventional investment wisdom, then think beyond bull and bear markets to formulate a strategy that truly fits you.

 

I invite you to join us at 5 p.m. on Monday, 19 May, at the Bangalore International Centre. Like before, we’re pricing tickets at Rs 1,999. The Ken’s subscribers get 50% off.

 

To live up to our promise that the event will be about you, every ticket holder can pose one great question to our panelists. What is the one thing you want answered by Nithin, Deepak, and Avinash? If you’ve ever felt like there’s a hole in your way of thinking about investments, this is the perfect opportunity to close that gap. We will bake your questions into the discussion.

 

Praveen and I are ready for an eye-opening evening. We expect to hear many contrarian and surprising perspectives from our three guests. It would be our privilege to have you there with us.
