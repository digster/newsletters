A year and a half ago, an Indian stockbroking startup, Upstox, took a leaf out of the Robinhood playbook by handing out complimentary shares. Thanks to a $25-million investment by VC firm Tiger Global, Upstox also did away with account-opening charges and started offering referral bonuses.В 

Upstox hit paydirt with these hacks, thanks to a newfound fascination with the stock market among retail investors during the lockdown.В
