The year started with Rohin's analysis of the consumer brands market and how it could succeed where e-commerce stumbled. Day 2 had Arundhati talk about the explosion in digital payments over the last year and how it puts the pin on one particular by-product in 2018—that of providing and acquiring loans.
 
Venkat's story on day 3 was about how media consumption in India is shifting more and more towards online content. This creates a pattern, for both production and dissemination. The year 2018, as he puts it, could be content's perfect storm moment in India.
 
The final story of Outlook 2018 (as we've been repeatedly referring to the past week's stories internally), came from Payal, and it was about how India's logistics space is really picking up. And investors want to put money where the tech is. All the sector needs, she writes, is a change in policy to take off.
