So here’s what we thought you thought you knew about Tiger Global, the Nessie of India’s startup Loch Ness.
1. It is single handedly responsible for India’s startup funding bubble

3. It has put the entire Indian startup ecosystem at risk
Allow Sumanth to debunk all of these lazy notions. Methodically, he traces the arc of India’s startup boom since the early 2000’s, and Tiger Global’s role in it.To arrive at how under Lee Fixel the fund irrevocably changed the “3 V’s” of venture investing - value, volume & velocity.
Far from destabilising the Indian startup ecosystem, Sumanth argues how Tiger Global should be credited for building it. "It wouldn’t be wrong to say that if Fixel hadn’t made these bets, e-commerce in India would’ve been a fraction of what it actually is today,” he says.
