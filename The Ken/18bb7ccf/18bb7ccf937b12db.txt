When The Ken turned seven, on 3 October, we wrote to you saying that we wanted to renew the promise we had made to subscribers at the time of our launch in 2016.

There’s a variety of reasons for that: a radically different universe of companies to write about than when we started out, an increasing number of digital-news publications claiming to do what we do, and workplaces transformed by Covid.

So, we told you:

These are compelling reasons for us to not just tell extensively reported and skillfully narrated stories, but also be quick to report on big developments, with sharp, actionable insights.

We will continue to chronicle the biggest disruptors in India’s startup ecosystem and internet economy, and dig deep into large publicly listed tech and consumer companies. And we will also report more frequently on the most consequential shifts in careers, workplaces, and the complex world of investing.

It’s early days for us on this journey, but whatever we do, we put you, the subscriber, at the centre of it. And your feedback shapes why and how we do what we do.

Which is why we’re coming to you with a subscriber survey focussed on our stories. You may be a free subscriber, a paid subscriber, or a former subscriber. We want to hear from every one of you. On what you like about our coverage. And what you don’t. On what you want us to do more of. And less.

It’ll take less than 10 minutes to take the survey, but it’ll be quite valuable to us.
