Good Morning Ishan,
 
I have a question. Have you watched the trailer of Manikarnika? It’s a Hindi movie that’s scheduled to release today in India.
 
You have? How do you have so much free time? What do you do? Are there vacancies? Please email me separately. 
 
If you haven’t, please don’t. It’s too late for me, but you can save yourselves. 
 
Let me tell you about it though. If the trailer is to be believed, Manikarnika is a period biopic about an Indian queen who valiantly fights the British with a sword, an army, and her voice. At one point, Manikarnika, who clearly wasn’t aware that the best way to get rid of the British was to simply hold a referendum, shoots down a CGI tiger and leaps over three men in one leap to stand atop an elephant. A character looks at her approvingly and announces to the camera, “There’s no one like her”. 
 
I believe him. There’s no one like smallpox either. 
 
The part of the trailer that caught my attention was right at the end - when the names of the producers come up. There’s a very interesting name up there. A company called EaseMyTrip. You may ask - why is an airline booking website producing a Bollywood movie? Excellent question. We asked that too. The answer is unexpected, and fascinating. 
 
EaseMyTrip is a strange company. It announced an annual revenue of around Rs 1900 crore in FY17. All without a rupee in venture funding. The subsequent year, it reported that revenue fell by 93%, and that the revenue the previous year wasn’t Rs 1900 crore, but Rs 71 crore. Ten days back, India’s largest business newspaper, The Economic Times reported that EaseMyTrip was planning to raise Rs 1,500 crore through an IPO, valuing the young company at between Rs 6,000-7,500 crore. 
 
So we thought - that remarkable balance sheet deserves a story. 
 
And what did we find on it? 
 
Coal.
 
Yes. Coal. 
 
Today’s story by Rohin is about a company which fights badly made-up monsters, claims victories over adversaries who aren’t even aware it exists and does deft pirouettes that clearly look unrealistic - all while being lauded approvingly by everyone around them.
