Bombay Sweet Shop isn’t just a regular sweet shop. Instead, they do something unique—they reimagine Indian sweets and mithai in a contemporary format. When I spoke to Sameer Seth, CEO of Hunger Inc, the company that runs Bombay Sweet Shop, he described what they do as “Imagine Willy Wonka’s chocolate factory meets mithai”. 

 

And we were sold right then. All their sweets are traditional and familiar, and yet they incorporate a wonderful twist. The result is something you’ve never seen before but still makes perfect sense. Their sweets are 100% vegetarian, hand-made, and preservative-free, and their packaging is simply stunning. In fact, many people have told me that they are the best sweet shop in India. 

 

In many ways, they remind us of The Ken. Hence, we figured, let’s work together.
