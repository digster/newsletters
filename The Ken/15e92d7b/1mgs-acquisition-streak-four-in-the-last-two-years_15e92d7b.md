This Sequoia capital, Omidyar Network backed company seems to have quite some appetite. It has acquired four companies in the last two years.
For the longest time, we’ve been wondering if 1mg is all hunger or spray and pray gluttony.
The company has its version: every acquisition is strategic. To help 1mg venture into a space where the company has no expertise. That’s the reason it bought Homeobuy, Mediangels, Medd and Dawailelo. “At 1mg, we want to be integrated healthcare platform for all healthcare needs. We want everything to be available for the consumer,” says Prashant Tandon, co-founder and CEO of 1mg. Let’s break it down. 1mg wants to be the one-stop shop for all health needs. It will order medicine, get second opinions, discover doctors, and even book diagnostics. The whole gamut.
