Dear Ishan, good morning! 

I am thrilled and proud to introduce you to a new and original podcast today, the likes of which you will be hard-pressed to find. It’s also our first subscriber-only, premium podcast.

It’s called Two by Two.

Perhaps the best way for me to introduce it to you might be, well, a 2x2.

Each week, Two by Two will be where Praveen Gopal Krishnan (PGK) and I, as the co-hosts, will be joined by a few interesting and opinionated guests to discuss some of the biggest questions from the world of Indian business.

Notice I said questions, not answers. When we set out to create this podcast, we wanted to do the opposite of what most podcasts do.

Most podcasts are about narratives, about explanations, about scripts, and about answers.

Instead, we will freely discard any narratives we start with if that’s where our discussions demand. We are not an “explainer” show. We don’t (and won’t) have a script. And we won’t always have the right answers.

We hope to exist at the intersection of curiosity and synthesis. And for you to be able to think of Two by Two as your personal investigative business brain.

Always asking questions. Always connecting the dots. Unfiltered and uninhibited.

Each week, we’ll find the biggest and most interesting issues you need to know about. We’ll spot the hidden connections and unasked questions. We’ll identify the cast of players and their motivations. We’ll bring in incredible people to discuss these with. And we’ll try to answer fundamental questions like, what is going on, why is it happening, who gains and who loses, and where is all of this leading to?

Each episode of the Two by Two podcast will feature an important story investigated and discussed and visualized as a 2x2. You’ll even see it as the episode cover! Here’s our first episode, cheekily titled, “Will Flipkart become Phonepe before Phonepe becomes Flipkart?”

In the episode, we speak to Professor R Srinivasan and Srikanth Rajagopalan on the brewing rivalry between Flipkart and Phonepe, once parent and child, but now more like cousins.

Prof. Srinivasan teaches Strategy at Indian Institute of Management–Bangalore (IIMB). In fact he has been teaching strategy for over a quarter of a century, and for the last decade, focused on studying platform business models. He also leads IIMB's Centre for Digital Public Goods (CDPG). 

Srikanth is the CEO of Perfios Account Aggregation and runs Anumati—one of the earliest startups in the Account Aggregator space. In a career spanning nearly 30 years, Srikanth has worked at FMCG companies, tech startups, global credit card giants, telecom and cloud computing.

I’m thrilled to add that both Prof. Srinivasan and Srikanth have been subscribers of The Ken since 2016!

I’d love to tell you more about what we discussed, but perhaps you should just listen to us instead?
