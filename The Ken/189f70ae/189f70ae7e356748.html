Good Morning Ishan,

We don’t have a story for you today.

Instead, I have three things for you.

First, I’ll start with some good news.

Last week, The Ken announced the closing of a $2 million Series B funding round from two new investors, Rainmatter Capital and Baskar Subramanian, co-founder and CEO of Amagi Networks. This is an important point for us at The Ken, and it represents the start of a new, much more ambitious journey. You can read Rohin’s detailed blog post here, in which he writes about how we’re setting our sights on becoming a business-news company that will be around not just for seven more years but for decades to come.

The entire post is worth reading, but there’s one part in it that I think is particularly relevant today, of all days.

It’s what we call “business-model independence”.

For a news organisation to become truly sustainable in the long term, it requires a business model that is resilient, diversified, and independent.

The Ken has had a revenue model since day 1: subscriptions. We’ve stayed focussed on it through ups and downs because we realise that editorial independence and excellence flow from business-model independence. We thank our subscribers for that.

Over the last few years, thanks to subscribers like you, we’ve strived to preserve and keep this independence. We did this by constantly innovating and creating new products, new formats, and new business models to expand The Ken’s subscriber base exponentially.

This journey continues but with greater ambition and more rigour.

Frankly, it’s a bit terrifying to go where nobody has gone before, but we’ve come this far, and with some help, I think we can go further, one step at a time.

Second, I’m asking you for help.

We’re looking to find more talented colleagues alongside those of us already here at The Ken.

Do take some time and check out the roles. We spend almost as much time writing the job descriptions as we do writing our stories and newsletters (really!). Then, either write to us if you’re interested (via the apply link embedded at the end of each description) or forward it to a friend or colleague you feel might be a good fit at The Ken.

Also, we’re looking for talented Product Managers, Podcast Producers, and Graphic and UI Designers. So, if you want to work with us but don’t see a role that fits your experience and (more importantly) aspirations, please drop us an email at careers@the-ken.com telling us about both. The Ken was built by smart and driven professionals doing many things they never set out to do but still ended up doing.

Being independent requires a lot of work. It takes a long time to create and even longer to protect and preserve. And it’s something that cannot be done alone. That’s the lesson we’ve learnt over the last 77 years. We’re proud to be a small part of this long journey of this great nation.

This brings me to the most important thing.

From all of us at The Ken, Happy Independence Day.

Thank you.

Regards,
