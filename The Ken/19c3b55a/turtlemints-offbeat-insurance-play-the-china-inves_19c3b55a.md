Two opposite bets on how India buys insurance. Turtlemint is scaling by selling insurance through 600,000+ digital partners. Policybazaar is scaling by making insurance behave like e-commerce. Public markets have already validated one model. Policybazaar has shown operating leverage and profitability—making its pitch to retail investors simpler than Turtlemint’s. Regulatory experiments like Bima Sugam (and emerging structures like MGAs) could push distribution costs down and reshape channels.
