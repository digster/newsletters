Sai Gov Commented

Thanks for the article, Olina. We run a microschool and also build AI tools to help with learning. The problem with almost all the tools out there is they equate learning with the acquisition of information. That’s the wrong metaphor for learning. Kids learn to model the world, their place in it, and themselves. And their world is the culture and community they inhabit.

The way we currently do learning and education is to break it down and isolate concepts that are not rooted in anything meaningful. AI chatbots cannot help solve the meaning problem however socratic or suave they get. Where AI can help is figuring out how to connect things to their world and showing it, rather than droning on in walls of text. That would require some world building, which AI tools can help with.
