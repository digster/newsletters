How has the new year been for you? For me, it started with marriage. Mercifully, not my own. As my mother once said to an overbearing aunt―the sort you see twice a decade and who's always surprised you've aged―no woman deserves that sort of suffering. A wise woman, my mother. Sadly, I only inherited her waistline. 

But back to the wedding.

As I stood in attendance, trying to contemplate why anyone would willingly wear suspenders in the 21st century, I realised something. Five days into 2019, my cousin's year was already defined. Even if aliens landed tomorrow, he would forever remember 2019 as the year he got hitched.
