315,532,800.
That's the number of seconds in a decade. All of them filled with events and memories. As we near 2020, I've been wracking my brain trying to remember as much as I can about the decade gone by.
It's a lot to fathom. I began this decade a student, lived in a forest, moved to Delhi, stumbled into journalism, collected an ever-growing circus of cats, and now sit here writing to you. As I set my eyes on a new decade, I realise that I'm a few worlds removed from the person who entered the current one.
I imagine this is the case for you as well. Ten years is a long time, and change is inevitable.
Today's throwback by Janane is about exactly this—change in the past decade. Specifically, in India's healthcare space. It condenses ten year's worth of healthcare developments into a few important takeaways. Like how Ayushman Bharat has impacted the space. Or digital players. Or where India stands with regard to its vaccine-related goals.
