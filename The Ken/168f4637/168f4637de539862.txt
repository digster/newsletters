You see, Elon Musk has funded a program called the Global Learning XPRIZE - which is an open global challenge to build software to teach children basic reading, writing and arithmetic in 15 months. 

The value of the prize? $15 million. 

So far, five teams are in the finals - and one of them is an Indian company called Chimple. There’s another team - AmritaCREATE, which was a finalist in another XPRIZE, for promoting adult literacy.
