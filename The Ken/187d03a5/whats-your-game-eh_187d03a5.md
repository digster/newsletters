Have you ever wanted to learn something so badly that a little part within you would never give up? Is that desire powerful enough for you to make that dream come true, regardless of your age, situation, or the city you are in?
Let’s call that little part of me
Red. Red has focused on nothing but learning to play football ever since I started my full-time job in 2019.
Initially, it was difficult to find places that would train women in their 20s to play the sport, but Red wanted what it wanted. In 2022, I stumbled upon Prakrida
—a football academy for women of all ages. Red was over the moon.
Whenever the team is short of players, the coach makes sure we still play, even if it means playing with 6-year-olds. And let me tell you, defeating those kids is no piece of cake.
It’s been a month since I have been training, and very soon, I will be playing in the upcoming women’s football tournament. I’m kind of in disbelief, yet overjoyed at the moment.
