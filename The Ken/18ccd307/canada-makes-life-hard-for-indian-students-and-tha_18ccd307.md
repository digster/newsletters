Good Morning Ishan,
If you haven’t come across at least a dozen people keen (or desperate) to relocate to Canada in the past five years, you’ve basically been living under a rock.
Our growing obsession with Canada is reflected in the North American country becoming Indian students’ second-most-popular destination, after the US. What’s more, Indians accounted for 40% of the over 800,000 international students in Canada in 2022.
But Canada has decided to end its love affair with Indian students.
It has doubled the minimum required amount in international students’ bank accounts before they can land on its shores to over CA$20,600.The stated objective is to make sure the students can deal with the surging cost of housing.
But that’s certainly not the only reason.
To many Indians, studying in Canada is the simplest gateway to eventually living there. But the host nation has now wisened up to the “diploma mills” thriving almost entirely on foreign students, especially from India.
Some of these colleges don’t even have enough classrooms.
