For the past 19 episodes, we’ve covered hiring in many shapes and forms. But what do the ground-reported numbers tell us? And more importantly, what do the numbers hide?

 

In this episode of CTC, I speak to Ruchee Anand, Senior Director of Talent and Learning Solutions at LinkedIn. She’s been working with the social media giant for close to a decade now, and has been monitoring the ever-changing hiring cycles pretty closely. We discuss: 

 

1. How skill-shifting is a force to be reckoned with in the hiring ecosystem. 

2. Why women are being forced to ‘self-hire’ after making it past the VP level in companies.

3. Why 2022 was the year of ‘The Great Mismatch’.

4. The messy road towards true flexibility at the workplace. 

5. Why employers and platforms (like LinkedIn itself) have a long way to go in making the hiring process more equitable and diverse.
