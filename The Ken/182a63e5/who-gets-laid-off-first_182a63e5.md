By now, you’ve probably worried more than a few times about what the next 12 months might look like. The bugles of an oncoming depression are blowing, and startups that hired at insane hikes only months ago are now laying the same people off, much faster.
If it gets bad, it’ll get bad for everyone. But the real question is, who will get laid off first?
In the [second episode](https://the-ken.com/podcasts/cost-to-company/who-gets-laid-off-first/?utm_source=launch&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=ctc) of our weekly podcast Cost to Company, my colleague and the show’s co-host Shreevar, sets out to find an answer to this fatal question. Voices of those who got laid off, those who had to lay off, and those who’ve seen the mayhem before come together in this tight 20-minute listen to make sense of what's to come.
