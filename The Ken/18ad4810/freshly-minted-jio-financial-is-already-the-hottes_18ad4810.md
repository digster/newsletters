Good Morning Ishan,
The $17.6 billion-worth Jio Financial Services Limited (JFSL) is the cynosure of all eyes. If you are in financial services, you have likely stalked on Linkedin someone who snagged a role there. This, even though the product lineup is yet to be announced.
But there is fervent hiring activity going on inside the company. Mandates to recruitment firms have been given, and Jio Financial is even hitting campuses for placements.
A lot of JFSL’s success will depend upon the team it builds.
After all, a large part of the success of Bajaj Finance, India’s top NBFC, goes to the crack team assembled by the late Nanoo Pamnani, former chairman of Bajaj Finserv, and Rajeev Jain, managing director of Bajaj Finance. They had what it takes to build a leading financial institution.
The core team at Jio Financial Services similarly stands at the threshold that can make or break its future.
Gaurav takes us inside all the hiring action at the group—who will lead the team, what roles are being hired for, and what mistakes of the past the company would try to avoid this time.
