Unofficial Sources is built on the foundation of what we’ve done since The Ken began —to tell great narrative business stories in a way that’s sharp, original and analytical.
We started with just one story a day in India, and today we have a burgeoning Southeast Asia edition, a popular weekend newsletter—The Nutgraf and an acclaimed daily newsletter—Beyond The First Order.
And now, we hope to take the lessons learnt from all these products and apply them to a new format—audio.
Unofficial Sources is hosted by Anushka Chhikara and Olina Banerji who talk to The Ken’s reporters, sources, and others to tell compelling stories about what’s happening with some of the most significant companies in India.
The first episode of Unofficial Sources is based on Arundhati’s stellar story about Chinese instant loan apps in India. Anushka and Olina talk to people affected by the crisis, and create a compelling tale that affects far too many people, but isn’t told enough.
Oh, there's also a fun segment about vaccinations in India. I’d love for you to listen to it.
