Good Morning Ishan,

Yes, he really does. So we tried making an image of him being intersected by circles, but it reminded me of the days I was discovering MS Paint back in school. So I gave up.

If I did have that skill, I’d have put Sanwal at the intersection of three circles named “irreverent”, “iconoclast” and “sharp”. Who’s Sanwal? He’s the Venn diagram-loving CEO and founder of CB Insights. What’s CB Insights? Sigh. 

In their own words: "We aggregate and analyze massive amounts of data and use machine learning, algorithms and data visualization to help corporations replace the three Gs (Google searches, gut instinct and guys with MBAs) so they can answer massive strategic questions using probability, not punditry.”

Gotta love an organisation that’s chilled enough to put this on their about us page.

Anyhow, CB Insights is a subscription-based research and insights service. They unapologetically charge tens of thousands of dollars a year for access. They also run a rip-roaringly insightful (never thought I’d use those two words together, but that’s CB Insights for you) free newsletter (with nearly 400K subscribers) that skewers trends, insults pundits/VCs/suits and packs in insightful data & graphics, all together.

Ashish did a long interview with Sanwal during one of his recent India trips. It’s long (it’s Ashish). It’s fun (it’s Sanwal). And there’s nice, no-nonsense insight on how to build a business and knowing whom to ignore, whom to insult and whom to nurture.
