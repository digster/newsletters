Gotta love an organisation that’s chilled enough to put this on their about us page.

They also run a rip-roaringly insightful (never thought I’d use those two words together, but that’s CB Insights for you) free newsletter (with nearly 400K subscribers) that skewers trends, insults pundits/VCs/suits and packs in insightful data & graphics, all together.
