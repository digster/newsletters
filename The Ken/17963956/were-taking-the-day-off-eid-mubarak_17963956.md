We don't have a story for you today as we're taking the day off on account of Eid.
Festivals might not mean much today, when the world—and especially India—is going through an incredibly dark period. But a festival or public holiday is as good a time as any to spend some time with your loved ones, take care of them, and reassure them that this too shall pass.
It's also a good time to support the people and organisations that are fighting the good battle on the frontlines, risking their lives for billions of others. Please consider donating to such organisations.
Please also get yourselves, your family members, and anyone working for you who might not be as privileged, vaccinated at the earliest.
From all of us at The Ken, Eid Mubarak! Stay safe, be vigilant, and remember: the night is darkest just before dawn.
