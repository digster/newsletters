Using the visa-service-provider-consulate partnership, they turn the ‘unavailable’ slots for US and Schengen visas accessible

Rental startups emerged alongside tech-focused platforms, but they faced challenges shortly after. Scaling up beyond the top Indian cities has been a pain point. Competition came not just from other players in the market, but also from organised co-living chains. The Philippines plans to shut its doors to foreign offshore gamblers, but replacing the lost revenue won’t be easy
Philippine Offshore Gaming Operators, or POGOs, serve foreign gamblers who play online casino games. Criminal syndicates have turned to using POGOs as fronts for human trafficking, money laundering, and kidnapping. Despite that, around 80,000 people work in the sector who claim that the job pays too well to pass up. The government hopes to shut down all POGOs by the end of 2024, so the Philippines’ casino regulator is seeking new sources of tax revenue.
