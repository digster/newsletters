Good Morning Ishan,
If you are looking to own a swanky home with a budget of about Rs 2 crore in a metro city, you might be spoilt for choice.
But if you are taking that aspiration to Delhi-NCR, you’re likely building castles in the air.
Why? Because the money that buys a premium flat in other cities won’t get you far in the national capital region.
And the reason is simple: a supply crunch coupled with a sky-high demand.
As a result, prices in Delhi-NCR have soared nearly 30% year-on-year, with areas like Dwarka Expressway and Greater Noida seeing even steeper increases. Meanwhile, other tier-I cities have experienced a more modest 10% rise.
“This is absolutely insane pricing. And the worst part is these prices go northwards within a week’s time. What gives these builders the confidence to jack up prices to this level?” wondered a young potential homebuyer for whom, like many, buying a home is a once-in-a-lifetime investment.
