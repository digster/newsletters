Every Tuesday, we try to find answers. And to do this, we turn to the people experiencing this first-hand. This includes CEOs, executives, leaders…but most importantly, you — subscribers of The Ken.
This podcast is hosted by my excellent colleagues, Sneha Vakharia and Shreevar Chhotaria. I’m sure you’ll get to know Sneha and Shreevar better as you keep listening to Cost of Company every week, but you can read more about them [here](https://the-ken.com/blog/four-sets-and-one-additional-rep/) and [here](https://the-ken.com/blog/introducing-raveena-shreevar-tanmay-and-vanita/).
Oh, and one last thing.
Cost to Company is just the first of our new slate of podcasts. On Thursday, I’ll write to you again when we launch our second podcast, which I’m also super excited about.
We have several more titles in the works right now, and over the next few weeks, I can’t wait to share them with you.
Finally, our podcasts are free to listen to for now, but not for long. Very soon, they’ll go behind our paywall and will be available only for subscribers. We’ll also launch some new plans, so keep an eye out for that.
This is a new beginning. We will make a fair bunch of mistakes, and we count on you to keep showing them to us. Especially the ones that are hard to see.
Please share your feedback, suggest more topics we should cover, or let us know what’s happening at your workplace! You might get a chance to land up on our podcast and share it with the rest of the subscribers of The Ken.
