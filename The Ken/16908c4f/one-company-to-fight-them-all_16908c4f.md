Picture me. Writing this.
Typing, deleting. Checking Slack, back and forth. Looking at the charts shared by my colleague Harveen. Once. Twice. Then, taking a walk. Thinking. A bit baffled at the thought of the larger picture. The sheer imprint of it. At the thought that after the endless rounds of questions, in meetings in Bengaluru, Delhi, and Mumbai, now is the time to actually put it in words.
What is common to the following companies in India?
Google. Facebook. WhatsApp. Amazon. Walmart-Flipkart. Snapdeal. ShopClues. Apple. Spotify. Netflix. Bharti Airtel. Vodafone-Idea. Dish TV. Tata Sky. Paytm. Bennett Coleman & Company Ltd. Zee Media Corporation. Hotstar. Pine Labs. Ezetap. Lybrate. Big Basket. Grofers...and this list still doesn’t cover all of them.
The short answer is Reliance Jio. The long answer is in today’s story from my colleagues Prajakta and Harveen.
