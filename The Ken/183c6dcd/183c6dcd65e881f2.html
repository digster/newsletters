Hello Ishan,

 

Go to any job search platform, and the most prominent part of a job listing is the title. It’s there in big fonts, it’s what you’re looking for, it’s what draws you in. Titles matter to us, we think deeply about them, but something is really wrong with how we’re doing job titles. 

 

The phenomenon we're talking about today is dubbed Title Inflation. It's when your job title overstates the actual work you do. Much like real inflation, it misjudges the value of what (whom) it is applied to and is catastrophic if not brought under control. Businesses take what matters so much to employees and weaponize it. Often, just to seem more attractive. What’s worse? You too, might be suffering from it.

Title inflation turns job titles into Trojan horses housing an army of career-killing conceptions. Shreevar lifts the veil on exactly how this works in today’s episode of Cost to Company.

 

In this episode, we cover:

 

  1. What exactly is title inflation?
  2. Why does it happen at all? 
  3. Who is responsible for fixing it?
