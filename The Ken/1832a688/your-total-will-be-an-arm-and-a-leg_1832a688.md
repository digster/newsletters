I believe you’d agree that keeping oneself motivated to follow a fitness routine at home is one of the hardest things to do. However, I have found that the lure of new fitness gear always helps me get back on the mat.
This thought led me to my first mistake of the week—a sports and fitness store. The goal was to buy a few things to make my daily workouts more comfortable. Browsing through the store, I kept getting swayed by offers and discounts and, as you might have guessed, racked up a five-digit bill. Though I knew these things would make my life easier, the cost made me curb my enthusiasm.
It costs a tidy sum to get what you want. But sometimes, you have to rationalise your expectations and look for alternatives.
