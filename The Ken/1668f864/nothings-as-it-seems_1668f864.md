Good Morning Ishan,
I enjoy festivities. I think they are awesome. But with the festival that just passed us by that one half of my family indulges in, to delightful levels, I can be a slight sceptic. And this despite my namesake.
See, Durga Puja, all the fun, food, fanfare and feminism aside, is not exactly what it seems. Good vs evil is great. But what is good and what is evil also depends on who gets to tell its history. Same goes for Dussehra.
Don't get me wrong, it's a great time for revelry, for family and for that delectable dose of Bengali nostalgia, but it does remind one that multiple narratives are important - a theme our stories this week seemed to reflect.
