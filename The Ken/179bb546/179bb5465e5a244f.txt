"With great power comes great responsibility."

While Spider-Man's uncle Ben popularised this quote in the movies, nobody's quite sure of its origin. I once spent some time on the internet trying to dig that out. Some credit the great French writer and satirist Voltaire. Others note how the comics had it in a narrative box.

Who knows. It could very well be an Aesop's Fables life lesson.

And with reason. Because as cliched as the line may be, it rings true for so many companies and entities we cover here at The Ken. The first step to responsibility, I would argue, is accountability. And our coverage past week has been a ready reckoner on accountability for those in powerвЂ”at various levels.
