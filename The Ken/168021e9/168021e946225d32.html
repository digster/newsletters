Happy old year.
 
We all get so excited about the new year, one we know so little about. It's a bit like the adrenaline rush of a new relationship or job or city. And yet, all those things grow in value with the passage of time.
 
It is easy to forget 2018 and all that it has given us on this very last day. So, let's not. Let's acknowledge the gifts it has given us, the lessons it has taught us, the many times it spared us and the many times it didn't. 
 
And in that spirit of gratitude, I present to you our very last year-ender. This one's for those who supported us, wrote for us, made us diverse, made us better—our contributors. We've had lawyers, authors, startup founders, techies and journalists, all write for us. All the illustrations you see on The Ken are contributions, too.
 
When we think of our organisation from the inside, it's usually labelled according to location—"Bombay doesn't have space to stand - literally" "Delhi needs an air purifier" "Bangalore is always running out of milk - looking at you, Sameer"—or the people one reports to—Ashish's team or PGK's growing empire... But our contributors support our journalism just as well as anyone in our various offices, and they do so while bringing some fresh perspective.
 
So as I take a moment to acknowledge the importance of a year that had much to give and take, I'd also like to thank you, our support system, our contributors. 
 
Here's the recap - https://the-ken.com/best-of-2018-contributors/

The coming year will doubtless see many more join the ranks of our contributors, helping us get bigger and better with their expertise and point of view.
 
That will be it from us in 2018. We'll be taking tomorrow off on account of New Year's. See you on the 2nd. And oh, a very fruitful 2019 to you.
