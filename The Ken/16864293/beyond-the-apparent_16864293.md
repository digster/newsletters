Good Morning Ishan,
I'm not sure if I've ever formally told you guys this, but I work on the desk here. Our emails often reflect a writer's process—what was her idea, how did she convince the editors, were they sold, did they have questions... We quantify the value of our stories based on originality, analysis, deep research and the data they bring you.
I tell you all this because a deskie does none of these things. And yet, here I am, writing you emails, sometimes, week after week. So, quite certainly, there must be something not too obvious but important that enables me to? I'd like to think so.
