Doctors have bad handwriting; worse lifestyles. Nobody gives a credit card to a lawyer. A Muslim name must always be at the receiving end of terrorism jokes. A single woman in her 30s. Men are awesome at driving cars. Women suck. Fat people. People with tattoos. You start thinking about it, and boy, the stereotype life digs such a nice hole for itself. And strange how it seeps, ever so silently, into our daily lives. One moment you are your smug self. Next, in the hole.
