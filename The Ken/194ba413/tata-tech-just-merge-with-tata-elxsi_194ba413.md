Good Morning Ishan,
Have you ever taken a bite out of what looked like a decadent, fudgy, perfect chocolate cookie… only to realise with a sinking feeling that it was made out of oatmeal?
Then you probably know what it feels like to be an investor in Tata Technologies.
Rewind to its listing in November 2023, investors were clamouring to be part of the first listing of a Tata Group company in nearly two decades. The stock sold like hotcakes when it hit the bourses, more than doubling from the offer price.
The very same investors probably let out a groan of disappointment 10 days ago when the engineering-research-services company posted half-baked December-quarter results. Tata Tech’s profits declined, from the year-ago period, for the fourth quarter in a row. No wonder its stock has crashed 40% since the day of listing.
That’s just the way the cookie crumbles.
But maybe some chocolate chips could save the day, says Anand in today’s story. In this case, that’s Tata Elxsi—another investor darling-turned-disappointment whose bread and butter is also engineering-research services.
Say, the two firms were to stack up like pancakes. It’s no cakewalk, for sure. But their diversified client base and billion-dollar revenue would only strengthen the resulting giant.
Read Anand’s [story](https://the-ken.com/story/tata-tech-just-merge-with-tata-elxsi/?utm_source=daily_story&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=daily_newsletter) today to understand how merging could be a winning recipe for Tata Tech, Tata Elxsi, and their shareholders—now more than ever.
