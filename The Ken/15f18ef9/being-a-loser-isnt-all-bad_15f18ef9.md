Good Morning Ishan,
The winner takes it all
The loser standing small
Beside the victory
That's her destiny
Beautiful, beautiful, horribly defeatist lines from ABBA right there.
I mean, sure, the loser stands small, but the winner absolutely doesn't take it all. One, the winner doesn't have the experience of stepping back, learning, trying something new, etc. Two, the winner will never know what a risk is, for they have nothing to lose.
And three, the winner doesn't always make for a great story.
