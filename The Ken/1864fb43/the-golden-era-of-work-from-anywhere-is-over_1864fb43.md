Hello Ishan,
Remember the good ol’ days of working from anywhere?
What was once touted as the biggest work trend to emerge from the pandemic, is slowly but surely on its way out.
Companies are realising that the 'work from anywhere' model isn't sustainable in the long run. Or even in the short run, in some cases.
Meesho, for one, had announced an extravagant WFA policy in February 2022.
They scrapped it only a few months later.
But in 2023, it's not just the employers who're putting the final nail in the WFA coffin. It's the employees too.
In this episode of Cost to Company, I speak to [Joel D'Souza](https://www.linkedin.com/in/joeldsouza1/), (Program Manager- Culture and Talent at Headout), Aditi (Management Consultant at a Big 4 firm), and [Saurabh Deep Singla](https://www.linkedin.com/in/saurabhdeepsingla/) (CHRO, upGrad) to find out how the golden era of work from anywhere is over.
