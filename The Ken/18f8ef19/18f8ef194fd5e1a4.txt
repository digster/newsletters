The Vietnamese carmaker’s billionaire founder is keen on pushing the EV narrative, but it’s mostly his own companies on board

Vietnamese EV maker Vinfast has fallen short of meeting its ambitious sales targets in 2023. Sri Chaitanya and Narayana are out to conquer new territories, but their winning formula begs a million tweaks

For over three decades, south Indian coaching-prep behemoths Narayana and Sri Chaitanya have capitalised on Indians’ obsession with competitive exams. They are now bringing this fervour to northern markets, setting ambitious growth targets for national expansion, especially post-covid. A young and feisty regulator that is coming for them

Since 2022, NFRA has debarred 78 auditors and has imposed Rs 16.6 crore in penalties. Earlier, during ICAI’s tenure, only 25 rogue auditors faced action in over a decade, with around 1,400 cases left pending. The current clampdown from regulatory authorities has caused fear among the auditors, leading to a spurt in resignations, which have shot up over 4X in the five years leading up to March 2022. And the problem is rooted in their scope of work.
