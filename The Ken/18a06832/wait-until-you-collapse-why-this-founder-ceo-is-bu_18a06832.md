Good Morning Ishan,
Don’t read Seema’s interview with Anand Madanagopal, founder of Cardiac Design Labs, if:
- You’re sure your heart is just fine, and you don’t need to know about a fascinating new technology that can help identify and fix problems you didn’t know you had
- You’re not interested in an Indian startup that invented a cardiac patch—weighing just 33 grams—that checks for an irregular heartbeat without the discomfort caused by the test doctors currently recommend
- You don’t care that the company has managed to license the patch to one of the world’s largest medtech companies
- You’re not impressed by the fact this tech can be used even at hospitals that don’t have cardiologists or technicians familiar with cardiac tests or that it allows for a heart-rhythm test to be done for $20 when it costs $300 in the US
- You think it’s no big deal that this startup also has a disruptive device for monitoring vitals remotely that is already deployed at Border Security Force hospitals
But even if one of the above is not true, you should head to this link right away: [https://the-ken.com/story/wait-until-you-collapse-why-this-founder-ceo-is-busting-the-cardiac-care-myth/](https://the-ken.com/story/wait-until-you-collapse-why-this-founder-ceo-is-busting-the-cardiac-care-myth/?utm_source=daily_story&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=daily_newsletter) [10-minute read]
