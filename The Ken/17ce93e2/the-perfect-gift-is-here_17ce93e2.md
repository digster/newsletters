Happy Diwali from all of us at The Ken!
2021 is finally drawing to a close. And the holiday season is upon us. Diwali. Christmas. And then, a New Year itself.
If 2020 was quite the unusual year, then 2021 was the one where everything started getting better, then fell apart and now life seems to be slowly limping back to the way things were. Very slowly, all those little Zoom windows on our screens are giving way to buzzing offices, packed cafes and weekend trips with friends and family.
Amidst all this, the world of business in India and Southeast Asia is transforming at a rate faster than ever. Entire sectors are being upended. Funding is at an all-time high. Many companies are winning, both in valuations and in the market. And the future beckons to us, in strange and unimaginable ways.
We’re slowly returning to a world we abandoned. But this new world is vastly different from the one we left behind us.
At The Ken, we’ve spent the last few months launching multiple products to help subscribers make sense of the world that lies ahead of us. How can we, this holiday season, offer that to our colleagues, business partners, friends and family members?
