I am going to give you a peak behind the curtain at The Ken. Mornings start at 8 a.m. with this email. And at 9.05 a.m., Ashish sends out a message on our internal Slack channel, “Good morning @channel”. It sends dread down our spines. Each and every staff writer shudders. Because it is good for you to know the terror we feel. And do you know when I didn’t feel the terror? On my Eastern European trip in April. I miss the Baltic states. And whenever Ashish sends that dreaded message, I miss them even more.
And yesterday, I reminisced about them with Arundhati, who got a chance to go to one of my favourite cities, Tallinn, in Estonia. It is a strange city. Bleak and beautiful, both, at the same time. All I knew about it was the unpasteurised beer, warm wine and beautiful churches. But apparently, over 80% of the city is cashless. Also, it wants Indians to start up there. And Indians are actually starting up in that crazy city. I want to tell you more but I know this will be addressed by Ashish at 9.06 a.m.
