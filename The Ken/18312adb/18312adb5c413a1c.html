Today I have a new episode of Cost to Company for you—our careers and workplaces podcast. And it talks about the most important document you can have at work—a brag doc. 

 

I’m sure you’re wondering what a brag doc is, so I tried to make one for our podcast to explain it to you (trust me, this doc will help our dear podcast when appraisal season comes).

Three most important questions this episode answers: 

  1. Why is it essential for you to have a brag doc at work? 
  2. What are some of the best ways to have our achievements seen, understood, recognised and rewarded? 
  3. Last, and the question I love the most: How does gender affect brag docs?
