Theatres cost more than ever to run. India’s biggest cinema chain thinks it’s cracked a cheaper way to grow
PVR Inox is changing the way it runs its theatres by partnering with real-estate developers and franchises to share the capex load. That’s similar to what hotels like Marriott and Hyatt have long been doing, which has helped them expand in multiple regions at a low cost. But unlike hotel rooms that have an occupancy rate of about 80%, movies still log under 30%. PVR’s pivot also relies on its belief that theatres will continue to attract people seeking entertainment.
