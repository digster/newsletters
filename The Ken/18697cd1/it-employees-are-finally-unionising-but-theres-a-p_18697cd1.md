Hello Ishan,
February 2023 ends today!
Interestingly though, the road to unionisation for India’s IT employees has only begun.
Gone are the days when unions were something that only caught the fancy of the blue-collar workforce.
After a slew of unending layoffs, white-collar employees in the IT sector are finally waking up to the bells of unionisation.
But it’s not without a myriad of roadblocks.
The road to collective bargaining wasn’t easy. But no one knew it would be this hard.
In this episode of Cost to Company, I speak to Sreyan Chatterjee (Lawyer/Researcher; Ex-Reuters), Harpreet Singh Saluja (Founding Member, Nascent Information Technology Employees Senate) and Suman Dasmahapatra (National Convenor, All India IT & ITeS Union) to understand more about the treacherous path of unionisation.
