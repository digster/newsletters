Good morning Ishan,
What's the weekend looking like for you? Did you get a long Diwali break? Are you up in the mountains or at the nearest beach? Did you go home? Wherever you are, we hope you enjoyed the festival, preferably away from all the pollution.
If you’ve been on social media like me, you’ve probably seen many ask: Why would Delhi burst crackers after a blanket ban? Here’s a thought: Cities all over India continue to choke. The problem is larger than Delhi and cannot be dealt with a last-minute ban.
Sometimes, in order to understand what's happening around us, we need to look at the larger picture. In such situations, addressing the macro is the only way forward. Be it with India's post-Diwali pollution problem, or any of our stories at The Ken this week.
