First Principles

Sun, 04 Aug 24

Good Morning Ishan. We talk a lot about the monsoons and rain in this newsletter. But sometimes, those connote unimaginable tragedy.

Earlier this week, Kerala’s lush and beautiful Wayanad district suffered massive landslides brought on by heavy rain, which washed away two entire villages. The Hindu’s Abdul Latheef Naha has a heart-breaking on-ground report https://www.thehindu.com/news/national/kerala/death-rains-on-wayanad/article68477646.ece of the scale of the tragedy.

As I write this, over 300 men, women, and children have been confirmed dead. Dozens of rescue teams are conducting round-the-clock search missions. 

Please consider donating to the Kerala Chief Minister’s Distress Relief Fund either as an individual or group. 

Government of Kerala: Chief Minister’s Distress Relief Fund https://donation.cmdrf.kerala.gov.in/

I am a Malayali. I have friends from Wayanad. I’ve visited it numerous times. These are just a few of the reasons I couldn’t bring myself to think or write about anything else this weekend.

I hope to be back in your inboxes next Sunday. 

Thank you, stay safe, and please donate what you can. 

Regards,

Rohin
