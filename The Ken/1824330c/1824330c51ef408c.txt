Welcome to Ka-Ching! In this newsletter, we often pick unique payments features and try to break down the need for them. Whether for the user or the company offering it. 

These past few years, one such feature has been rising in popularity: paying rent via credit card. PhonePe, Paytm, No-broker, and Cred… all offer this option now. There are even platforms like RedGirraffe dedicated entirely to this use case. 

Until I came across promotions for this feature, I didn’t realise paying rent was something that needed such special treatment. I mean, all you need to do is set a standing instruction to debit your bank account at the end of the month. Use case solved. 

But it seems I couldn’t have been more wrong. A senior fintech executive I spoke to estimates that this could be a Rs 50,000 crore (~US$6.3 billion) market—per month(!).

How is paying rent via credit card a Rs 50,000 crore market?
