And just like that, the first week of April 2018 is gone, never to return. You probably tolerated a few silly Fool's Day shenanigans early in the week; I know we certainly did. Day 3 saw Arundhati write https://the-ken.com/story/banks-pos-plans-tied-in-a-three-legged-race/ on?utm_campaign=The%20Ken&utm_source=Email&utm_medium=Newsletter banks and Point of Sales, and how surprisingly dependent the former is on the latter. Long queues of companies waiting for permission slips."
