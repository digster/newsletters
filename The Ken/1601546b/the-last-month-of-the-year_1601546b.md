If you somehow haven't noticed, the last month of the year is here. For many, it means festivities, the warmth of a family, home. For many others, it means aggressively working in tandem with a blink-and-you-miss world.
The Ken falls into the second category.
While it gets colder around the world—even here, in Bengaluru, thanks to cyclonic rains—we're working to bring you stories fresh out of the oven. And everybody loves a good bake in December.
Take, for instance, the week that went by.
We started with a [contributor piece](https://the-ken.com/indian-self-publishing/) on publishing written by Amish. You needn't have read between the lines here, for he spelt out exactly how technology is helping evolve publishing in India, one self-published book at a time.
Tuesday saw [Payal focus](https://the-ken.com/amazon-now-maybe-much-later/) on the burgeoning grocery sector, with players like Amazon trying their hand at sending you that loaf of bread you'd really like. Amazon Now, though, is no bigbasket.

We hope this kickstarts your December. Do subscribe to The Ken.
