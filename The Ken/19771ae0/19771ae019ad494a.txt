Indian EV makers could be forced to halt production by 15 July, industry insiders warn. Licensed investment guides mostly cater to the rich. Fintechs like ET Money and Value Research may have a solution for those who aren’t served

Sebi-registered investment advisors are declining in number, even after the regulator made it possible to conduct business more freely. Most retail investors either invest in direct plans without proper guidance, or buy a regular plan—both lead to lacklustre returns. Fintechs now offer automated advice for direct plans, but can they plug all the gaps?
