There are very few things in the world that I like more than driving a car. I know it sounds completely silly, and you probably are wondering what sort of lunatic enjoys driving in Indian city traffic. My sort of lunatic.
I love driving because that is the one thing I can do with as much confidence as Salman Khan when he's shirtless or Mark Zuckerberg when he's saying things he really shouldn't be saying. It's brazen; it's brilliant.
Anyway, I'm not taking you for a pointless ride here. I do this most weeks. Sort of sit back and look through our week's stories, and this week, all I could think about was road signs. Maybe I miss driving too much or maybe I'm on to something here.
