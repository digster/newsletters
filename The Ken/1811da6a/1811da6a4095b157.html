Good Morning Ishan,
 
Earlier this month, Indian wear retailer Manyavar gave its investors something to cheer.
 
Its parent company Vedant Fashions Ltd (VFL), which was listed this February, saw its revenue for the year ended March 2022 rise 85% and its bottom line 135%. And despite the volatility in the markets, its shares are up 12% since its listing.
 
In fact, you may not know this, but VFL is India's third-largest apparel retailer by market cap. Only behind Trent, which runs Westside, and Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail (ABFRL), which owns Pantaloons.
 
The most apparent difference between VFL and the other two is that VFL focuses primarily on celebration wear, especially for men.
 
But there are a few other reasons why VFL stands out. 
What is the real reason behind VFL's success?
