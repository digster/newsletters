The death of 20 workers in a fire in Goa has thrown the spotlight on major shortcomings of the Employee Provident Fund Organisation. Ather Energy, Toyota Kirloskar, and JSW Group are driving a shift as higher costs and scarce land begin to slow expansion and strain component supply
Hosur, India’s EV-manufacturing epicentre, is getting too crowded to hold the growing industry. Rising land prices and labour costs are pushing EV makers to consider secondary options, like Maharashtra’s Sambhajinagar. Along with subsidies, the city offers a legacy of automobile manufacturing and a vendor base built over generations. The combination has already drawn companies like Ather, Toyota Kirloskar Motors, and JSW to set up shop in the city’s newest industrial hub.
