Is EPFO the reason your employer isn’t depositing your provident fund?

The EPFO, India’s largest retirement-corpus provider, has faltered in keeping track of defaulting employers, affecting millions of employees. Hosur powered India’s EV boom. So why are companies heading to Sambhajinagar?

Ather Energy, Toyota Kirloskar, and JSW Group are driving a shift as higher costs and scarce land begin to slow expansion and strain component supply

Hosur, India’s EV-manufacturing epicentre, is getting too crowded to hold the growing industry. Rising land prices and labour costs are pushing EV makers to consider secondary options, like Maharashtra’s Sambhajinagar. Along with subsidies, the city offers a legacy of automobile manufacturing and a vendor base built over generations. The combination has already drawn companies like Ather, Toyota Kirloskar Motors, and JSW to set up shop in the city’s newest industrial hub. [Read the full story]
