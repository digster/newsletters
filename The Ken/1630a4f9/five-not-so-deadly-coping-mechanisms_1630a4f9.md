You've heard of the seven deadly sins. Greed. Lust. Gluttony. Pride. Wrath. Envy. Sloth. I studied literature, so these seven were the kind of suspect friends Snow White probably had in that home with seven little people. Or perhaps, she was the suspect one. Who knows, eh?
I'm not writing this to tell you about these sins - to be fair, they're fairly self-descriptive. Nope. It's just that I was going through this week's stories to write to you and I realised something. We largely covered five not-so-deadly coping mechanisms over our five stories. Let's have a look; see if you agree with me?
