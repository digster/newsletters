Through the perspectives of those most impacted by a tectonic shift in how we work, this episode takes a sharp look at what we call home, what we call office, and why they aren’t the same in the first place.
Join Shreevar Chhotaria as he talks to employees and employers to figure out what this new breed of employees really wants from their workplace.
Since I had the privilege of listening to this episode before it went out, I can tell you some questions that get answered during its duration:
- What’s it like being stuck at home, away from home?
- Why are employees latching on to work from home doing so?
- What happens when companies enforce any one of the two?
- What is it that GenZs really want out of their workplaces?

In other updates
My favourite First Principles episode dropped last week. It features Nithin Kamath, the founder of Zerodha, and I must say that it’s a company out of a fairy tale book. Why?
- Nearly no attrition in 5 years
- No inflation in team size as the user base grew fivefold
- No funds raised. Ever. Ever. And yet valued at nearly 6 billion dollars by Nithin’s standards.
Nithin shares some excellent insights about leadership, mentoring teams to help them grow and finding unlikely heroes in unlikely places. You can give the episode a listen anywhere you like.
