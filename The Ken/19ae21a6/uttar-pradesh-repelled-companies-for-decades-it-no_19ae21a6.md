Uttar Pradesh isn’t the first place that comes to mind when you think of ease of doing business. Yet, that hasn’t stopped a range of companies from planting their electronics-manufacturing facilities in the state.
Dixon Technologies, Samsung, Oppo, Haier, and more now assemble their products in Greater Noida. It’s a revival of sorts—fresh investments are pouring in, and thousands of jobs are being created.
But look closely, and you’ll find that this development is concentrated in Greater Noida and along the Yamuna Expressway—essentially UP’s western belt. The rest of the state remains largely agrarian, and the benefits from new developments are not evenly distributed.
Major manufacturers are setting up shop in Uttar Pradesh not because the state is aggressively building out infrastructure and offering incentives to businesses, but because it’s right next to Delhi.
