Good Morning Ishan,
 

In case you missed last week’s update, The Nutgraf is on a two-week break because I am on vacation (which ends tomorrow). 

 

My break was a lot of fun. I walked around Tokyo till my legs could barely support me, cycled around a volcano, hiked across a moss-covered ravine, and snorkelled in an empty bay. While other tourists usually go around marvelling at Japanese food, culture, and other things, I am from Bengaluru, so I couldn’t stop congratulating locals on their functioning metro system. 

 

Somewhere in the middle, I even found time to teach some curious Japanese kids how to play arcade games.

As they say here a million times a day, arigato gozaimasu and see you all next week.

 

Regards,

Praveen Gopal Krishnan
