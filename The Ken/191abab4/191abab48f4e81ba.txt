Matchmaking between cash-strapped hospitals and the booming insurance market, startups like Gmoney and Digisparsh are chasing the 10 million patients seeking  reimbursements

The number of people covered by health insurance has increased more than 2.5X over the past decade. And recent government scheme budgets and cashless claims indicate a burgeoning US$5.7 billion market for cashless transactions. But 85% of health facilities in India are small-to-mid-sized hospitals, which frequently encounter months of delays for cashless claims settlements from insurers. So, a new wave of health fintechs is emerging to address the cashless claims issue for hospitals. But non-bank competitors may soon begin to take notice.
