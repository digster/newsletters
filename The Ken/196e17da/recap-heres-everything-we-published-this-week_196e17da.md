More skills, same pay, and barely any job guarantee—the IT services giant is raising the bar for freshers
Infosys trainees are in for a rough ride, as qualifying tests for 2024-25 become tougher due to higher “business standards”. This is an IT sector-wide phenomenon—service companies want trainees who come with higher skillsets, and faster billability. Freshers’ salaries, however, have remained stagnant for almost a decade. Companies use these lower salaries to maintain margins amidst business slowdown, as they face intense competition from other service firms, GCCs, and automation.
