Seema's piece on Wednesday covered just about any question you may have had about Mankind Pharma—endorsed by the likes of Sunny Leone, Amitabh Bachchan, Bipasha Basu. The company, which values itself at more than $4 billion (disputed number), is expanding. And fast.
 
The week closed with Swiggy and Zomato, for very different reasons. Payal and Patanjali's co-authored Swiggy piece on Thursday discussed the Flipkart-Swiggy marriage, and how it curiously works.
 
Patanjali's piece on Zomato yesterday though was all about its big offering—Zomato Gold. The story traced Zomato's (alleged) initial 'fine diner, crème de la crème' promises to restaurateurs and how it eventually signed up everyone and their uncle for Gold. Pro tip: If you've got Gold, max out this weekend before restaurants consider pulling out.
