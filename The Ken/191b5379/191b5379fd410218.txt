<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Dear Ishan,</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A warm welcome to </span><i><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Ken</span></i><span style="font-weight: 400;">. I am Seema, Editor and co-founder. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Over seven years ago, we launched with a simple promise to our subscribers: we’d publish one original, analytical, and deeply reported </span><b>longform story</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> every weekday. We believed—and continue to believe—that in an era replete with quick-takes and rehashed press releases, the value of longform is priceless.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In business journalism, nuance and analysis are critical. Without them, business journalism cannot tell you the full story, or the real story. We believe the best way to do that is through longform. This is why, while we have added many more products over the years like newsletters, visual stories, and podcasts, our longform features are widely loved by our subscribers even today.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The journey of a longform story is fascinating. It starts with a hypothesis, followed by dozens or even hundreds of conversations with sources and stakeholders, all of which are vetted and analysed to arrive at a final narrative arc. When we say original and deeply reported, we mean it. We go above and beyond to create a story that tells the truth in a way that is interesting to you. It takes about 15 days on an average for each of our longform features to go from idea to published story. Sometimes even more. And it’s not just the time taken, but the sheer effort that goes into it that makes them special.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We chronicle the biggest disruptors in India’s startup ecosystem and internet economy, and dig deep into large publicly listed tech and consumer companies. And we do it for you, our paying subscribers.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Years of such effort have let us create a rich and valuable archive of stories, some of which remain valid even today, years later. You can simply search for a company or topic on our site to find such stories, or head over to our ‘All Stories’ section.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To get you started, I’ve compiled a list of stories published over the last six months that our subscribers absolutely loved. </span></p>
<p><a href="https://the-ken.com/story/you-can-retire-at-40-and-thats-a-lie/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You can retire at 40. And that&#8217;s a lie</span></a></p>
<p><a href="https://the-ken.com/story/zepto-is-a-quick-commerce-bronze-medallist-acting-like-a-gold-medallist/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Zepto a quick commerce bronze medallist acting like a gold medalist</span></a></p>
<p><a href="https://the-ken.com/story/pharmeasys-hard-sell-puts-its-3-9-million-customers-health-at-risk/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Pharmeasy&#8217;s mis-selling puts it&#8217;s 3.9 million customer&#8217;s health at risk</span></a></p>
<p><a href="https://the-ken.com/story/inside-flipkart-a-high-pressure-workplace-thanks-to-its-ipo-dreams-and-walmart/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Inside Flipkart: a high-pressure workplace thanks to it’s IPO dreams. And Walmart</span></a></p>
<p><a href="https://the-ken.com/story/at-home-blood-tests-are-soaring-and-throwing-up-wrong-results/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">At-home blood tests are soaring, and throwing up wrong results</span></a></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Reading these stories would be a great way to understand the value of </span><i><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Ken</span></i><span style="font-weight: 400;">’s longform features. Also, our subscribers have left some very interesting perspectives in the comments section, so don’t forget to check them out and participate in the conversations.</span></p>
<p>You can read these on <i><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Ken</span></i><span style="font-weight: 400;">’s</span> <a href="https://apps.apple.com/in/app/the-ken/id1282944688">iOS</a>, <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ken.core&amp;hl=en_IN">Android</a> and <a href="https://apps.apple.com/in/app/the-ken/id1282944688">iPad</a> apps too. With the app, you can not just read on the go but also personalise your reading experience. I&#8217;d recommend downloading the app.</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We know that it takes time to start appreciating longform stories, especially if you aren’t in the habit of reading for longer than five–eight minutes. But we believe that it is something worth investing in.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">That’s it from me. You’ll occasionally see me in your inbox introducing one of our longform stories. Until then, happy reading!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Regards, </span></p>
