Good Morning Ishan,

Chances are the majority of our readers haven’t heard of MX Player. But chances also are that by tomorrow, many of you will be wanting to know more about this Korean mobile video app. 

Because Times Internet, the digital arm of India’s media behemoth, the Times of India Group, is acquiring MX Player. For roughly $2 per user.

There’s just one additional detail—MX Player has over 100 million of those. And oh, Times Internet is apparently paying most or all of it in cash. 

Ka-ching, baby!
