As you are reading this, on the 13 November 2018, there are more than a hundred startups in India who believe there is a significant opportunity in helping Indians pick a mutual fund. Either through an app or a website. Some believe they can get these customers by charging them a small fee; others say what’s small must only be disrupted by free.
It is too early to tell who is winning, but just about the right time to ask questions. Where exactly are these new-age digital mutual funds alternatives getting their models? Primarily, the US and China. Are the American and Chinese models suited to India? Are there ways to adapt? And most crucially, is there an outright better model to create a roadmap for India?
