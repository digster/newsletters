Good Morning Ishan,
Some of you may have bags packed. Others may be preparing cakes, mulled wine and other delights. Some may be preparing their bodies to party hard without collapsing the next morning. Whatever it may be, the year ends in a week and everyone has plans.
The one question everyone asks everyone else this time of the year, "Sooo, what plans?" Well, I'm writing this to tell you that it's okay to not have any celebratory plans. Maybe your plans are to meet certain goals you couldn't this year, due to work or your daily routine; I know I sure fall in that category. No pressure.
Calendar years are fascinating, for they're as random as language—it helps us encapsulate life into meaningful fragments. "Oh, 2016 was when I lost a tooth!" or "In 2005, I dated a celebrity..." And so, as random as a year is, there's a lot to look back at and reflect on. Something we'll be doing over the coming week, in a most exciting way.
But while we're on the subject of looking back, let's get down to our last weekly recap of 2018, shall we?
