Good Morning Ishan,

Coffee. Sunday. You. Me. All good things together, so let’s begin. 

Now, this human being says, and I am quoting him: “The incel issue is not about dating; it is a political war.” Hmm..that’s interesting. What does this sentence even mean? So, I read more: “...it is not a matter of who you would date, it is a fucking war going on here. Don't think it's just about you, it's about MEN in general, female supremacists will try to abolish any male problem because nowadays the "victim" wins, if you can show how much of a "victim" you are you will gain power, and so by trying to present incels as the problem itself and not people with problems they try win this war.”

Human, you’ve got to be kidding me.

Now I don’t take this world very seriously, and I don’t recommend otherwise to anyone. So, at first, I thought this is a joke. I understand rage. I get disappointment. I understand the Internet and the anonymity and comfort of its dark corners, but this; who talks like this? Nasty surprise! A whole lot of people. People who identify themselves as incels or involuntary celibates, the two most random words put together to form a ridiculous phrase in the English language, after Donald Trump. 

...But I am getting ahead of myself. Let’s talk about incels. 

The incels are a group who see themselves as the victims of a culture that sexually favours good-looking people. As a result of which, they don’t get laid. Their premise is that their demands for sex should be met via enforced monogamy, or else…Incels have an internet-solidified language. A Chad is an American jock-variety male (or basically anyone they consider better looking than them); Chad’s female counterpart in these spaces is a Stacy (a woman who rejected them). Chads and Stacys are to be hated—and also acquired in the latter’s case. These are men and women ranked eight and above on a scale of 10, and thus, not “sub8”. Beyond these allegedly super attractive humans, there’s the Normie—anyone that incels don’t see as their own. Women, by definition, aren’t accepted as incels.

As the good, old cliche goes, this is just the tip of the iceberg.
