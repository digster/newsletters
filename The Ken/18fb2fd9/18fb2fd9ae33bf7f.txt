The question is not whether it should be at a discount to Zomato, but by how much. Without leaving too much money on the table

Swiggy’s going public at a time when Zomato shares have trebled over the past year, thanks partly to Blinkit’s staggering rise. Zomato’s dream run has laid bare Swiggy’s struggles in food delivery and quick commerce, in both of which it has ceded ground in recent years. Unlike food delivery, quick commerce is not a two-horse race, complicating Swiggy’s prospects. Once it lists, Swiggy has to measure up to Zomato in delivering on its targets quarter after quarter. In India, six out of 10 people diagnosed with cancer today will die of this disease. Screening for early detection of cancer is needed, but its business model has a problem.
