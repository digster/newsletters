Hello Ishan,

Christmas is approaching, and we at The Ken, take Christmas rather seriously. We even have a fully decorated tree sitting prettily in the middle of our workspace. There may or may not be a Secret Santa. We’ve not decided yet.

We’re giving you more.

That’s not all. We launched the Partner Subscriptions last month, and that got us thinking—December is also the wedding season. Why not give you the option to gift a Couple Subscription? 

And that’s exactly what we’ve done. 

Starting today, you can be the thoughtful friend who gifts happy couples an enormous wealth of knowledge. At a nominal price of course. Gift cards may last only till they are spent, but The Ken’s Couple Subscription will keep you in their fond memories forever.
