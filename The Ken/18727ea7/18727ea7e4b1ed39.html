Earlier this month, the Prime Minister inaugurated a two-day Global Millets Conference in the national capital. He even released a postal stamp and the official coin on the occasion.

 2023 has been declared  International Year of Millets by the UN.

 

Ever since the year began, every week we have been having some event or the other celebrating millets. 

 

The army has included them in its rations, state governments are promoting them to farmers, and companies are launching packaged millet-based foods.

 

The conversation about why millets should be the next big thing in food seems to be getting louder and louder.

 

But how do you create the next big food trend? Can you even do something like that? Because at the end of the day, such matters are decided at the dining table.
