Good Morning Ishan,

If 2017 was the year millions of Indians started making digital payments for all sorts of products and services, 2018 will see many of those same Indians being offered digital loans to finance many of their purchases. And not entirely ironically, the payments from 2017 would’ve laid the path for the loans of 2018.

A bunch of stars are aligning in 2018 for digital lending startups. Millions of customers. A government that has put its weight behind digital payments. Digital KYC enabled through Aadhaar. An update to the UPI money transfer protocol that allows for automatic repayment deductions from bank accounts. And most importantly, online trails for all purchases and payments.

Buy an LED TV? Here’s a loan. Get an iPhone? Here’s a loan. Want cosmetic surgery? Here’s a loan. Want to repay your credit card bill? Here’s a loan.

Digital lending startups are in the thick of the action, of course. But so are banks, who are partnering them to address new segments. As are the big kahunas like Paytm and Flipkart’s PhonePe, who own the largest trails of transactions.
