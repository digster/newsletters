T-shirt slogans are very rarely smart. In fact, they most often devolve into poor jokes and cliches. But every so often, someone walks past you with an ingenious message scribbled across their chest. I remember the greatest shirt slogan I ever saw. “Apparently” it said. Nothing more. Minimal? Of course. But also the perfect disinterested put down to almost any statement.
“Nice shirt!”
Apparently…
But recently, I saw a shirt that was actually sorta profound. Not jump out of the tub yelling “Eureka!” profound. But well above par for musings printed on mass-produced tees. Humans, it read, are the only animals that wake up when they’re tired and go to sleep when they aren’t.
The weekend plays out in a similar way. We spend the better part of our work week counting down to it. We call friends. Plan every detail. And then it’s upon us and we lose all desire to interact with the world outside. Before you know it, it’s Monday morning and the cycle begins again.
I know what you’re thinking—God, I hope my stacked weekend doesn’t pan out like that. Well, here’s a good way to kickstart it: catch up on The Ken stories you may have missed this week. I know, I know. You aren’t a paid subscriber. Well, that takes about a minute to change. And even if you don’t do that, there’s our free story. And, if it’s one of our paid pieces that catches your eye this week, guess what, we’ve rolled out a pay-per-piece feature. Basically, there’s something for everyone.
