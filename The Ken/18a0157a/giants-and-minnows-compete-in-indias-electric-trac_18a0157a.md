Good Morning Ishan,
The annual wrestling matches of Haryana became world-famous after the Aamir Khan-starrer Dangal moved audiences nearly seven years ago. Such was the power of this true story of a wrestler and his two daughters.
It was a roaring commercial success, too.
Make a guess: Which product has chosen the wrestling fields of Sonipat to sway the (local) audience?
When Rounak visited Aterna on Tuesday, nearly a thousand men, mostly farmers, had gathered for a wrestling showdown. Quietly came an e-tractor to smoothen the fields after every bout.
It’s kinda cute.
Heck, anything to bring about a seismic shift in mindset. Tractors are as much about farming and hauling as they are about rumble and machismo. Autonxt Automation, a seven-year-old Mumbai-based electric tractor manufacturer, chose the annual wrestling tournament to showcase its e-tractor to farmers.
India is the world’s largest tractor market. Some 830,000 homegrown tractors ploughed their way into the fields in the year ended March 2023. Auto giant Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M), with 37% of the overall tractor market share, has been characteristically quiet on the e-tractor front. But some newcomers and some surprise bettors are revving up the segment.
As Shaswata, Rounak, and Nathan write today, e-tractors are naturally facing their own set of issues, just as electric bikes and cars faced in the beginning, even though the total cost of ownership over 10 years for e-tractors equals their diesel counterparts.
The question is, will the tractor market level the playing field like Ola Electric and Ather Energy did for the electric two-wheelers before the incumbents such as TVS and Hero joined the transition? And what do Aterna’s farmers think?
