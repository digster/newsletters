Study I’m excited to share

“Psychedelic drug helped people with alcohol use disorder reduce
drinking, study shows (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/8ku6kplgkosoh02d68vi5/dpheh0hq94340eam/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubmJjbmV3cy5jb20vaGVhbHRoL21lbnRhbC1oZWFsdGgvcHNpbG9jeWJpbi1tdXNocm9vbS1oZWxwLXBlb3BsZS1hbGNvaG9sLXVzZS1kaXNvcmRlci1yY25hNDQxODA=
).” From the article: “More than 80% of those who were given the
psychedelic treatment had drastically reduced their drinking
eight months after the study started, compared to just over 50%
in the antihistamine (active placebo) control group, according to
results published Wednesday in JAMA Psychiatry (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/8ku6kplgkosoh02d68vi5/e0hph7hk3pxp0qh8/aHR0cHM6Ly9qYW1hbmV0d29yay5jb20vam91cm5hbHMvamFtYXBzeWNoaWF0cnkvZnVsbGFydGljbGUvMTAuMTAwMS9qYW1hcHN5Y2hpYXRyeS4yMDIyLjIwOTY_Z3Vlc3RBY2Nlc3NLZXk9ZTEzMjhhN2EtNmU3OS00M2Y4LWFkMDQtMTQzZmQ4MTM0ODM5JnV0bV9zb3VyY2U9Rm9yX1RoZV9NZWRpYSZ1dG1fbWVkaXVtPXJlZmVycmFsJnV0bV9jYW1wYWlnbj1mdG1fbGlua3MmdXRtX2NvbnRlbnQ9dGZsJnV0bV90ZXJtPTA4MjQyMg==

study’s participants: “When he began the psilocybin trial at age
25, he was consuming almost 23 drinks in a single bender.” Flash
forward to today, and Jon “has stayed sober since the trial and
founded a nonprofit organization for psychedelic mental health
research.” Huge kudos to Dr. Michael Bogenschutz (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/8ku6kplgkosoh02d68vi5/7qh7h8h02kgkozbz/aHR0cHM6Ly9jZW50ZXJmb3Jwc3ljaGVkZWxpY21lZGljaW5lLm9yZy9taWNoYWVsLWJvZ2Vuc2NodXR6Lw==
), Dr. Stephen Ross (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/8ku6kplgkosoh02d68vi5/owhkhqh4mkzkr5iv/aHR0cHM6Ly9jZW50ZXJmb3Jwc3ljaGVkZWxpY21lZGljaW5lLm9yZy9zdGVwaGVuLXJvc3MtbWQv
), and the entire team at the NYU Langone Health Center for
Psychedelic Medicine (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/8ku6kplgkosoh02d68vi5/z2hghnho24g435tp/aHR0cHM6Ly9jZW50ZXJmb3Jwc3ljaGVkZWxpY21lZGljaW5lLm9yZy8=
