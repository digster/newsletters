Tech trick I found useful
-------------------------

​Resetting your cellular data stats on an iPhone (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/lmuzwpd0wosmhnkn8x8c3i895z000bgh0o4/48hvhehmq6n398ix/aHR0cHM6Ly9zdXBwb3J0LmFwcGxlLmNvbS9lbi1jYS9ndWlkZS9pcGhvbmUvaXBoM2RkNWYyMTMvaW9zIzp-OnRleHQ9VG8lMjByZXNldCUyMHRoZXNlJTIwc3RhdGlzdGljcyUyQyUyMGdvLGJvdHRvbSUyQyUyMHRoZW4lMjB0YXAlMjBSZXNldCUyMFN0YXRpc3RpY3Mu
). I was consuming mobile data at an unusually high rate (e.g.,
“You have 10% remaining for the next 10 days”), and I followed
these steps to figure out what the hell was gobbling it up. Step
one is resetting your cellular data stats to zero, which most of
us did years ago, if ever: “To reset these statistics, go to
Settings > Cellular, scroll to the bottom, then tap Reset
Statistics.” Within a few hours, I knew who the offenders were
and could minimize overages.

What I’m enjoying in the morning
--------------------------------

​Vashon Island Coffee Dust (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/lmuzwpd0wosmhnkn8x8c3i895z000bgh0o4/reh8hohmvwep7lt2/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudmFzaG9uY29mZmVlZHVzdC5jb20v
). One of the simplest ways to reduce glucose/insulin spikes and
improve health (and lose more fat) is to change how you drink
your daily coffee. A lot of readers of The 4-Hour Body (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/lmuzwpd0wosmhnkn8x8c3i895z000bgh0o4/8ghqhohopqx83rik/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9Ib3VyLUJvZHktVW5jb21tb24tSW5jcmVkaWJsZS1TdXBlcmh1bWFuL2RwLzAzMDc0NjM2M1gv
) have thanked me for the tip to use cinnamon and/or vanilla
extract instead of their usual milk and sugar. Achtung! Some
artificial sweeteners (sucralose, saccharin, acesulfame-K, etc.)
can trigger insulin release through taste receptors or metabolic
pathways, even in the absence of carbohydrates.
​
​Now, cinnamon is great, but it can lose its novelty. That’s
where Vashon Coffee Dust comes in: “The Coffee Dust Flavor
Expedition Kit is your all-access pass to the magical world of
coffee flavoring—with zero sweeteners, preservatives, or
nonsense. Inside, you’ll find 72 servings of flavor-packed
goodness across all six of our fabulous blends, plus a perfectly
sized spoon because you deserve precision when sprinkling joy
into your coffee.” I particularly love the Healthful (cinnamon,
turmeric, ginger, nutmeg, cardamom, black pepper, sea salt) and
Hot Momma (Ghirardelli cocoa, cayenne, cinnamon, sea salt), but
they’re all delicious.
