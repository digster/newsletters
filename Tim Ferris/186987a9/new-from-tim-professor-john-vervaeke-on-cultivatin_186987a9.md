Vervaeke is the director of UToronto’s Consciousness and Wisdom Studies Laboratory and its Cognitive Science program, where he teaches Introduction to Cognitive Science and The Cognitive Science of Consciousness, emphasizing the 4E model, which contends that cognition and consciousness are embodied, embedded, enacted, and extended beyond the brain.
Vervaeke has taught courses on Buddhism and Cognitive Science in the Buddhism, Psychology, and Mental Health program for 15 years. He is the author and presenter of the YouTube series “[Awakening from the Meaning Crisis](https://click.fourhourmail.com/r8ug29rn25boh25z7nxc0/l2hehmhog2x026u6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vcGxheWxpc3Q_bGlzdD1QTE5EMUpDUnE4VnVoM2YwUDVxanJTZGI1ZUMxWmZad1dK)” and his brand new series, “[After Socrates](https://click.fourhourmail.com/r8ug29rn25boh25z7nxc0/m2h7h5hor54g5eim/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_dj1iSUp1SU42a1VjVQ==).”
