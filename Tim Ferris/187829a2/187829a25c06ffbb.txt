Documentary I’m revisiting
--------------------------

​Bird by Bird with Annie (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/27u53lgo36fohv7p0oes0/7qh7h8hokvx4w8hz/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9ncC9wcm9kdWN0L0IwMDNQTjJPVjIv
). I’ve loved Anne Lamott (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/27u53lgo36fohv7p0oes0/owhkhqhrk308gluv/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDIxLzA3LzA5L2FubmUtbGFtb3R0Lz91dG1fc291cmNlPWNvbnZlcnRraXQmdXRtX21lZGl1bT1jb252ZXJ0a2l0JnV0bV9jYW1wYWlnbj01YmY=
) (@ANNELAMOTT (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/27u53lgo36fohv7p0oes0/z2hghnh349mdp8ip/aHR0cHM6Ly90d2l0dGVyLmNvbS9Bbm5lTGFtb3R0
)) and her work for more than a decade, and ever since I had her
on the podcast (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/27u53lgo36fohv7p0oes0/owhkhqhrk308gluv/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDIxLzA3LzA5L2FubmUtbGFtb3R0Lz91dG1fc291cmNlPWNvbnZlcnRraXQmdXRtX21lZGl1bT1jb252ZXJ0a2l0JnV0bV9jYW1wYWlnbj01YmY=
), I’ve gone even deeper into the Annieverse. This documentary
had just the right blend of humor, humanity, and insight to help
me with some difficult emotions this week. Description: “Perhaps
best known for her widely celebrated book on writing, Bird by
Bird (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/27u53lgo36fohv7p0oes0/p8heh9hz3kp7g7hq/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9CaXJkLVNvbWUtSW5zdHJ1Y3Rpb25zLVdyaXRpbmctTGlmZS9kcC8wMzg1NDgwMDE2Lw==
), Anne Lamott is one of the rare artists who can teach us not
only how to write, but how to live. From Academy Award-winning
filmmaker Freida Lee Mock (Maya Lin: A Strong Clear Vision), Bird
by Bird with Annie offers an intimate portrait of the writer and
her craft, interweaving the story of Lamott’s life—in itself a
deeply moving tale of addiction and redemption, grief and joy,
intellect and faith—with a year’s worth of interviews, public
lectures, and readings and footage of the writer at work,
focusing particularly on Lamott’s candid, humorous, and
disarmingly straightforward advice on the struggles and joys of
writing. In the end, the author’s genuine reassurance and
guidance concerning the actual process of writing—which has
little resemblance to its glorified image—becomes a stirring call
to action that celebrates the potential of each individual, the
silencing of our inner critics, and the courage to create
something honest, meaningful, and real. Poignant and
inspirational, Bird by Bird with Annie takes us deep into Anne
Lamott’s intoxicatingly brave world, one in which writing is a
means of finding out who we are, how we live, and why we’re
here.” You can find the trailer here (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/27u53lgo36fohv7p0oes0/x0hph6hwr8479nt5/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_dj1EbDJYSmt3dk1yYw==
).
