--------------------------------

“Well-rounded doesn’t cut (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8h5d3nepa2ipx5wkkkh9/qvh8h7hd220nq0bl/aHR0cHM6Ly9zaXZlLnJzL3JvdW5kZWQ=

). Pair with “1,000 True Fans (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8h5d3nepa2ipx5wkkkh9/9qhzhnhd33l9ngu9/aHR0cHM6Ly9ray5vcmcvdGhldGVjaG5pdW0vMTAwMC10cnVlLWZhbnMv

more valuable in the coming AI deluge of machine-created content.

time. Think big first, and you’ll likely get lost, defeated by
AIs instructed to do the same. Think small, and you’ll have a
better chance of enduring… and snowballing into something big.
