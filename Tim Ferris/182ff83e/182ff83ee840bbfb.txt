What I’m using once again for workouts
--------------------------------------

​XPO Trainer (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/38u8kd42kehkho2en5vsz/e0hph7hk3n3q34f8/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYXJtb3JlZGZpdG5lc3MuY29tL3Byb2R1Y3RzL3hwby10cmFpbmVyLTI=
). I love the XPO Trainer (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/38u8kd42kehkho2en5vsz/e0hph7hk3n3q34f8/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYXJtb3JlZGZpdG5lc3MuY29tL3Byb2R1Y3RzL3hwby10cmFpbmVyLTI=
). Recommended to me by elite powerlifter Mark Bell (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/38u8kd42kehkho2en5vsz/7qh7h8h0242w23sz/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuaW5zdGFncmFtLmNvbS9tYXJrc21lbGx5YmVsbC8_aGw9ZW4=
) (check his other gear here (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/38u8kd42kehkho2en5vsz/owhkhqh4m8mgmxtv/aHR0cHM6Ly9tYXJrYmVsbHNsaW5nc2hvdC5jb20v
); our podcast together here (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/38u8kd42kehkho2en5vsz/z2hghnho2d2p2ncp/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDE3LzA3LzEyL2luc2lkZS10aGUtd29ybGQtb2Ytc3VwZXJ0cmFpbmluZy1tYXJrLWJlbGwvP3V0bV9zb3VyY2U9Y29udmVydGtpdCZ1dG1fbWVkaXVtPWNvbnZlcnRraXQmdXRtX2NhbXBhaWduPTViZg==

was initially skeptical, but it’s been one of my favorite torture
tools for years now. The harder you push, the harder it pushes
back, and the wheels allow me to easily use this anywhere.
Perhaps counterintuitively, it is much more difficult to use on
asphalt than soft or loose surfaces, which suits my urban
environment perfectly. No weight plates are required. The XPO is
not made in high volume, and it’s a bit expensive, so here are a
few alternatives (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/38u8kd42kehkho2en5vsz/p8heh9h9w7wgw6fq/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9zP2s9cHJvd2xlcitzbGVkJmxpbmtDb2RlPXNsMiZsaW5rSWQ9ZWYxYWExZmRlM2Y3YzFkODJmYTAxMzMxODhmMmQ4OWUmdGFnPW9mZnNpdG9mdGltZmUtMjAmcmVmPWFzX2xpX3NzX3Rs
) that are most suitable for grass or sand. I use the 1.0 version
of the XPO Trainer, but there are newer models available. Bonus:
If you want to see a walk-through of Mark’s incredible gym,
here’s a video (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/38u8kd42kehkho2en5vsz/x0hph6hnv7v9v0s5/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_dj1pXzdhV25TZjJtVQ==

a push sled, a few kettlebells, a jump rope, and a weighted
rucksack, you can get in exceptional shape quickly.
