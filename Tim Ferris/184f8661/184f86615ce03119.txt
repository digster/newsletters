Quote I’m pondering
-------------------

“Creative people, as I see them, are distinguished by the fact
that they can live with anxiety, even though a high price may be
paid in terms of insecurity, sensitivity, and defenselessness for
the gift of the ‘divine madness,’ to borrow the term used by the
classical Greeks. They do not run away from non-being, but by
encountering and wrestling with it, force it to produce being.
They knock on silence for an answering music; they pursue
meaninglessness until they can force it to mean.”
​— Rollo May (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/75u0qzo2qkh8hkpel7rsn/25h2hoh7q0wv6ea3/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvUm9sbG9fTWF5
