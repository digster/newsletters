Movie I’m watching once again
-----------------------------

​Jodorowsky’s Dune (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8h5wqx8kb2ip6ddokkh9/dpheh0heoo5dgecm/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9ncC92aWRlby9kZXRhaWwvYW16bjEuZHYuZ3RpLmY0YTlmNzM1LWU4ZWUtZWIzMi05Njc2LTAyMTY1NTg5Yjg5Nj9hdXRvcGxheT0wJnJlZl89YXR2X2NmX3N0cmdfd2I=
). Sometimes, you can learn a lot from failures, which is why I
(and Nassim Taleb (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8h5wqx8kb2ip6ddokkh9/e0hph7h7ll4mgqi8/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDIzLzA5LzA3L25hc3NpbS1uaWNob2xhcy10YWxlYi1zY290dC1wYXR0ZXJzb24vP3V0bV9jYW1wYWlnbj01YmYmdXRtX21lZGl1bT1jb252ZXJ0a2l0JnV0bV9zb3VyY2U9Y29udmVydGtpdCNjb250ZW50
)) like the book What I Learned Losing a Million Dollars (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8h5wqx8kb2ip6ddokkh9/7qh7h8h966zd7zbz/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYXVkaWJsZS5jb20vcGQvV2hhdC1JLUxlYXJuZWQtTG9zaW5nLWEtTWlsbGlvbi1Eb2xsYXJzLUF1ZGlvYm9vay9CMDBOQzhYVlZD

spectacularly bold failure. My favorite parts are the highlights
of lessons learned, as well as the stories of how team members
later helped create spectacular successes (e.g., the Alien in
Alien (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8h5wqx8kb2ip6ddokkh9/owhkhqhw66oxv5fv/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvQWxpZW5fKGZpbG0p

General Magic startup story, which you can learn about here (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8h5wqx8kb2ip6ddokkh9/z2hghnhekkw5v5hp/aHR0cHM6Ly95b3V0dS5iZS81TTVTSjV1MEtlND90PTUwMw==
).
