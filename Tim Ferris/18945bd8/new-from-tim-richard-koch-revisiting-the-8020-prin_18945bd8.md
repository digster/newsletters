He has worked for Boston Consulting Group and was a partner at Bain & Co. before joining Jim Lawrence and Iain Evans to start LEK, which expanded from three to 350 professionals during the six years Richard was there.
In 1997, Richard’s book [The 80/20 Principle](https://click.fourhourmail.com/k0u3wp9vw7b6hdn0xzohxi7/n2hohvh3gr8lppi6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS84MC0yMC1QcmluY2lwbGUtU2VjcmV0LUFjaGlldmluZy9kcC8wMzg1NDkxNzQzLw==) reinterpreted the Pareto Rule—which states that most results come from a small minority of causes—and extended it beyond its well-known application in business into personal life, happiness, and success. The book, rewritten in 2022, has sold more than a million copies, been translated into roughly 40 languages, and has become a business classic. It was named by GQ as one of the top 25 business books of all time. Richard’s latest book is [Unreasonable Success and How to Achieve It](https://click.fourhourmail.com/k0u3wp9vw7b6hdn0xzohxi7/48hvhehr9g57edfx/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9VbnJlYXNvbmFibGUtU3VjY2Vzcy1Ib3ctQWNoaWV2ZS1VbmxvY2tpbmcvZHAvMTY0MjAxMTM2My8=).
He has two upcoming books: 80/20 Beliefs, which identifies the very few beliefs in our lives that strongly influence what we do, and, therefore, the results we get, and 80/20 Daily, a collection of 365 short daily readings using the 80/20 philosophy to achieve the good life.
