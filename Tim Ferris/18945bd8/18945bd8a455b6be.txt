well-known application in business into personal life, happiness,
and success. The book, rewritten in 2022, has sold more than a
million copies, been translated into roughly 40 languages, and
has become a business classic. It was named by GQ as one of the
top 25 business books of all time. Richard’s latest book is
Unreasonable Success and How to Achieve It (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/k0u3wp9vw7b6hdn0xzohxi7/48hvhehr9g57edfx/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9VbnJlYXNvbmFibGUtU3VjY2Vzcy1Ib3ctQWNoaWV2ZS1VbmxvY2tpbmcvZHAvMTY0MjAxMTM2My8=
).

He has two upcoming books: 80/20 Beliefs, which identifies the
very few beliefs in our lives that strongly influence what we do,
and, therefore, the results we get, and 80/20 Daily, a collection
of 365 short daily readings using the 80/20 philosophy to achieve
the good life.
