Medical headset I’m investigating closely
-----------------------------------------

​Cognito Therapeutics (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/0vum69236ps9h9mq0qma0ivmerk55fnhpq8/qvh8h7hd67me62cl/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuY29nbml0b3R4LmNvbS9hYm91dA==
). The team at Cognito is developing a novel, non-invasive,
wearable medical device aimed at slowing the progression of
Alzheimer’s disease by using light and sound stimulation to
restore brain-wave activity. They are focusing specifically on
gamma waves, which are critical for memory, learning, and healthy
brain function. In Alzheimer’s patients, gamma waves are altered,
indicating impairment in normal brain function. Related:
“Evidence that gamma rhythm stimulation can treat neurological
disorders is emerging (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/0vum69236ps9h9mq0qma0ivmerk55fnhpq8/g3hnh5hmzd8gzkbr/aHR0cHM6Ly9uZXdzLm1pdC5lZHUvMjAyNC9ldmlkZW5jZS1nYW1tYS1yaHl0aG0tc3RpbXVsYXRpb24tY2FuLXRyZWF0LW5ldXJvbG9naWNhbC1kaXNvcmRlcnMtZW1lcmdpbmctMDExOA==
