He has written of his own journey in his memoir [One Blade of Grass: Finding the Old Road of the Heart, a Zen Memoir](https://click.fourhourmail.com/v8unvm27v9srh62zdviz/m2h7h5hoezlg5wsm/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9PbmUtQmxhZGUtR3Jhc3MtRmluZGluZy1NZW1vaXIvZHAvMTY0MDA5MjYyNS8=).
