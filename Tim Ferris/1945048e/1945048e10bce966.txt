Long-standing tech problem I recently solved
--------------------------------------------

​Texting Android friends from iMessage (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/8ku6kplgkosoh2e7m9vc5ikw73g99u3/8ghqhohl8x004vik/aHR0cHM6Ly9zdXBwb3J0LmFwcGxlLmNvbS9lbi11cy8xMDI1NDU=
)! I prefer using my laptop for texting during work hours, as
it’s much faster. Sadly, it’s impossible to iMessage my friends
and family who are on Android… or so I thought. For years, my
workaround has been using the WhatsApp desktop app, but my friend
Mike taught me an easier way.
​
In simplest terms, here’s what you do:​
​
1. On your iPhone, go to Settings > Apps > Messages.
​2. Tap Text Message Forwarding. (If you don’t see it, turn
iMessage off and back on, then tap Send & Receive and sign in
again.)
​3. Tap to turn on each device that you want to allow to send and
receive messages from this iPhone.​
​
​It’s unintuitive. You need to turn on this additional feature
that forwards SMS messages from your MacBook to your iPhone and
then to the Android recipient. But it works like a charm. Even
Google and ChatGPT insisted I would need to use third-party apps,
but it’s not true. The above does the trick.
​
For more details and troubleshooting, click here (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/8ku6kplgkosoh2e7m9vc5ikw73g99u3/8ghqhohl8x004vik/aHR0cHM6Ly9zdXBwb3J0LmFwcGxlLmNvbS9lbi11cy8xMDI1NDU=
) to read the full instructions. Enjoy!
