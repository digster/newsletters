[Azolla event](https://click.fourhourmail.com/d0uv8kxd86s0hxm905b9/7qh7h8h03v0wkduz/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvQXpvbGxhX2V2ZW50). From Wikipedia: “The Azolla event is a scenario hypothesized to have occurred in the middle Eocene epoch, around 49 million years ago, when blooms of the [freshwater fern Azolla](https://click.fourhourmail.com/d0uv8kxd86s0hxm905b9/owhkhqh4534gkptv/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvQXpvbGxh) are thought to have happened in the Arctic Ocean. As they sank to the stagnant sea floor, they were incorporated into the sediment; the resulting draw-down of carbon dioxide has been speculated to have helped transform the planet from a ‘greenhouse Earth’ state, hot enough for turtles and palm trees to prosper at the poles, to the current icehouse Earth known as the Late Cenozoic Ice Age.”
