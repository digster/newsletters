Phrase I’m enjoying
-------------------

​Festina lente (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/92u58xp28qfnhq8g4p8i5iz3vmd33aw/g3hnh5h380wd93br/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi5tLndpa2lwZWRpYS5vcmcvd2lraS9GZXN0aW5hX2xlbnRl
). Festina lente or speûde bradéōs (σπεῦδε βραδέως) is a
classical adage and oxymoron meaning “make haste slowly.” It has
been adopted as a motto numerous times, particularly by the
emperors Augustus and Titus, then later by the Medicis and the
Onslows. (Wikipedia (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/92u58xp28qfnhq8g4p8i5iz3vmd33aw/g3hnh5h380wd93br/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi5tLndpa2lwZWRpYS5vcmcvd2lraS9GZXN0aW5hX2xlbnRl
