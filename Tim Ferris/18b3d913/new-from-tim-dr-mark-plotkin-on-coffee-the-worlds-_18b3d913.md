So, who is Dr. Mark Plotkin? Mark ([@DocMarkPlotkin](https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4udw372wpivh8e5dr0s5i7ng85gg/8ghqhohl5900lgak/aHR0cHM6Ly90d2l0dGVyLmNvbS9Eb2NNYXJrUGxvdGtpbg==)) is an ethnobotanist who serves as president of the [Amazon Conservation Team](https://click.fourhourmail.com/n4udw372wpivh8e5dr0s5i7ng85gg/vqh3hrhpkgxxppag/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9udGVhbS5vcmcv), which has partnered with ~80 tribes to map and improve management and protection of ~100 million acres of ancestral rainforests.
