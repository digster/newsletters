Invention that’s long overdue
[Braun All-in-One Style Kit](https://click.fourhourmail.com/4zu50ed20nfeh5eq8x9fmi3d0ql77/wnh2hghw8lgdl9i7/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9kcC9CMEJOTFZMNzk2Lw==). Every once in a while, innovations come around that solve problems with a “Why didn’t someone think of this earlier?” effect. Luggage on wheels is one example. For me, this Braun trimmer is another. I have TONS of clippers. Why? Because I lose the plastic attachments for different lengths, then I’m screwed and need a new set. This superior Braun design is an all-in-one solution that uses a radial dial allowing 40 length settings in 0.02″ steps. I love it.
