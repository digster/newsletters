What I’m reading
----------------

​Guidelines for Ketamine Use (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/v8unvm27v9srhvoo6pli6fv2edlllf9h6x0/x0hph6hew3m62xf5/aHR0cHM6Ly9rZXRhbWluZXJlc2VhcmNoZm91bmRhdGlvbi5vcmcvZ3VpZGVsaW5lcy8=
). While ketamine holds incredible potential for treating certain
conditions (e.g., acute suicidality, depression, chronic pain),
I’ve also seen lives unravel because of its addictive properties,
and it’s become a Faustian alcohol replacement for many. This PDF
is one of the best overviews of ketamine-assisted therapies I’ve
found, primarily because it also addresses mitigating the risks
of addiction and long-term health consequences from excessive,
chronic use. There is a baby in the bath water, but you need to
know how to separate them. I found this to be a valuable
resource.
