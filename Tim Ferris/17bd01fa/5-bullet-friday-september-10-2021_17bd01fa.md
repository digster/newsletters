I’ve had dozens of friends recommend this over the years, and I finally watched it last night. It’s SPECTACULAR. It was exactly the feel-good pick-me-up that I needed. The film’s accolades include [95% on Rotten Tomatoes](https://click.fourhourmail.com/p9uwl9zvl6f9hnnk7msp/6qheh8hp7r338gio/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cucm90dGVudG9tYXRvZXMuY29tL20vc2VhcmNoaW5nX2Zvcl9zdWdhcl9tYW4=), a BAFTA Award for Best Documentary, and an Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature, just to mention a few. Here’s the official description: “Searching for Sugar Man tells the incredible true story of [Rodriguez](https://click.fourhourmail.com/p9uwl9zvl6f9hnnk7msp/kkhmh6h8lwoo00hl/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvU2l4dG9fUm9kcmlndWV6), the greatest ’70s rock icon who never was. After being discovered in a Detroit bar, Rodriguez’s sound struck two renowned producers, and they signed a recording deal. But when the album bombed, the singer disappeared into obscurity. A bootleg recording found its way into apartheid South Africa, and over the next two decades, he became a phenomenon. The film follows the story of two South African fans who set out to find out what really happened to their hero.” Watch the trailer [here](https://click.fourhourmail.com/p9uwl9zvl6f9hnnk7msp/58hvh7h5mx44dru6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_dj1RTDVUZmZkT1E3Zw==).

Comedian I’m watching — “[Why I speak French](https://click.fourhourmail.com/p9uwl9zvl6f9hnnk7msp/3ohphkhq7mkke6sr/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuaW5zdGFncmFtLmNvbS9wL0NIdnFuU25BWlRvLw==)” by [TJ](https://click.fourhourmail.com/p9uwl9zvl6f9hnnk7msp/n2hohvhn3qllz8i6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuaW5zdGFncmFtLmNvbS90anN0YW5kdXAv). I first saw Tanael “TJ” Joachim at the [Gotham Comedy Club](https://click.fourhourmail.com/p9uwl9zvl6f9hnnk7msp/48hvheh0rl774pbx/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuZ290aGFtY29tZWR5Y2x1Yi5jb20v) ([@gothamcomedy](https://click.fourhourmail.com/p9uwl9zvl6f9hnnk7msp/wnh2hghrw7lle4h7/aHR0cHM6Ly90d2l0dGVyLmNvbS9nb3RoYW1jb21lZHk=)) in New York City. He calmly captured the room, and while I was laughing, I realized how great he was at tackling “off-limits” topics. His comedy was really smart. People got very uncomfortable, and I loved every minute of it. His performance at Gotham a few weeks ago was tighter than most of his older clips online, like the sample above. If he continues to refine and has the right management, I think he could be big.
What I’m reading — “[The Collapse of Wild Red Wolves Is a Warning That Should Worry Us All](https://click.fourhourmail.com/p9uwl9zvl6f9hnnk7msp/reh8hohq0doogvu2/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlbmF0aW9uLmNvbS9hcnRpY2xlL2Vudmlyb25tZW50L3JlZC13b2xmLWV4dGluY3Rpb24v)” by [Jimmy Tobias](https://click.fourhourmail.com/p9uwl9zvl6f9hnnk7msp/08hwh9hmdepp3kil/aHR0cHM6Ly90d2l0dGVyLmNvbS9KYW1lc0NUb2JpYXM=) (The Nation). Thanks to [Ryan Holiday](https://click.fourhourmail.com/p9uwl9zvl6f9hnnk7msp/8ghqhohgld77m5sk/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDIwLzA0LzA5L3J5YW4taG9saWRheS1zdG9pY2lzbS8_dXRtX3NvdXJjZT1jb252ZXJ0a2l0JnV0bV9tZWRpdW09Y29udmVydGtpdCZ1dG1fY2FtcGFpZ249NWJm) ([@RyanHoliday](https://click.fourhourmail.com/p9uwl9zvl6f9hnnk7msp/vqh3hrhnp0qqznbg/aHR0cHM6Ly90d2l0dGVyLmNvbS9yeWFuaG9saWRheQ==)) for the recommendation. This is a compelling story of a canary in the coal mine: wild red wolves. Even if you’re a hunter, as I am, it’s a critical preview of what’s coming for many other species if we don’t make important changes soon. It’s also a complete case study on many fronts.
