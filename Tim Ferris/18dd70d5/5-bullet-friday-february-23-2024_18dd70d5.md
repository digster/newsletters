What I’m reading
[String Theory: David Foster Wallace on Tennis](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hr20ew8f6i030dzll/9qhzhnhpz5ed2eb9/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9TdHJpbmctVGhlb3J5LVdhbGxhY2UtTGlicmFyeS1QdWJsaWNhdGlvbi9kcC8xNTk4NTM0ODA3Lw==). “Federer Both Flesh and Not” is considered one of Wallace’s best essays, and it doesn’t disappoint. The essay was first published in The New York Times as “[Federer as Religious Experience](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hr20ew8f6i030dzll/3ohphkh74pk3d8tr/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubnl0aW1lcy5jb20vMjAwNi8wOC8yMC9zcG9ydHMvcGxheW1hZ2F6aW5lLzIwZmVkZXJlci5odG1s)” in 2006. Here are two excerpts to give you a taste: There are three kinds of valid explanation for Federer’s ascendancy. One kind involves mystery and metaphysics and is, I think, closest to the real truth. The others are more technical and make for better journalism. The metaphysical explanation is that Roger Federer is one of those rare, preternatural athletes who appear to be exempt, at least in part, from certain physical laws. Good analogs here include Michael Jordan, who could not only jump inhumanly high but actually hang there a beat or two longer than gravity allows, and Muhammad Ali, who really could “float” across the canvas and land two or three jabs in the clock-time required for one. There are probably a half-dozen other examples since 1960. And Roger Federer is of this type—a type that one could call genius, or mutant, or avatar. [...] In 2006, though, it’s fair to ask why this kind of talent still matters so much. Recall what is true about dogma and Wimbledon’s sign. Kinesthetic virtuoso or no, Roger Federer is now dominating the largest, strongest, fittest, best-trained and -coached field of male pros who’ve ever existed, with everyone using a kind of nuclear racket that’s said to have made the finer calibrations of kinesthetic sense irrelevant, like trying to whistle Mozart during a Metallica concert. Thanks to [David Perell](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hr20ew8f6i030dzll/n2hohvh367lvodc6/aHR0cHM6Ly9wZXJlbGwuY29tL2Vzc2F5cy8=) for the recommendation!
