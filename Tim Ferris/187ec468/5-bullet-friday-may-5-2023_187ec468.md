[Acumobility Massage Ball](https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h7r0xvpuq/z2hghnh346dz5qap/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9kcC9CMDlOQ0hWREs1L3JlZj10c21fMV90cF90Yw==). Yes, this model looks like a pink nipple. The basics: I’m lying on the ground on my back and placing the nub where I am sorest on my “QL” ([quadratus lumborum](https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h7r0xvpuq/p8heh9hz367erqiq/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvUXVhZHJhdHVzX2x1bWJvcnVtX211c2NsZQ==)). Starting with my right side, I would then bring my right knee towards my chest, followed by extending the same leg straight and back to resting position. Repeat this motion, almost like a single-leg high march from the back, for 10 repetitions. Then switch sides. This is all easiest to follow by watching [this short video](https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h7r0xvpuq/x0hph6hwr27mk4t5/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_dj1zaUl5T195TTFlSQ==). Personally, I’m placing the nub just above the crest of my hip, but you’ll find your most sensitive spot. From [this page](https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h7r0xvpuq/kkhmh6hldw9pz7il/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cua2VuaHViLmNvbS9lbi9saWJyYXJ5L2FuYXRvbXkvcXVhZHJhdHVzLWx1bWJvcnVtLW11c2NsZQ==): “In order to palpate the muscle, one needs to place the fingers above the posterior iliac crest at the level of the hip.” Special thanks to [Eric Cressey](https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h7r0xvpuq/25h2hoh2v9ox6vi3/aHR0cHM6Ly9lcmljY3Jlc3NleS5jb20v) for the recommendation and for making the above short video for you fine subscribers.
