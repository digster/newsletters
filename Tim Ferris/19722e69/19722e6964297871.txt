Article I’m reading

“Dick Garwin Fought Nuclear Armageddon. He Hid a 50-Year Secret.
(
https://click.fourhourmail.com/27u53lgo36foh8e9lges0ig63w344hg/l2hehmhl4d8nmpt6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubnl0aW1lcy5jb20vMjAyNS8wNS8xOS9zY2llbmNlL3JpY2hhcmQtZ2Fyd2luLWh5ZHJvZ2VuLWJvbWIuaHRtbA==
)” by William J. Broad (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/27u53lgo36foh8e9lges0ig63w344hg/m2h7h5h39ldq6rcm/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvV2lsbGlhbV9Ccm9hZA==
) (The New York Times). I deeply enjoyed this riveting profile of
an incredible man, arguably the most influential scientist you’ve
never heard of. From the piece: “Richard Garwin’s role in
designing the hydrogen bomb was obscured from the public, even
his family, as he advised presidents and devoted his life to
undoing the danger he created.” Click here (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/27u53lgo36foh8e9lges0ig63w344hg/dpheh0hek2lolzam/aHR0cHM6Ly9hcmNoaXZlLmlzLzE2aTBZ
) if you have any issues reading through the above link.
​
Here’s one excerpt that pulled me in: “Dr. Garwin never
officially joined any president’s cabinet, as [Henry] Kissinger
had. But in our last interview, the physicist looked over a list
of presidents and one by one identified the commanders-in-chief
he had counseled. There were 13.”

Quote I’m pondering
-------------------

“You’re never so centered on yourself as when you’re depressed.
You’re never so ready to forget yourself as when you are happy.
Happiness releases you from self. It is suffering and pain and
misery and depression that tie you to the self. Look how
conscious you are of your tooth when you have a toothache. When
you don’t have a toothache, you’re not even aware you have a
tooth, or that you have a head, for that matter, when you don’t
have a headache.”
​— Awareness (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/27u53lgo36foh8e9lges0ig63w344hg/e0hph7h7ed8l8pu8/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9Bd2FyZW5lc3MtT3Bwb3J0dW5pdGllcy1SZWFsaXR5LUFudGhvbnktTWVsbG8vZHAvMDM4NTI0OTM3My8=
