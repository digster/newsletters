Phrase I’m enjoying
-------------------

​Solvitur ambulando (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hrmgdrdu6i29erlllsn/reh8hoh0gze4dzu2/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvU29sdml0dXJfYW1idWxhbmRv
). From Wikipedia: “Solvitur ambulando is a Latin phrase which
means ‘it is solved by walking,’ referring to an anecdotal,
practical solution to a seemingly complex philosophical problem.
It is often attributed to Saint Augustine in a refutation of
Zeno’s paradoxes of motion.”
