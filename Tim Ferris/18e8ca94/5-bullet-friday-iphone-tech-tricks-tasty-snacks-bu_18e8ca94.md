What I’m drinking
[Brodo Bone Broth](https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h7pw5q6sqinpxowkk/owhkhqhr5np60oiv/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYnJvZG8uY29tL3Byb2R1Y3Qvc2FtcGxlci1ib3gv). Brodo was founded by [Chef Marco Canora](https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h7pw5q6sqinpxowkk/z2hghnh307nkm0fp/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDIxLzExLzEwL21hcmNvLWNhbm9yYS8_dXRtX3NvdXJjZT1jb252ZXJ0a2l0JnV0bV9tZWRpdW09Y29udmVydGtpdCZ1dG1fY2FtcGFpZ249NWJmI2NvbnRlbnQ=), who many will recognize from his extensive appearances in [The 4-Hour Chef](https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h7pw5q6sqinpxowkk/p8heh9hz8dm2praq/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS80LUhvdXItQ2hlZi1Db29raW5nLUxlYXJuaW5nLUFueXRoaW5nL2RwLzA1NDc4ODQ1OTE=) as one of my main teachers. Brodo has quickly become the most famous broth in NYC. The challenge for Marco has always been creating versions of his broths that (A) were shelf-stable, (B) met his high standards, and (C) didn’t require refrigeration… which he finally nailed. My favorite flavors so far are his Tom Yum and Tuscan Sun, which are included in their [sampler box](https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h7pw5q6sqinpxowkk/owhkhqhr5np60oiv/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYnJvZG8uY29tL3Byb2R1Y3Qvc2FtcGxlci1ib3gv). Though I haven’t tried it yet, Marco offered that you can use a milk frother to buzz extra-virgin olive oil into Tuscan Sun and butter into Deeply Rooted to add calories and mouthfeel. For the Tom Yum, skip the added fat but squeeze in a dash of lime juice. For a little boost and nutritional insurance, I’ve really been enjoying one packet each morning. Confession: I do use the microwave instead of stove top. Marco is the real deal, and he has the accolades to prove it: He won the James Beard Award (the equivalent of the Oscars in the cooking world) for Best Chef: New York City, and his first cookbook, [Salt to Taste: The Keys to Confident, Delicious Cooking](https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h7pw5q6sqinpxowkk/x0hph6hwxzop4ou5/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9TYWx0LVRhc3RlLUNvbmZpZGVudC1EZWxpY2lvdXMtQ29va2luZy9kcC8xNTk0ODY3ODAxLw==) was nominated for a James Beard Award. For my friends near Manhattan, I recommend visiting Marco’s restaurant, [Hearth](https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h7pw5q6sqinpxowkk/6qheh8h70wz53rco/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cucmVzdGF1cmFudGhlYXJ0aC5jb20v).
