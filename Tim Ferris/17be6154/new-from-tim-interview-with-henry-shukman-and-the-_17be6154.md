📢 What I’m celebrating —
[Today is the launch of my collaboration with author Michael Pollan: The Ferriss – UC Berkeley Psychedelic Journalism Fellowship](https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8hm9mpqi2/08hwh9hmd6gd53hl/aHR0cHM6Ly9qb3VybmFsaXNtLmJlcmtlbGV5LmVkdS90aW0tZmVycmlzcy1naWZ0Lw==).
I couldn’t be more excited to support the [Ferriss – UC Berkeley Journalism Fellowship](https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8hm9mpqi2/e0hph7hk03q03ph8/aHR0cHM6Ly9qb3VybmFsaXNtLmJlcmtlbGV5LmVkdS9iY3NwZmVsbG93cy8=). If you want to bend the arc of history, longform and investigative journalism is one of the best contemporary tools that we have. The Cambrian explosion of psychedelic medicine brings with it both incredible promise and incredible complexity. Dedicated journalists are needed to help separate fact from fiction, hold people accountable, and much more. [Michael Pollan](https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8hm9mpqi2/7qh7h8h0o2wo26bz/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDIxLzA2LzI4L21pY2hhZWwtcG9sbGFuLXRoaXMtaXMteW91ci1taW5kLW9uLXBsYW50cy8_dXRtX3NvdXJjZT1jb252ZXJ0a2l0JnV0bV9tZWRpdW09Y29udmVydGtpdCZ1dG1fY2FtcGFpZ249d2Vla2x5LXJvdW5kdXAtaGVucnktc2h1a21hbg==) ([@michaelpollan](https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8hm9mpqi2/owhkhqh4rmgrmnuv/aHR0cHM6Ly90d2l0dGVyLmNvbS9taWNoYWVscG9sbGFu)) is the perfect person to spearhead this ambitious fellowship, and I am very grateful to be involved.
Mark your calendars, the [application form to apply](https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8hm9mpqi2/e0hph7hk03q03ph8/aHR0cHM6Ly9qb3VybmFsaXNtLmJlcmtlbGV5LmVkdS9iY3NwZmVsbG93cy8=) will go live on December 1st.
Here is the official description: “The Ferriss – UC Berkeley Psychedelic Journalism Fellowship offers journalists reporting grants ranging from $5,000 to $15,000 to report in-depth print and audio stories on the science, policy, business, and culture of this new era of psychedelics. In addition to underwriting individual stories, the Fellowship aims to establish and nurture a new generation of journalists covering the frontlines of this rapidly changing field. We’re looking for big, underreported, narratively compelling stories placed in rich political, economic, scientific, and cultural contexts. We are committed to supporting journalists from diverse backgrounds and of all nationalities.”
