What I’m wearing for long hikes
-------------------------------

​L’eggs Women’s Everyday Knee Highs (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8hkw9535c2/z2hghnhoqxv0wmsp/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9MZWdncy1Xb21lbnMtRXZlcnlkYXktU2hlZXItTmF0dXJhbC9kcC9CMDdTUkZDTjhTLw==
). OK, OK, hear me out. An old Italian fella, veteran of the
Camino de Santiago, shared this gem with a close friend of mine:
Skip fancy sock liners and just put these on before your hiking
socks. Et voilà! No blisters. So far, it’s worked like a charm.

Book I’m revisiting
-------------------

​Travels with Charley: In Search of America (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8hkw9535c2/p8heh9h9no08xeuq/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9UcmF2ZWxzLUNoYXJsZXktU2VhcmNoLUFtZXJpY2EtU3RlaW5iZWNrL2RwLzAxNDAwNTMyMDQ=
) by John Steinbeck (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8hkw9535c2/x0hph6hn506x3gs5/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvSm9obl9TdGVpbmJlY2s=
). This was originally given to me by my dear mom, who knows I’ve
fantasized for ages about a countrywide road trip with my pooch,
Molly (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8hkw9535c2/dpheh0hqlngrrzum/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuaW5zdGFncmFtLmNvbS9wL0NnbXZvbGNBNWFyLw==
). The book was published in 1962, the same year Steinbeck won
the Nobel Prize in Literature for his work. It’s beautiful and
surprisingly hilarious at points. Here is a brief description:
“With Charley, his French poodle, Steinbeck drives the
interstates and the country roads, dines with truckers,
encounters bears at Yellowstone and old friends in San Francisco.
Along the way, he reflects on the American character, racial
hostility, the particular form of American loneliness he finds
almost everywhere, and the unexpected kindness of strangers.”
