Project I’m enjoying

​Movie Iris: Visualizing movie frames as art (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/4zu50ed20nfehpz8om2fmi620lm77i5/l2hehmh3dpm8p0a6/aHR0cHM6Ly9naXRodWIuY29tL0xvU2luQ29zL21vdmllLWlyaXM=
). From the creator, data scientist Lotan Weininger (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/4zu50ed20nfehpz8om2fmi620lm77i5/m2h7h5h8lx6dxqbm/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubGlua2VkaW4uY29tL2luL2xvdGFuLXdlaW5pbmdlci8=
): “[This is] effectively taking raw movie frames, compressing
them to their core colors, and visualizing the color schemes of a
movie. I wanted to see how movies evolve, as well as how
directors intended for different colors to be prevalent across
different scenes.” Examples include The Matrix, Up, and Hero.
