Book I devoured in one sitting
------------------------------

​Tomorrow, and Tomorrow, and Tomorrow (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/8ku6kplgkosoh25lwkgt5ikl8x999c3hp9k/reh8hohmvz4k6ws2/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9Ub21vcnJvdy1ub3ZlbC1HYWJyaWVsbGUtWmV2aW4vZHAvMDU5MzQ2NjQ5Ny8=
) by Gabrielle Zevin (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/8ku6kplgkosoh25lwkgt5ikl8x999c3hp9k/08hwh9h2wrvnlzal/aHR0cHM6Ly9nYWJyaWVsbGV6ZXZpbi5jb20v
). I could not stop reading this book. It’s a page-turner in the
most addictive sense and was not only a New York Times
bestseller, but it also placed #76 on the NYT’s list of The 100
Best Books of the 21st Century.
​
The less you know about this book before you read it, the better,
in my opinion, but if you must get a tease, here’s a snippet of
the description: “Sam and Sadie—two college friends, often in
love, but never lovers—become creative partners in a dazzling and
intricately imagined world of video game design, where success
brings them fame, joy, tragedy, duplicity, and, ultimately, a
kind of immortality. It is a love story, but not one you have
read before.”

Newest workout staple that I’m loving
-------------------------------------

​TANK M3 Push Sled (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/8ku6kplgkosoh25lwkgt5ikl8x999c3hp9k/8ghqhohopnzwrkfk/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudG9ycXVlZml0bmVzcy5jb20vcHJvZHVjdHMvdGFuay1tMw==
). This is, bar none, the best sled I have ever used for pushing
or pulling. 10–15-minute workouts in the morning sun have become
my favorite way to set the stage for a great, mood-elevated day.
The ease of rotation and traction makes it perfect for even a
gravel driveway like mine. From the description: “Designed with
patented Mag-Force™ Resistance Technology, the TANK M3 delivers
scalable intensity, accommodating many fitness levels while
minimizing the need for weight plates and clunky user swaps. The
TANK M3’s three-wheeled design glides effortlessly across turf,
rubber, or concrete, ensuring smooth operation on any surface.
Ergonomic multi-position handles allow users to switch grips
seamlessly, enhancing comfort and workout variety. Real-time
performance monitoring helps users track progress and training
metrics with an oversized digital screen to empower better
workouts.”
