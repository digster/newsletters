Tech workaround that I’ll use again
-----------------------------------

​Airalo (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/o8u50wr80xfqhkww8e0ig/g3hnh5h3lvzorxsr/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYWlyYWxvLmNvbS8=
). What to do for cellular data when traveling overseas? For a
while, my answer was the app GigSky (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/o8u50wr80xfqhkww8e0ig/9qhzhnhp672304u9/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuZ2lnc2t5LmNvbS8=
), but on a recent trip abroad, I had all sorts of problems, so I
posted a plea for help on social media. Special thanks to Noah
Kagan (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/o8u50wr80xfqhkww8e0ig/n2hohvh3xdo5mqh6/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDExLzA5LzI0L2hvdy10by1jcmVhdGUtYS1taWxsaW9uLWRvbGxhci1idXNpbmVzcy10aGlzLXdlZWtlbmQtZXhhbXBsZXMtYXBwc3Vtby1taW50LWNoaWh1YWh1YXMvP3V0bV9zb3VyY2U9Y29udmVydGtpdCZ1dG1fbWVkaXVtPWNvbnZlcnRraXQmdXRtX2NhbXBhaWduPTViZg==
) (@noahkagan (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/o8u50wr80xfqhkww8e0ig/48hvhehrpo2qv8ux/aHR0cHM6Ly90d2l0dGVyLmNvbS9ub2Foa2FnYW4=
)) and everyone else who responded to my query in this Twitter
thread (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/o8u50wr80xfqhkww8e0ig/reh8hoh0385v4ls2/aHR0cHM6Ly90d2l0dGVyLmNvbS90ZmVycmlzcy9zdGF0dXMvMTY2NjQ5NTQ5MzA5NzAyMTQ5NA==
). Airalo was extremely easy to use and did the trick. To avoid
expensive roaming bills when traveling abroad and using data, you
can install the Airalo app (iOS (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/o8u50wr80xfqhkww8e0ig/08hwh9hd987wv3tl/aHR0cHM6Ly9hcHBzLmFwcGxlLmNvbS9hcHAvYWlyYWxvLWVzaW0tc3RvcmUvaWQxNDc1OTExNzIw
), Android (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/o8u50wr80xfqhkww8e0ig/vqh3hrhpv5k3lqhg/aHR0cHM6Ly9wbGF5Lmdvb2dsZS5jb20vc3RvcmUvYXBwcy9kZXRhaWxzP2lkPWNvbS5tb2JpbGxpdW0uYWlyYWxvJnBsaT0x
)) and buy a local eSIM. In minutes, you will have data at an
affordable price. The app might be easiest, but I personally did
something slightly different: I visited their website (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/o8u50wr80xfqhkww8e0ig/g3hnh5h3lvzorxsr/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYWlyYWxvLmNvbS8=
) on my laptop and then took a pic of the QR code with my phone.
Here’s the official description: “Airalo is the world’s first
eSIM store that solves the pain of high roaming bills by giving
you access to 200+ eSIMs (digital SIM cards) globally at
affordable prices.”
