What I’m using for better sleep

​Nayoya Acupressure Mat (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/wvumzkq3z7sgh5x0eeguoieweekxx/p8heh9hz5nx2vlbq/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9OYXlveWEtQmFjay1OZWNrLVBhaW4tUmVsaWVmL2RwL0IwMDQ5UTBQOU0v

Bondarenko (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/wvumzkq3z7sgh5x0eeguoieweekxx/x0hph6hwd53pg7b5/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuaW5zdGFncmFtLmNvbS9hbmRyaWlfYm9uZGFyZW5rby8_aGw9ZW4=

tightness and discomfort throughout the night, and the neck-roll
portion is much easier to pack for travel.

I love macadamia nuts and have been enjoying them often since
keto expert Dr. Dominic D’Agostino recommended them on the
podcast in 2015. More recently, Andrew Huberman has mentioned
macadamias as a snack of choice in GQ. They taste great, and with
more monounsaturated fat (prevalent in the Mediterranean diet)
than both olive oil and avocados, 27% fewer grams of carbs than
almonds, and more than 50% fewer grams of carbs than cashews,
they’re the perfect low-carb, keto-friendly, nutty snack. They’re
also rich in rare omega-7s, which promote the natural production
of collagen, an essential protein.
