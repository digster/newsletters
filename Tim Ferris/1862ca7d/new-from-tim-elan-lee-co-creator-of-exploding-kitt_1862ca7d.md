Prior to that, he was the founder and chief creative officer of Fourth Wall Studios and co-founder of 42 Entertainment. He began his career at Microsoft Games Studios as a lead designer on the original Xbox.
Lee has won a Primetime Emmy for the series [Dirty Work](https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8hk26mnlb2/l2hehmhog5wxn0a6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuaW1kYi5jb20vdGl0bGUvdHQyMzMxNjEzLw==); Game Innovator of the Year for Exploding Kittens; a Peabody Award for the world’s first alternate reality game, [The Beast](https://click.fourhourmail.com/68ug9o7692b8hk26mnlb2/m2h7h5horkw4qqbm/aHR0cHM6Ly9wZWFib2R5YXdhcmRzLmNvbS90aGViZWFzdC8=); and an IndieCade Trailblazer Award for a distinguished career in interactive entertainment, among others.
