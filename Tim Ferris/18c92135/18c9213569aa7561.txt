Book I’m savoring
-----------------

​Masterpieces of Fantasy Art (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/r8ug29rn25boh2494v8u0ix2pw666/9qhzhnhppw28xzf9/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9NYXN0ZXJwaWVjZXMtRmFudGFzeS1BcnQtRGlhbi1IYW5zb24vZHAvMzgzNjU5MzYyOQ==
) by Dian Hanson (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/r8ug29rn25boh2494v8u0ix2pw666/3ohphkh77md8rzsr/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvRGlhbl9IYW5zb24=
). This is a sumptuous dive back into the most gorgeous art I met
through Heavy Metal magazine, fantasy nerdom, and everything in
between. I think of it as a buffet of creative inspiration, as
the styles vary widely. From the official description: “Fantasy
art, that colorful blend of myth, muscle and sexy maidens, took
off in 1923 with the launch of Weird Tales magazine, was
reinvigorated in the 1960s with The Lord of the Rings, Conan the
Barbarian paperbacks with Frank Frazetta covers, and the late
’60s emergence of fantasy psychedelia. This book features
original paintings, contextualized by preparatory sketches,
sculptures, calendars, magazines, and paperback books, for an
immersive dive into this dynamic, fanciful genre.”

---------------------------------------------------------

​Tate’s Bake Shop Ginger Zinger Cookies (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/r8ug29rn25boh2494v8u0ix2pw666/n2hohvh33qo4gef6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9UYXRlcy1CYWtlLVNob3AtR2x1dGVuLUNvb2tpZXMvZHAvQjAwTUVCU1JTMC8=

fact. Tate’s Chocolate Chip Cookies (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/r8ug29rn25boh2494v8u0ix2pw666/48hvhehrrl239wtx/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9UYXRlcy1CYWtlLVNob3AtQ2hvY29sYXRlLUNvb2tpZXMvZHAvQjAwQjYyVzMwNj90aD0x

few of these around Christmas to convince myself I’m behaving.
