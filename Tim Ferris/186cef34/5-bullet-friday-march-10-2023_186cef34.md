Self-documenting their experience, whoever stays the longest wins.” Hat tip to Mr. High Performance himself, [Kelly Starrett](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hr7me47s6/m2h7h5horrxn6gtm/aHR0cHM6Ly90aGVyZWFkeXN0YXRlLmNvbS90aGUtdHJzLWRpZmZlcmVuY2UvI3Ryc3RlYW0=), for the recommendation.
