​The Gene: An Intimate History (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h4q2m9qilh820mwkkb4h2vk/l2hehmhlre5eo2f6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cucGJzbGVhcm5pbmdtZWRpYS5vcmcvcmVzb3VyY2UvdGhlLWdlbmUtZnVsbC1maWxtL3RoZS1nZW5lLWludGltYXRlLWhpc3Rvcnkv
). The Gene is a collaboration of all-stars, including executive
producer Ken Burns (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h4q2m9qilh820mwkkb4h2vk/dpheh0he5vzvqgim/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDE5LzA5LzEyL2tlbi1idXJucy8_dXRtX2NhbXBhaWduPTViZiZ1dG1fbWVkaXVtPWNvbnZlcnRraXQmdXRtX3NvdXJjZT1jb252ZXJ0a2l0
), award-winning senior producer and co-writer Barak Goodman (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h4q2m9qilh820mwkkb4h2vk/e0hph7h74696k3h8/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cucGJzLm9yZy9rZW5idXJucy90aGUtZ2VuZS9tZWV0LXRoZS1maWxtbWFrZXJz
) (Cancer: The Emperor of All Maladies), and Dr. Siddhartha
Mukherjee (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h4q2m9qilh820mwkkb4h2vk/7qh7h8h9zqvq0raz/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvU2lkZGhhcnRoYV9NdWtoZXJqZWU=
), an executive producer and the Pulitzer Prize-winning author of
the book on which the documentary is based.

In roughly four hours, it covers the history of genetics, moving
from the earliest theories of Aristotle and Gregor Mendel’s pea
plant experiments to the discovery of the double helix in 1953
and the modern era of CRISPR and gene editing.

The doc features on-screen interviews with the titans of the
field, including James Watson (co-discoverer of DNA’s structure),
Francis Collins (former director of the National Human Genome
Research Institute), Jennifer Doudna (Nobel Laureate and CRISPR
pioneer), and Nancy Wexler (co-discoverer of the Huntington’s
disease gene), among many others. Check out the trailer here (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/qdu5egp6ekf7h4q2m9qilh820mwkkb4h2vk/owhkhqhwop3p4qtv/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_dj1zMnYyYkNTNjRRNA==
