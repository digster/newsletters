iPhone settings change I FINALLY made
-------------------------------------

“How to take JPEG pictures on your iPhone instead of HEIC (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/e5u4vlw0vkt7hpw8wlnum/m2h7h5holkg2p8im/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuaW1vcmUuY29tL2hvdy10YWtlLWpwZWctcGljdHVyZXMtaW9zLTExLWluc3RlYWQtaGVpZg==
)” Here are the instructions: Settings → Camera → Formats →
Select “Most Compatible.” To switch back to HEIC, simply switch
from “Most Compatible” to “High Efficiency.”
