Documentary I’m watching
------------------------

​Stutz (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/k0u3wp9vw7b6hdkwv03hxi844zr77i8/qvh8h7hrw9n47wfl/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubmV0ZmxpeC5jb20vdGl0bGUvODEzODc5NjI=
). This doc pairs well with Adam’s “what I’m reading” post above.
Stutz was recommended to me by multiple friends, and it does a
beautiful job of sharing both a loving relationship and effective
therapeutic tools for changing your life. I was hesitant to watch
this at first. I worried that it might dredge up old, painful
personal issues that I feel I’ve spent enough time on, but it
didn’t open Pandora’s box at all. As my friends correctly
reassured me, it’s nothing but upside. Here’s the official
description: “In candid conversations with actor Jonah Hill (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/k0u3wp9vw7b6hdkwv03hxi844zr77i8/g3hnh5h394pldwtr/aHR0cHM6Ly9lbi53aWtpcGVkaWEub3JnL3dpa2kvSm9uYWhfSGlsbA==

https://click.fourhourmail.com/k0u3wp9vw7b6hdkwv03hxi844zr77i8/9qhzhnhpwo96qmt9/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhldG9vbHNib29rLmNvbS9hYm91dC1waGlsLWFuZC1iYXJyeQ==

therapy.” Watch the trailer here (
