What I’m using —
[Reusable cup pods for Keurig 2.0 & 1.0 Brewers](https://click.fourhourmail.com/mvu9xermxzs5h47k96u3/e0hph7hk7wgkxzc8/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9kcC9CMDBMR0VLT01TL3JlZj1jbV9zd19yX2FwYW5fZ2x0X2ZhYmNfUURGMlhUSjdGU1Y1MkcxV0NCODk=). Using Keurig coffee machines normally hurts my soul. Plastic garbage up the wazoo. To my delight, I came across these reusable cup pods at a friend’s house. Just pop open, pack with ground coffee (I typically drink [Four Sigmatic](https://click.fourhourmail.com/mvu9xermxzs5h47k96u3/7qh7h8h09p70ggbz/aHR0cDovL2ZvdXJzaWdtYXRpYy5jb20vdGlt)), and you’re ready for the next cup. If you own a Keurig, there’s no need to toss that beautiful machine. These inexpensive, refillable cups will dramatically reduce waste and improve karma.
