What I’m celebrating
--------------------

“MDMA Therapy Inches Closer to Approval (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/0vum69236ps9hoz2ndqf0iwnop855/8ghqhohlx9elngak/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubnl0aW1lcy5jb20vMjAyMy8wOS8xNC9oZWFsdGgvbWRtYS1wdHNkLXBzeWNoZWRlbGljcy5odG1s
)” (The New York Times). Rachel Nuwer (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/0vum69236ps9hoz2ndqf0iwnop855/vqh3hrhpdg2prpag/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDIzLzA1LzMwL21kbWEtYXV0aXNtLXNvY2lhbC1hbnhpZXR5Lz91dG1fc291cmNlPWNvbnZlcnRraXQmdXRtX21lZGl1bT1jb252ZXJ0a2l0JnV0bV9jYW1wYWlnbj01YmY=
) writes: “MDMA-assisted therapy seems to be effective in
reducing symptoms of post-traumatic stress disorder, according to
a study published on Thursday. The research is the final trial
conducted by MAPS Public Benefit Corporation, a company that is
developing prescription psychedelics. It plans to submit the
results to the Food and Drug Administration as part of an
application for approval to market MDMA, the psychedelic drug, as
a treatment for PTSD, when paired with talk therapy.”
​
Additional reading in Nature: “MDMA-assisted therapy for moderate
to severe PTSD: a randomized, placebo-controlled phase 3 trial (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/0vum69236ps9hoz2ndqf0iwnop855/l2hehmh3zr537ls6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubmF0dXJlLmNvbS9hcnRpY2xlcy9zNDE1OTEtMDIzLTAyNTY1LTQ=
