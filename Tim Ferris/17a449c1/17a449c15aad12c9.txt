easiest person to fool.” Many of the compounds that psychedelic
practitioners want to have millenia-old indigenous roots simply
do not; 5-MeO-DMT from the Sonoran Desert toad (often referred to
as “bufo” or “toad”) is one such example. As chemist and
filmmaker Hamilton Morris (

alvarius smoking before the publication of Ken Nelson’s pamphlet
(

attributed to outside influences.” For more thoughts on
toad-derived 5-MeO-DMT, please read this (
https://click.fourhourmail.com/38u8kd42kehkh547qqsz/dpheh0hq7loordfm/aHR0cHM6Ly90aW0uYmxvZy8yMDIxLzAyLzIxL3VyZ2VudC1wbGVhLXVzZXJzLW9mLXBzeWNoZWRlbGljcy1ldGhpY2FsLXBsYW50cy1jb21wb3VuZHMvP3V0bV9zb3VyY2U9Y29udmVydGtpdCZ1dG1fbWVkaXVtPWNvbnZlcnRraXQmdXRtX2NhbXBhaWduPTViZg==
