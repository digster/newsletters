“I had broken myself of the habit of thinking in short song
cycles and began reading longer and longer poems to see if I
could remember anything I read about in the beginning. I trained
my mind to do this, had cast off gloomy habits and learned to
settle myself down. … I began cramming my brain with all kinds of
