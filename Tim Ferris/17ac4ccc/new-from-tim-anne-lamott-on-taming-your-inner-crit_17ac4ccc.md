Anne Lamott ([@AnneLamott](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/kkhmh6h88l7v34al/aHR0cHM6Ly90d2l0dGVyLmNvbS9Bbm5lTGFtb3R0)) uses honesty, empathy, and humor to write about our world. In her beloved and bestselling books, like [Operating Instructions](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/58hvh7h55me2z3t6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9PcGVyYXRpbmctSW5zdHJ1Y3Rpb25zLUpvdXJuYWwtU29ucy1GaXJzdC9kcC8xNDAwMDc5MDk4Lw==) (an account of her son’s first year), [Bird by Bird](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/25h2hoh7725we6i3/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9CaXJkLVNvbWUtSW5zdHJ1Y3Rpb25zLVdyaXRpbmctTGlmZS9kcC8wMzg1NDgwMDE2Lw==) (her classic book on writing), and [Help, Thanks, Wow](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/qvh8h7h88rxp0wul/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9IZWxwLVRoYW5rcy1Xb3ctRXNzZW50aWFsLVByYXllcnMvZHAvMTU5NDYzMTI5OC8=) (a celebration of prayer), Lamott delves into what makes us human. She explores the wide experience of life that unites us: birth and death, parenthood and family, faith and doubt, love and loss, forgiveness and hope.
In each of her 19 books, which have sold millions of copies worldwide, Lamott brings her distinctive mix of bracing candor, clarifying insight, and refreshing humor to convert serious subjects like addiction, motherhood, loss, and faith into human truths we can all share. She is the author of several essay collections on faith, including [Traveling Mercies](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/g3hnh5hee3vwkwir/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9UcmF2ZWxpbmctTWVyY2llcy1Tb21lLVRob3VnaHRzLUZhaXRoL2RwLzAzODU0OTYwOTUv), [Grace (Eventually)](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/9qhzhnhggp7rlms9/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9HcmFjZS1FdmVudHVhbGx5LVRob3VnaHRzLUFubmUtTGFtb3R0L2RwLzE1OTQ0ODI4N1gv), and [Plan B](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/3ohphkhqq7wgn5ir/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9QbGFuLUItRnVydGhlci1UaG91Z2h0cy1GYWl0aC9kcC8xNTk0NDgxNTcxLw==), as well as several novels, including [Imperfect Birds](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/n2hohvhnn3d0kzt6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9JbXBlcmZlY3QtQmlyZHMtTm92ZWwtQW5uZS1MYW1vdHQvZHAvMTU5NDQ4NTA0Ni8=), [Blue Shoe](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/48hvheh00ro8nxbx/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9CbHVlLVNob2UtQW5uZS1MYW1vdHQvZHAvMTU3MzIyMzQyNS8=), and [Rosie](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/wnh2hghrrwz63ls7/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9Sb3NpZS1Bbm5lLUxhbW90dC9kcC8wMTQwMjY0Nzk1Lw==).
Lamott has been honored with a Guggenheim Fellowship and has taught at UC Davis and writing conferences across the country. She is an inductee of the California Hall of Fame and the subject of Academy Award-winning filmmaker Freida Mock’s documentary [Bird by Bird with Annie](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/reh8hohqq08zeps2/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9CaXJkLUFubmUtTGFtb3R0L2RwL0IwMDNQTjJPVjIv) (1999).
Her most recent book is [Dusk, Night, Dawn: On Revival and Courage](https://click.fourhourmail.com/xmuzxlgnxos6hvq0g0t6/08hwh9hmmd8rk8al/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYW1hem9uLmNvbS9EdXNrLU5pZ2h0LURhd24tUmV2aXZhbC1Db3VyYWdlL2RwLzA1OTMzOTU3MzUv).
