Friends, 
Today, we’re introducing two additions to The Generalist: the Deal Club and Reading Club! Every week, we’ll share the most interesting startups we’ve discovered, and our favorite recent reads. 
Our mission at The Generalist is to make you a deeper thinker, better builder, and sharper investor. The Deal Club and Reading Club are new, interactive ways to do that as part of this community. These clubs are exclusively for paying subscribers, so if you’ve been on the fence, now is a great time to join and jump in. 
Here’s how it will work: 
Reading Club. Every Friday, I’ll share the most interesting articles, books, and podcasts I discovered that week. As well as sharing my picks, I invite you to share yours! We’ll all enter the weekend with curated, high-quality information to dig into. The goal is to find new ideas, hear from other hyper-intelligent people, and get visibility into new, trusted information sources. It will also simply be fun :) 
Deal Club. Every Monday, I’ll share the most interesting startups and funding rounds I discovered the week before. I’d love to hear your thoughts and any companies you’ve recently found interesting. The goal is to introduce members to high-potential startups early, sharpen our theses through conversation, and discover new opportunities. You’ll get a chance to compare notes with other investors, operators, and founders. 
We’re getting started right now! Below, you’ll find a link to our first Reading Club chat, sharing my favorite information sources from this week. I’d love to have you join!
As mentioned, to keep the quality of conversation high and forge a tightly knit group, this is just for paying subscribers. Become a premium member to unlock the conversation, and much more. 
How to get started
Get the Substack app by clicking the button below. New chat threads won’t be sent via email. As a result, you may want to have the app on your phone with push notifications on to ensure you don’t miss a conversation. (If you prefer, you can access chats on the web, too.)
Open the app and tap the Chat icon. It looks like a speech bubble icon in the bottom bar. You’ll see The Generalist’s chat inside. 
That’s it! Jump in and say hello.
