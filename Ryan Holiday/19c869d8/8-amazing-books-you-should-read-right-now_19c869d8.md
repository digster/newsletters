MY FAVORITE PHILOSOPHY BOOK
[Meditations](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/g3hnh5h3pqrlzpir/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9tZWRpdGF0aW9ucy1tYXJjdXMtYXVyZWxpdXMtZ3JlZ29yeS1oYXlzLXRyYW5zbGF0aW9uLXByZW1pdW0tbGVhdGhlci1lZGl0aW9u) by Marcus Aurelius
To me, [this is the greatest book ever written](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/9qhzhnhp95062ns9/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9tZWRpdGF0aW9ucy1tYXJjdXMtYXVyZWxpdXMtZ3JlZ29yeS1oYXlzLXRyYW5zbGF0aW9uLXByZW1pdW0tbGVhdGhlci1lZGl0aW9uP19wb3M9NCZfc2lkPWNiMjZhOWFlMCZfc3M9cg==). I've read it [a couple hundred times](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/48hvheh0g4322wux/aHR0cHM6Ly9yeWFuaG9saWRheS5uZXQvMTAwLXRoaW5ncy1sZWFybmVkLTEwLXllYXJzLTEwMC1yZWFkcy1tYXJjdXMtYXVyZWxpdXNzLW1lZGl0YXRpb25zLw==) and have a large passage that I printed out and posted above my desk to look at before I start each day. For me, [this book](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/9qhzhnhp95062ns9/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9tZWRpdGF0aW9ucy1tYXJjdXMtYXVyZWxpdXMtZ3JlZ29yeS1oYXlzLXRyYW5zbGF0aW9uLXByZW1pdW0tbGVhdGhlci1lZGl0aW9uP19wb3M9NCZfc2lkPWNiMjZhOWFlMCZfc3M9cg==) was what Tyler Cowen calls a “quake book”—shaking everything I thought I knew about the world. It is the definitive text on self-discipline, personal ethics, humility, self-actualization and strength. If you [read it](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/9qhzhnhp95062ns9/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9tZWRpdGF0aW9ucy1tYXJjdXMtYXVyZWxpdXMtZ3JlZ29yeS1oYXlzLXRyYW5zbGF0aW9uLXByZW1pdW0tbGVhdGhlci1lZGl0aW9uP19wb3M9NCZfc2lkPWNiMjZhOWFlMCZfc3M9cg==) and aren’t profoundly changed by it, it’s probably because as Aurelius says “what doesn’t transmit light creates its own darkness.” You HAVE to read the [Gregory Hays translation](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/3ohphkh7zp02dxbr/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9waGlsb3NvcGh5MTU_X3Bvcz0yJl9zaWQ9OGZlOWI3OGI5Jl9zcz1y). In fact, we loved it so much, we created a [leatherbound version to withstand the test of time](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/9qhzhnhp95062ns9/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9tZWRpdGF0aW9ucy1tYXJjdXMtYXVyZWxpdXMtZ3JlZ29yeS1oYXlzLXRyYW5zbGF0aW9uLXByZW1pdW0tbGVhdGhlci1lZGl0aW9uP19wb3M9NCZfc2lkPWNiMjZhOWFlMCZfc3M9cg==).

MY FAVORITE NOVEL
[What Makes Sammy Run?](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/6qheh8hpom4276uo/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9maWN0aW9uMzE_X3Bvcz0xJl9zaWQ9MDk3ZTA2ZGFiJl9zcz1y) by Budd Schulberg
This was one of the [first books I read](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/6qheh8hpom4276uo/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9maWN0aW9uMzE_X3Bvcz0xJl9zaWQ9MDk3ZTA2ZGFiJl9zcz1y) when I started working in Hollywood, and it had a powerful impact on me at 19. Some ten years later, I pulled the book from my shelf while finishing the first draft of [Ego Is The Enemy](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/l2hehmhor8pk38t6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9yeWFuMj9fcG9zPTEmX3NpZD1kMWRjNGNlNGUmX3NzPXI=) and rediscovered three handwritten pages of notes I folded and stuck in the back. Those notes expressed many of the painful lessons I wanted to share in [Ego Is The Enemy](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/l2hehmhor8pk38t6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9yeWFuMj9fcG9zPTEmX3NpZD1kMWRjNGNlNGUmX3NzPXI=), so I adapted them into the epilogue that made it to publication. [What Makes Sammy Run?](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/6qheh8hpom4276uo/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9maWN0aW9uMzE_X3Bvcz0xJl9zaWQ9MDk3ZTA2ZGFiJl9zcz1y) is a novel that reminds us that even when egotists "win," they lose. It’s also a fascinating look at the entertainment industry and what makes hustlers and strivers do the things they do.
Honestly, I have trouble limiting this to just one, so I also recommend Walker Percy’s [The Moviegoer](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/48hvhehrgxvp2lsx/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9tb3ZpZWdvZXI=), Ralph Ellison’s [Invisible Man](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/wnh2hghwg0nko6u7/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9maWN0aW9uMTY=) and John Fante’s [Ask the Dust](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/08hwh9hdzlv976ul/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9maWN0aW9uMz9fcG9zPTEmX3NpZD05MjVhMzdhY2YmX3NzPXI=). I also put together [this list](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/8ghqhohl9rz25mtk/aHR0cHM6Ly90aG91Z2h0Y2F0YWxvZy5jb20vcnlhbi1ob2xpZGF5LzIwMTUvMDQvMjQtZmljdGlvbi1ib29rcy10aGF0LWNhbi1jaGFuZ2UteW91ci1saWZlLw==) of my favorite 24 fiction books.
MY FAVORITE NON-FICTION BOOK
[The Tiger: A True Story of Vengeance and Survival](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/dpheh0hq5lx7rwcm/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9ub25maWN0aW9uMTc_X3Bvcz0yJl9zaWQ9MWIwZDg0M2Q1Jl9zcz1y) by John Vaillant
Damn [this book is good](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/vqh3hrhpgwlvkwcg/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9ub25maWN0aW9uMTc=). Just [so good](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/vqh3hrhpgwlvkwcg/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9ub25maWN0aW9uMTc=). Even if it was just the main narrative—the chase to kill a man-eating Tiger in Siberia in post-communist Russia—it would be worth [reading](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/vqh3hrhpgwlvkwcg/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9ub25maWN0aW9uMTc=). But it is so much more than that. The author explains the Russian psyche, the psyche of man vs predator, the psyches of primitive peoples and animals—and he does it in such a masterful way that you’re shocked to find 1) that he knows this, and 2) that he fit it all into [this readable and relatively short book](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/vqh3hrhpgwlvkwcg/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9ub25maWN0aW9uMTc=). I’ve recommended [this book](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/vqh3hrhpgwlvkwcg/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9ub25maWN0aW9uMTc=) to thousands of people and have heard countless times how much they loved it. Not a single complaint.

MY FAVORITE MEMOIR
[Totto-Chan: The Little Girl at the Window](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/e0hph7hk48znm2h8/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9tZW1vaXIxND9fcG9zPTEmX3NpZD00M2U5OWU4YTImX3NzPXI=) by Tetsuko Kuroyanagi
[This book](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/e0hph7h04r6lk9f8/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9tZW1vaXIxNA==) has sold something like 8 million copies in Japan alone (an insane number). Totto-Chan is a special figure in modern Japanese culture—she is a celebrity on par with Oprah, with a magazine, a news show, and a foundation with international impact. [This book](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/e0hph7h04r6lk9f8/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9tZW1vaXIxNA==) describes her childhood in pre-WWII Japan as a misunderstood girl who obviously suffered from attention disorders and excess energy. It wasn't until she met a special school principal—unlike any I have ever heard of—who finally GOT her. Meaning, she understood, cared about and unconditionally supported her in a way that both inspires me and makes me deeply jealous. If only all of us could be so lucky…
And if you haven’t read [My Bondage and My Freedom](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/7qh7h8hoznq60ktz/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9tZW1vaXIxMQ==) by Frederick Douglass, man are you missing out. [This is the incredible story](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/7qh7h8hoznq60ktz/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlcGFpbnRlZHBvcmNoLmNvbS9wcm9kdWN0cy9tZW1vaXIxMQ==) of a man who is born a slave, teaches himself to read, decides he will no longer consent to being whipped, then runs away and becomes one of America’s foremost intellectuals and one of our second founding fathers. ([Here are 5 of my favorite pages](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/8ghqhohg9m85zgsk/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuaW5zdGFncmFtLmNvbS9wL0NCWVExbEVId2FILw==)).

MY FAVORITE BUSINESS BOOK
[Letters from a Self-Made Merchant to His Son](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/3ohphkh7zp02qxur/aHR0cHM6Ly9nZW5pLnVzL1gweXpERA==) by George Horace Lorimer
It might seem weird to recommend [this 100 year old book](https://bc5923e4.click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uknz08p6smur26z2fdfz2q9970clhkp4x5/3ohphkh7zp02qxur/aHR0cHM6Ly9nZW5pLnVzL1gweXpERA==), but it really is the best business book I’ve ever read.
