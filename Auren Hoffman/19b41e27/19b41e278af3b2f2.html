Palantir’s CTO on the history of the post-Cold War defense industry.

The Origins of Wokeness by Paul Graham
every society has self-righteous moral enforcers. What changes is the rules they enforce, from Victorian Christian virtue to today’s social justice.

Data businesses are good businesses. They make good profits. They grow (albeit slowly) well. But they are not great businesses – and should not take VC funding.
