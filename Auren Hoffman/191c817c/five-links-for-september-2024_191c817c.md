Here are five links worth reading…
[How California Turned Against Growth](https://substack.com/redirect/25447ac9-f6eb-48d2-95e5-57d4b248a74f?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by [Brian Potter](https://open.substack.com/users/3518108-brian-potter?utm_source=mentions)
For over a century, California was a fast growing state (often the fastest growing). In 2020, the state lost population for the first time. This is a deep dive on how California built itself so big and why that hundred-year growth trend is reversing.
[The Right Kind of Stubborn](https://substack.com/redirect/0ef98873-ccd9-4619-bdd6-dfd07e60c974?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by Paul Graham
Some essential nuance for the common advice to “be persistent.” PG breaks down what makes the persistent different from the obstinate.
[REVIEW: The Real North Korea, by Andrei Lankov](https://substack.com/redirect/b35fd9a2-9a31-4e8e-b4a3-567d9e380b72?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by [John Psmith](https://open.substack.com/users/119039652-john-psmith?utm_source=mentions)
An insider’s view of North Korea full of fascinating tidbits, like submarine-assisted kidnappings, a communist caste system, and black markets run almost entirely by women.
Bonus (Listen): [Bryan Caplan - The Signaling Game in Education & Parenting](https://substack.com/redirect/300bff8e-4949-4f59-a6cd-7b9dd1771b5f?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) on World of DaaS Podcast
Economist Bryan Caplan has done the research on education and concluded 80% of the value is signaling and only 20% is actually human capital building.
listen [at World of DaaS](https://substack.com/redirect/300bff8e-4949-4f59-a6cd-7b9dd1771b5f?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o)
[Moscow’s Spies Were Stealing US Tech — Until the FBI Started a Sabotage Campaign](https://substack.com/redirect/ef347927-3297-45f3-a640-d9264ae67358?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by Zach Dorfman
How the FBI used Silicon Valley connections to sell sabotaged technology to Eastern Bloc countries, which undermined Soviet efforts to acquire advanced US tech.
[America’s Top Export May Be Anxiety](https://substack.com/redirect/f5d41a51-b18e-47de-9afb-695132b18a05?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by Derek Thompson
In the former Soviet Union and East Asia, there have been large increases in happiness, while anxiety and depression in the English speaking world seem to be getting worse.
Bonus (Hustle): [Priscila, Queen of the Rideshare Mafia](https://substack.com/redirect/ebddb0e7-abb8-40d1-874c-c5e1c94a7c46?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by Lauren Smiley
On a Brazilian immigrant who figured out how to make tens of thousands of dollars a week off Uber and Lyft without driving.
Bonus (Unexpected): [The A.I. Boom Makes Millions for an Unlikely Industry Player: Anguilla](https://substack.com/redirect/7240694b-badc-4d00-a944-506ecfd29923?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by Emma Bubola
