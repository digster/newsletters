’The Mozart of the attention economy': why MrBeast is the world's biggest YouTube star [ https://substack.com/redirect/085f7c28-06a7-4021-9d0b-c2f3b5f10785?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] by Mark O'Connell

How Barry Diller Stayed on Top [ https://substack.com/redirect/5db3c3b9-c3d5-4efa-ae59-5ac520522663?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] by Adam Gopnik
Over his 50 year career, Barry Diller greenlit The Simpsons and Indiana Jones, founded QVC and Fox, and introduced Rupert Murdoch and Roger Ailes. His memoir shows how entertainment hits actually get made - through high-stakes bets and improbable wins.
Who owns U.S. and Japanese debt [ https://substack.com/redirect/c75a2e23-9ed9-4464-a1e7-c31d09d705df?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] by StockMarket.News
A viral thread breaking down how Japan's central bank owns 52% of the country's $7.8 trillion debt, while foreign investors hold over $9 trillion in U.S. debt - creating very different but equally concerning vulnerabilities for both countries
Bonus (Listen): Superpower Founder/President Max Marchione - Billionaire Health Protocols and the Future of Medicine [ https://substack.com/redirect/00513b71-ad47-40b4-ad2c-8bc13f304dbf?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] on World of DaaS  [ https://substack.com/redirect/00513b71-ad47-40b4-ad2c-8bc13f304dbf?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] pod
Max argues that a lot of "billionaire-level" health protocols are surprisingly accessible (or at least they will be soon), and in the next ten years we’ll move away from preventing disease towards active biological engineering.
How Japan Invented Modern Shipbuilding [ https://substack.com/redirect/3ca73765-ac47-4257-83d9-244e16a04e55?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] by Brian Potter  
Japan revolutionized shipbuilding by combining WWII-era prefabricated welding techniques with aircraft-style production, allowing them to build ships faster and cheaper than anyone else, eventually capturing 50% of global shipbuilding by 1970.
The Mysterious Billionaire Behind the OnlyFans Porn Empire [ https://substack.com/redirect/e972fcad-9b76-4cd1-9399-1f1292a55dbf?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] by Sam Schechner and Katherine Sayre
Leo Radvinsky bought OnlyFans in 2018 and transformed it from ad-supported videos into a subscription model that's made him nearly $4 billion - now he's seeking an $8 billion exit while maintaining extreme secrecy about his identity and business operations.
Bonus (Friendship): How to have friends past age 30 [ https://substack.com/redirect/e1b17f19-5508-4c51-a16b-66df6943b51e?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] by Noah Smith
Noah's simple formula for adult friendship, from creating a friend group to fostering deep relationships.
