The Kidnapping I Can’t Escape [ https://substack.com/redirect/c4a8486c-fab4-4cc5-9d52-4df136eb61ee?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] by Taffy Brodesser-Akner
50 years ago, Jack Teich survived being kidnapped and held ransom for a week. He was returned to his family after they paid $750,000 ransom, the highest ransom ever paid in the US at that time. This story is about his life after the kidnapping. 
Everything you think you know about your first VC pitch is wrong [ https://substack.com/redirect/d522cf06-7eee-421e-8bfe-fb0835eea6d1?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ]
There’s so much bad advice out there about pitching to VCs. This is a simple no BS manual for early stage VC pitches. 
Immunotherapy Is Changing Cancer Treatment Forever [ https://substack.com/redirect/6391507f-ca73-4282-95b8-a2cce6ddda47?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] by Christopher Cox
Glioblastoma, the most common type of malignant brain cancer, was treated the same way for decades with an average survival rate of about a year after diagnosis. Now, patients are receiving injections that can shrink their tumors without chemo or radiation.

Legacy Lost: How Toyota Beat GM [ https://substack.com/redirect/ea17e60f-3c15-404d-9c4f-c89b85a3845d?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] by Henry Juszkiewicz
