Here are five links worth reading…
[The Two Big Games](https://substack.com/redirect/a2dac65a-e9a0-4515-88cb-6ef5bf9fc76d?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by [Robin Hanson](https://open.substack.com/users/280980-robin-hanson?utm_source=mentions)
On group decision making, consensus games and outcome games. If you’re a leader, you should be thinking about which of these games you’re creating in your organization.
[the decline of the investment bankers and the rise of lawyers](https://substack.com/redirect/7ce282cf-47ba-4699-b861-d1e38b00fce6?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o)
Looking at the hierarchy of prestigious finance jobs over five decades, investment bankers have fallen from the top spot to fifth place, while lawyers have climbed from fifth to second (and they might rise even further thanks to AI leverage)
[Where Geniuses Hide Today](https://substack.com/redirect/6a07c5b3-86ff-466f-bc7d-460247dd1276?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by [Tomas Pueyo](https://open.substack.com/users/5362415-tomas-pueyo?utm_source=mentions)
“Dozens of thinkers complain that there are no more geniuses. Most just don’t know where to find them.”
Listen: [Mark Pincus - Product Instincts: “Proven, Better, New”](https://substack.com/redirect/3b373ce9-c4a5-44fa-8897-73c563c598d2?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) (World of DaaS pod)
Mark Pincus helped build one of the most popular games of all time and has one of the best product frameworks out there.
[The Origins of Wokeness](https://substack.com/redirect/3fd19c65-3379-470e-9ef0-974b93735181?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by Paul Graham
Paul’s deep investigation into "wokeness" reveals it's actually a recurring pattern: every society has self-righteous moral enforcers. What changes is the rules they enforce, from Victorian Christian virtue to today's social justice.
[The Global Hunt for Putin’s ‘Sleeper Agents’](https://substack.com/redirect/9a76bf08-960d-44bc-8c59-767b40fd7de1?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by Drew Hinshaw & Jo Parkinson
Inside the international manhunt for Russia’s undercover European agents, including a hard-drinking war correspondent to a suburban mom running an art gallery who spent years building false identities to spy on the West from within.
Bonus: [the ultimate guide to holding world class dinner parties](https://substack.com/redirect/c5293f6b-3017-4291-980e-5972f0a3b98f?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o)
There is nothing lamer than an ill-planned dinner. I have attended enough not so great dinner parties to put together a solid blueprint for hosting a good one.
Bonus (Crystal ball) [Crazy bets on 2025 - 12 personal hedge funds predict the year](https://substack.com/redirect/aa0a7099-f213-4777-b8b9-0603b33768e2?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o)
Although our 2024 predictions returned a measly 3.13%, I brought together another anonymous group of VIPs to make predictions for 2025.
[TitleDock](https://substack.com/redirect/9459279d-2bf0-4fbb-8927-cc9733e12284?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) launched: it is Superhuman for Contacts. It has been my secret way of managing contacts.
Bonus (Listen): [Samo Burja live players vs NPCs](https://substack.com/redirect/ca80740e-a623-4877-ad61-dc57b9c67dfa?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) (World of DaaS)
Your dose of ancient civilizations, technology, parenting, philosophy. Samo is a unique thinker who pulls the whole scope of Western history into his views.
