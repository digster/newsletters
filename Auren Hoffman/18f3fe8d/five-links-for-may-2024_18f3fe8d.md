[Why You Can’t Get a Restaurant Reservation](https://substack.com/redirect/68d0dde0-061e-4c7e-8cfa-1a592b127984?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by Adam Iscoe

Since the beginning of COVID, California added a sum total of only 6k jobs in the tech industry—compared to roughly 570k across the rest of the United States.

Some rare good business news from the media industry– how CEO (and former [World of DaaS guest](https://substack.com/redirect/8be8c02f-e0de-4b5e-aa7a-5bd9c6887623?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o)) Nick Thompson took The Atlantic from a $20 million deficit to profitability.
[What Makes Housing So Expensive?](https://substack.com/redirect/c9523d2a-4774-48a6-b2d5-a0f418e0929e?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o) by Brian Potter
When we talk about reducing housing costs, we usually talk about land use and zoning. This is a useful primer on the actual material costs that go into building a home, and how they could be reduced.

Data on 3,067 startups founded in 2018. The takeaway: It’s the second 5 years where the real value starts to compound. Startups are a long game.
