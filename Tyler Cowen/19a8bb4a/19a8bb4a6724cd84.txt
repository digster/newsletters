Post       : Best fiction of 2025
URL        : https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/11/best-fiction-of-2025.html
Posted     : November 16, 2025 at 3:04 am
Author     : Tyler Cowen
Categories : Books, Uncategorized

Solvej Balle, On the Calculation of Volume I, Volume II ( https://www.amazon.com/Calculation-Book-II-Solvej-Balle/dp/0811237273/ref=tmm_pap_swatch_0 ) .  Volume III is due out in English late this year I have read it already in German.  A very strong series, reading ahead in German is a good demonstration of how much I like them.

Suat Dervis, The Prisoner of Ankara ( https://www.amazon.com/Prisoner-Ankara-Novel-Suat-Dervis/dp/189274693X/ref=sr_1_1?crid=3A9UQYFJKMWHC&dib=eyJ2IjoiMSJ9.tRhvakDIaj2HQy0VFV-OFk5wcVQu_MsQGn7EaaIjfUS-j6kWyaefYox5n7c3zZSoBffWHiwdXSCTkMQ6WwxXqlgaoIpZHBLPqVwnJzIGWP8.F4qRTKnw3nFvTGkM3ZR7AAdwtMl2ogXvr6RKRUI9qJ4&dib_tag=se&keywords=prisoner+of+ankara&qid=1763077802&sprefix=prisoner+of+ankara%2Caps%2C308&sr=8-1 ) .  A Turkish novel from mid-century, in English for the first time.

Emmanuel Carrere, V13: Chronicle of a Trial ( https://www.amazon.com/V13-Chronicle-Trial-Emmanuel-Carr%C3%A8re/dp/0374615705/ref=sr_1_1?crid=3948W0GDCCIY9&dib=eyJ2IjoiMSJ9.huXCi8js5TDxe8bKbE6pTA.wgGt0Tu2rQQngyv4Jy_oBV9biYfsZCduHDcYISPMAJU&dib_tag=se&keywords=V13%3A+Chronicle+of+a+Trial&qid=1763077836&sprefix=v13+chronicle+of+a+trial%2Caps%2C311&sr=8-1 ) .  Non-fiction but it is more likely reading fiction, it just happens to be true (supposedly).

Alain Mabanckou, Dealing with the Dead ( https://www.amazon.com/Dealing-Dead-Alain-Mabanckou/dp/1800817703/ref=sr_1_1?crid=8AHZF2K5FK43&dib=eyJ2IjoiMSJ9.t90tpqDFoG6Ym8ZC8svA-Tyc1m7Qx-KBd1uIt7A17RSwhklyhzR5mLf5uFo8bRnWJUtZkduRTq1Vcbvj9zgXSHymw5m18B7ZR63ZLM-aKWgMNI_W-MAL-aLlMw94R78SSOEzG7bzmk9QeAPOnEUVjCLH2gu6trtkigVEssbI569JpY9trcJmeHXfztsBI4G6OEy05h_2aBYqTuvBjl4ay-Opw3XYLmpYbuMe1ZLGgFo.2HBgE_8J8q5ElRWjapz_IFxJyATZA-LEq_UVHUO8UqU&dib_tag=se&keywords=Dealing+with+the+Dead&qid=1740611764&sprefix=dealing+with+the+dead%2Caps%2C68&sr=8-1 ) .  Most African fiction does not connect with me, and there is a tendency for the reviews to be untrustworthy.  This “cemetery memoir,” from the Congo (via UCLA), held my interest throughout.

Kiran Desai, The Loneliness of Sonia and Sunny ( https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/10/the-loneliness-of-sonia-and-sunny-now-finished.html ) .

Eça de Queiros ( https://pt.wikipedia.org/wiki/E%C3%A7a_de_Queiroz ) , Adam and Eve in Paradise ( https://www.amazon.com/Adam-Eve-Paradise-E%C3%A7a-Queir%C3%B3s/dp/0811239144/ref=sr_1_1?crid=2SRNFP4QUFKWA&dib=eyJ2IjoiMSJ9.yIRo6NFdPE62enBR9vhTq92yORv79H9zk1LEHdDRdVJpsWkILHnBQDfowSosq3RMECpiifSvVGDsoAVWJA_8JE0SDY1nblMd08_ar49Gjyg5bs-EwS5jPfWl5H21jekbOxN4sv1xHUSxFqE3O6mMpVNKyIK_iX6-GjPIm_iaik_ra8TuSXSEscyIsR58Zr4FasL5zbqEh50Q9O-6CrZ1ndi3JXqBaWdJAjug2AJ2-dY.VMF06zJl0OvyxFpBtwcetDVqq8cPcj_qRzjLx_Oxg18&dib_tag=se&keywords=adam+and+eve+in+paradise+book&qid=1763077970&sprefix=adam+and+eve+in+para%2Caps%2C485&sr=8-1 ) .  Originally from the 19th century, but translated into English only this year. A 60 pp. novella about exactly what the title indicates, noting that matters are not as simple as the first telling of that story might have suggested.

The Poems of Seamus Heaney ( https://www.amazon.com/Poems-Seamus-Heaney/dp/0374235236/ref=sr_1_1?crid=1M3NGY6HK90XD&dib=eyJ2IjoiMSJ9.BfkikyrpcPX8MClNC_KPeYglVOQGUppydom6FtgdgfjazC5t9uEN6dGJJTRS21rNXfNoU5ottsmRvtwHvG5gN9yW9luuqrIhRf1NbvQPQcvb4dX6n_Yym2h3J0jIx552Vflmwsh6PNZw_IylCzmHb8GD04BaMNJi2WARj3Y06PcNApWWyhITj11_BCENaJDf9bju45CFVk1B92fGV_gE2E0zjMgD_7bX-C1-41c_l4c.nYpvlhvs8GP8hxkzoNj6nXtgAdbeI-0kH2X87J5AhTk&dib_tag=se&keywords=poems+of+seamus+heaney&qid=1763077601&sprefix=poems+of+sea%2Caps%2C319&sr=8-1 ) .  Not yet received, but obviously this is a winner.

Overall, the Balle, Desai, and Heaney make for very strong entries, so this was a good year for fiction.
