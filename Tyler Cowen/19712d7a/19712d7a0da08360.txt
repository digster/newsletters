Post       : The Ohio Adam Smith mandate
URL        : https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/05/the-ohio-adam-smith-mandate.html
Posted     : May 27, 2025 at 1:40 pm
Author     : Tyler Cowen
Categories : Current Affairs, Education, Law, Uncategorized

> For inspiration they might look to Ohio, where next month, the recently signed Senate Bill 1 ( https://www.legislature.ohio.gov/legislation/136/sb1 )  (The Advance Ohio Higher Education Act) will take effect, mandating, among other things, that every state institution of higher education require its bachelor’s students to pass a course in “the subject area of American civic literacy.” At a minimum, no student will graduate without demonstrating proficiency in the Constitution, the Declaration of Independence, the Federalist Papers, the Emancipation Proclamation, the Gettysburg Address, Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.’s “Letter from Birmingham Jail,” and (for the sake of understanding the free market) selections from the writings of Adam Smith.

Personnel is policy I say!  That is from Solveig Lucia Gold at The Free Press ( https://www.thefp.com/p/trump-harvard-america-first?utm_campaign=trueanthem&utm_medium=organic-social&utm_source=twitter ) .
