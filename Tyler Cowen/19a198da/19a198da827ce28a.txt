Post       : Emergent Ventures India, 11th cohort

Saket Sinha ( https://www.instagram.com/saketkeshav )  is an accomplished bansuri virtuoso with more than seventy students worldwide. His grant enables a move to Mumbai.

Riddhi Jain, 17, received her grant to build an AI-powered mental health system addressing unaffordable and stigmatized therapy.

Advik Kapoor ( https://x.com/heyadvikkapoor ) , 16, received his grant for Exerton ( https://exerton.xyz/ ) , to help builders get started with their dream projects.

Vibhuti Bafna ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/vibhuti-bafna-b409a3285/ ) , Aliya Mamadfozilova ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/aliyamamadfozilova/ ) , Julian Drotkiewicz ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/julian-drotkiewicz-3b2a74298/ )  and Enya Dumitru ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/enya-dumitru/ )  are high-schoolers in four different countries. They received their grant for Waste2o, turning agricultural waste into potable water.

Ishan Khire ( https://x.com/IshanKhire ) , 18, received his grant for Rural Analytics ( https://www.ruralanalytics.org/ ) , to make rural development data more accessible for researchers.

Nikitaa Sivaakumar ( https://www.wonder-yonder.com/ )  received her grant to develop interactive visual aids for high school science teachers.

Jhillika Trisal ( https://x.com/jhillikaa )  (with Falguni Shrivastava ( https://x.com/falguni__ )  and Souvik Ghosh ( https://x.com/souvikg_tweets ) ) received her grant for building Cognitii ( http://www.cognitii.com ) , an AI‑plus‑human learning platform for children with special needs; the grant scales pilots and the personalization engine.

Piyush Jha ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/piyushdevlpr/ ) , 18, founder of Vasudeva Innovations ( https://www.linkedin.com/company/vasudeva-innovations/ ) , received his grant to turn wastewater into clean energy while earning carbon credits.

Ambreen Deol ( https://ambreendeol.com/ )  is an aspiring surgeon who has rotated at Cleveland Clinic, Stanford, Mount Sinai and UAB, received her grant for travel and general career support.

Anjali Jayaraman ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/anjali-j-390073237/ ) , 14, received her grant for Repay Smart, to help young adults make smarter financial decisions using gamification.

Arjun Khemani ( https://x.com/arjunkhemani )  received his grant for the Arjun Khemani Podcast, and work on his writing. His latest book Lords of the Cosmos ( https://www.amazon.com/Lords-Cosmos-Stasis-Arjun-Khemani-ebook/dp/B0FQGJD86K?ref_=ast_author_dp&th=1&psc=1 )  (With Logal Chipkin) is out now.

Adwait Dandwate ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/adwait-dandwate/ )  received his grant for Vardhishnu ( https://vardhishnu.org/ ) , to create learning spaces for children from vulnerable backgrounds.

Amruth Ravindranath ( https://linkedin.com/in/amruth )  is a neuroscience researcher, and received his grant to develop cognitive assessments and AI models that personalize mental health chatbots to each person’s unique cognitive fingerprint.

Shaunak Agarkhedkar ( https://espionage.substack.com/ )  is a novelist ( https://www.amazon.com/stores/author/B074VB5LK3/allbooks ) , and received his grant to write novels challenging myths about stray animals.

Kaustubh Bankapure ( https://appliedtheatreindia.com/ )  received his grant to create an online learning model of applied theatre education for Indian educators.

Kavish Garg ( https://www.kavishgarg.com/ ) , 18, a sophomore studying math and philosophy at Stanford, received his grant for conference and travel support.

Ria Khurana ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/ria-khurana-88175124b/ )  and Tanmaya Gulati ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/tanmaya-gulati/ ) , both 22 and studying medicine, and founders of RNT Health Insights ( https://www.rntinsights.com/ ) , received their grant to develop medical devices detecting early-stage gastrointestinal cancers.
