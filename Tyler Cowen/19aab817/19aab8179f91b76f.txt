In my post, Horseshoe Theory: Trump and the Progressive Left ( https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/07/horseshoe-theory-trump-and-the-progressive-left.html ) , I said:

> Trump’s political coalition isn’t policy-driven. It’s built on anger, grievance, and zero-sum thinking. With minor tweaks, there is no reason why such a coalition could not become even more leftist. Consider the grotesque canonization of Luigi Mangione, the (alleged) murderer of UnitedHealthcare CEO Brian Thompson. We already have a proposed CA ballot initiative named the Luigi Mangione Access to Health Care Act ( https://www.newsweek.com/luigi-mangione-inspired-ballot-initiative-targets-health-insurance-denials-2052789 ) , a Luigi Mangione musical ( https://www.sfchronicle.com/entertainment/article/luigi-the-musical-mangione-san-francisco-20783179.php )  and comparisons of Mangione to Jesus ( https://www.telegraph.co.uk/politics/2025/07/23/doctors-union-leader-compared-luigi-mangione-to-jesus/ ) . The anger is very Trumpian.

In that light, consider one of Trump's recent postings ( https://x.com/StephenMoore/status/1991226859665625571?s=20 ) :

> THE ONLY HEALTHCARE I WILL SUPPORT OR APPROVE IS SENDING THE MONEY DIRECTLY BACK TO THE PEOPLE, WITH NOTHING GOING TO THE BIG, FAT, RICH INSURANCE COMPANIES, WHO HAVE MADE $TRILLIONS, AND RIPPED OFF AMERICA LONG ENOUGH.
