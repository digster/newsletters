No, this is not an AI post.  Codex ( https://codexbooks.info/ )  is a NYC bookshop at 1 Bleecker St., at Bowery.  It is quite extraordinary in its curation of used books.  The fiction section is large, yet you can pick up virtually any title on the shelves and it is worth reading.  A wonderful place to go to get reading ideas, plus the prices are reasonable and the used books are in decent shape.  Such achievements should be praised.
