Posted     : March 18, 2025 at 7:18 am
Author     : Alex Tabarrok
Categories : Current Affairs, Economics, Education

The annual Public Choice Outreach Conference is a crash course in public choice. The conference is designed for undergraduates and graduates in a wide variety of fields. It’s entirely free. Indeed scholarships are available! The conference will be held Friday May 30-Sunday June 1, 2025, near Washington, DC in Arlington, VA. Lots of great speakers. More details in the poster. Please encourage your students to apply ( https://publicchoice.gmu.edu/center-programs/public-choice-outreach-conference-2025 ) .
