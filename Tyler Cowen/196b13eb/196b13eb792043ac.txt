Post       : Globalization did not hollow out the U.S. middle class
URL        : https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/05/globalization-did-not-hollow-out-the-u-s-middle-class.html
Posted     : May 8, 2025 at 2:49 pm
Author     : Tyler Cowen
Categories : Data Source, Economics, Uncategorized

From Noah Smith:

> Trade deficits ( https://substack.com/redirect/1e9a9b61-b340-45f3-8bd1-8a17b263a1f5?j=eyJ1IjoiNmc3N3YifQ.o5vzsFdJxuM1h9-cxhYnEIJl5uQa-l9E7gQ2m0qwXKQ )  are an even smaller amount of GDP. U.S. imports of manufactured goods minus exports are equal to about 4% of GDP per year. Our trade deficit with China is about 1% of GDP ( https://substack.com/redirect/9253c136-a562-4175-ac8a-30ac133122c1?j=eyJ1IjoiNmc3N3YifQ.o5vzsFdJxuM1h9-cxhYnEIJl5uQa-l9E7gQ2m0qwXKQ ) .
>
> In terms of imported components, America manufactures most of what it uses in production. China’s exports to the U.S. are actually more likely to be intermediate goods rather than the consumer goods we see on the shelves of Wal-Mart — another thing the typical narrative misses. But even so, China makes only about 3.5% ( https://substack.com/redirect/65709af0-70a0-40d0-90be-7aab5e86ffa2?j=eyJ1IjoiNmc3N3YifQ.o5vzsFdJxuM1h9-cxhYnEIJl5uQa-l9E7gQ2m0qwXKQ )  of the intermediate goods that American manufacturers need
>
> ...trade deficits and manufacturing aren’t as tightly linked as most people seem to think. France has become steadily less manufacturing-intensive since 1960, despite the fact that it historically had very balanced trade, and even ran big trade surpluses ( https://substack.com/redirect/2b89e3f9-b778-4b72-bbbf-5cd02ce4cdd9?j=eyJ1IjoiNmc3N3YifQ.o5vzsFdJxuM1h9-cxhYnEIJl5uQa-l9E7gQ2m0qwXKQ )  in the 90s and 00s. Meanwhile, out of all the countries on the chart, Japan has done the best job of preserving its manufacturing share since 2010, despite running a trade deficit ( https://substack.com/redirect/59f9607d-1dfe-4cea-b788-29b9c30672af?j=eyJ1IjoiNmc3N3YifQ.o5vzsFdJxuM1h9-cxhYnEIJl5uQa-l9E7gQ2m0qwXKQ )  over that time period.

Excellent throughout, do read the whole thing ( https://www.noahpinion.blog/p/globalization-did-not-hollow-out ) .
