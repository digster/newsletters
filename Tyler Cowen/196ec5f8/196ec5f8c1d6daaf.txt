Post       : The economics of sleep
URL        : https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/05/the-economics-of-sleep.html
Posted     : May 20, 2025 at 2:23 am
Author     : Tyler Cowen
Categories : Data Source, Economics, Uncategorized

> Full-time, prime-age male workers in the top income quartile sleep around half an hour less per day than those in the lowest quartile.
>
> At the macro level, average sleep duration decreases as a country’s GDP increases.
>
> Higher-income individuals allocate more time to other leisure activities, such as social outings and internet usage, substituting sleep.

Here is the paper ( https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1570677X25000292 )  by Cristián Jara, Francisca Pérez, and Rodrigo Wagner. Via the excellent Kevin Lewis ( https://www.nationalaffairs.com/blog/detail/findings-a-daily-roundup/top-to-bottom ) .
