> When will AI forecasters match top human forecasters at predicting the future? In a recent @cowenconvos  ( https://x.com/cowenconvos ) podcast episode, @NateSilver538  ( https://x.com/NateSilver538 )  said 10–15 years while @tylercowen  ( https://x.com/tylercowen ) predicted 1–2 years. Who was right? Our updated AI forecasting benchmark, ForecastBench, suggests that Tyler Cowen is more likely to be right.

Here is the tweet ( https://x.com/Research_FRI/status/1975921240486379922 ) , here is the associated Substack ( https://forecastingresearch.substack.com/p/ai-llm-forecasting-model-forecastbench-benchmark ) .  Phil Tetlock informs me their best guess is probably sometime in 2026.
