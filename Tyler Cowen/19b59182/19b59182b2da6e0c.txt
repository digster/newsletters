Post       : Emergent Ventures India, 15th cohort
URL        : https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/12/emergent-ventures-india-15th-cohort.html
Posted     : December 26, 2025 at 12:14 am
Author     : Tyler Cowen
Categories : Education

Adnan Abbasi ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/adnan-abbasi-bb659a116/?originalSubdomain=in ) , 25, founder of Thotica ( https://thothica.com/ ) , received his grant to add an archive reader to make rare historical texts accessible using AI-powered translation. Also check out his AI generated debate between Nehru and Hayek ( https://open.spotify.com/episode/2rvdq5qiavbpeFxcXOYAB2 ) .

Dheemanth Reddy ( https://x.com/dheemanthreddy_?lang=en ) , co-founder of Maya Research ( https://www.mayaresearch.ai/ ) , received his grant to build Veena ( https://x.com/Dheemanthreddy_/status/1937839083281437021 )  - cutting-edge speech models for English and Indian languages as naturally spoken by Indians.

Ritisha Sethi, 16, a high schooler from Lucknow, received her grant to develop Qubit Quest, her solution to help learn quantum computing through gamification.

Jnanendra K S received his grant to convert vintage cars to EVs in his automotive mechanic shop.

Sankalp Shrivastava ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/shrivastavasankalp/ ) , 21, self-taught developer and entrepreneur from Bhopal, received his grant for general career development.

Bharath H G received his grant to build a robotic system safely cleaning manholes remotely.

Sarthak Pandit ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/sarthak-pandit-357510216/ ) , an engineering student, received his grant for building a wireless drone recharging system to eliminate manual battery swaps.

Namrata Rajagopal ( http://www.linkedin.com/in/namrata-rajagopal )  received her grant for Exception Raised ( https://www.exceptionraised.com/ )  - a grants program to enable India's AI research ecosystem through funding, community, and mentorship. Check out their first cohort ( https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nr_lxnmXQRA ) .

CEDA (Center for Economic Data and Analysis) ( https://ceda.ashoka.edu.in/ )  at Ashoka University, received a grant to build the Economic Enterprises Tool, to integrate datasets delivering harmonized indicators across India's enterprises.

Saransh Duharia ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/saransh-duharia-434b00223/ )  received his grant for Garudakshak, to build a smart drone detection and neutralization system for civil use.

Aditya Gupta ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/guptaaditya2004 ) , 21, received his grant to develop a breath diagnostics tool screening for complex gut disorders non-invasively.

Farraz Mir ( https://farrazmir.replit.app/ )  received his grant for a bioinformatics automation project saving researchers time and lowering barriers to entry.

Yasmin Qureshi ( https://in.linkedin.com/in/yasmin-qureshi-9603a8289 ) , 20, received her grant for travel and career development.

Jainul Abedin ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/jainulabedin )  received his grant to scale Abyom SpaceTech ( https://abyom.com/ ) , and develop India's first reusable rocket and commercial rocket engine testing facility.

Kunjpreet Arora ( https://www.angirusind.com/ ) , 27, received his grant for Angirus, to transform plastic and industrial waste into waterproof, low-carbon bricks.

Vrinda Borkar ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/vrinda-borkar-58b9bb17b/?originalSubdomain=in ) , 30, received her grant for Wingrow Agritech ( https://wingrowagritech.com/ ) , to develop agricultural markets for small farmers.
