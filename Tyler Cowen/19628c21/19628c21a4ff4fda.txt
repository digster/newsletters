If you are willing to pay $250 or so, you can watch it in The Sphere.  From Wikipedia ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Postcard_from_Earth ) :

> Postcard from Earth is a 2023 film directed by Darren Aronofsky ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Darren_Aronofsky ) , starring Brandon Santana and Zaya Ribeiro. Created specifically to be screened at Sphere ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sphere_(venue) )  in the Las Vegas Valley ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Las_Vegas_Valley )  on the venue's 160,000 square-foot video screen, the film was shot in an 18K resolution with the Big Sky camera system. The 4D film ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/4D_film )  features 270 degrees of viewing experience, climate control, haptic ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Haptic_technology )  capabilities for the venue's seating, and scents to create an immersive environment that tells the story of life on Earth. The film is one of two entertainment features to inaugurate the Sphere, along with U2 ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/U2 ) 's concert residency ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/U2:UV_Achtung_Baby_Live_at_Sphere ) .

For visuals, and "integration with its venue," I give the film an A++.  For script a D?  (Not having read Julian Simon is the least of it.)  For soundtrack C minus?  So it is hardly the Gesamtkunstwerk you might have been hoping for.  But it was worth the money, though barely.
