Post       : David Brooks on the AI race
URL        : https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/07/david-brooks-on-the-ai-race.html
Posted     : July 17, 2025 at 10:25 pm
Author     : Tyler Cowen
Categories : Current Affairs, Science, Web/Tech

> When it comes to confidence, some nations have it and some don’t. Some nations once had it but then lost it. Last week on his blog ( https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/07/the-sputnik-vs-deepseek-moment-why-the-difference.html ) , “Marginal Revolution,” Alex Tabarrok, a George Mason economist, asked us to compare America’s behavior during Cold War I (against the Soviet Union) with America’s behavior during Cold War II (against China). I look at that difference and I see a stark contrast — between a nation back in the 1950s that possessed an assumed self-confidence versus a nation today that is even more powerful but has had its easy self-confidence stripped away.

There is much more at the NYT link ( https://nam11.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.nytimes.com%2F2025%2F07%2F17%2Fopinion%2Ftrump-america-china.html&data=05%7C02%7Ctcowen%40gmu.edu%7Cfd4e4d1137d2455df2e608ddc591cab3%7C9e857255df574c47a0c00546460380cb%7C0%7C0%7C638883952135434015%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=Ee%2BTJjABdY7jO7okiIYWX9dYNyf3mZqtpy1gB%2FLASkY%3D&reserved=0 ) .
