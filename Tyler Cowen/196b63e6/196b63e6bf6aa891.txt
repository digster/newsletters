> President Trump’s budget proposes a significant rethink of federal rental assistance programs, consolidating a number of them — and cutting them by more than $26 billion — next fiscal year. Many experts previously told The New York Times ( https://www.nytimes.com/2025/04/17/us/politics/housing-aid-hud-federal-budget.html )  that this could result in low-income Americans losing access to federal housing benefits.

Here is more from the NYT ( https://www.nytimes.com/live/2025/05/02/us/trump-budget-2026 ) .  If you are a YIMBY type, odds are you should favor this, since some of the subsidy gain likely would end up captured by landlords.
