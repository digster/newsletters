Post       : Emergent Ventures India, 13th cohort

Posted     : November 26, 2025 at 12:04 am
Author     : Tyler Cowen
Categories : Uncategorized

Khyathi Komalan ( https://khyathikomalan.github.io/ ) , sophomore at Caltech majoring in math, received her grant for career development, and to support her research applying category theory to everything from quantum physics to social relationships.

Soumil Nema ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/soumil-nema-81a56b219/ ) , cofounder of NeoVes, received his grant to develop stem cell therapies treating neurological disorders like stroke and neuropathy.

Anushka Punukollu ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/apunukollu/ ) , 17, a high school student in Canada, received her grant for SucroSoil, to repurpose sugarcane waste into hydrogels combating soil erosion in rural India.

Deev Mehta, 17, received his grant to develop a rover making farming autonomous.

Adithya Sakaray ( https://x.com/AdithyaTec ) , Steve Aldrin ( https://x.com/SteveAldrin_ ) , and Aadhithya D ( https://x.com/Aadhithya_D2003 )  received their grant for Recruitr AI ( https://recruitrai.com/ ) , to automate video interviews using AI.

Samarth K J ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/samarthkj/?originalSubdomain=in ) , 20, civil engineering student at IIT madras, received a general career development grant.

Prakyath Gowda, 25, received his grant to develop a lightweight and efficient electric vehicle battery.

Aaron Rego ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/aaronreg0/ )  received his grant for Rightful ( https://rightful.in/ ) , to help Indian families recover unclaimed financial assets.

Vatsal Hariramani ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/vatsal-hariramani-165852218/?originalSubdomain=in ) , 21, engineering student, received his grant to develop a smart and affordable neonatal incubator for remote terrain.

Rushab M received his grant to develop a jacket controlling body temperature for outdoor workers.

Sajal Deolikar ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/sajal-deolikar-15a24a235/ ) , received his grant to develop hybrid powertrains for commercial vehicles improving mileage and reducing emissions.

Mihir Maroju ( https://pidgon.com/ ) , received his grant for Open Blood, to build an open-source blood donation platform connecting blood banks nationwide.

Habel Anwar, 13, middle-schooler in Kerala, for furthering his physics Olympiad preparation, and working on advancing his physics knowledge and research.

Yash Darji ( https://vgec-rocketry-space.vercel.app/home ) , 20, engineering student, received his grant to kickstart an experimental rocketry community in Ahmedabad.

Uddhav Gupta, 15, received his grant to develop a speech therapy application for children with special needs.

Krupal Virani ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/krupal-virani/?originalSubdomain=in ) , 19, received his grant for general career development.

Shwapno Rahman, 17, received his grant to develop low-cost computers for people living below the poverty line.

Sudhir Sarnobat ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/sudhirsarnobat/ )  and Rajendra Bagwe ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/rbagwe0708/ )  received their grant for HowFrameworks, to help Indian SMEs unlock sustainable growth through a learning portal.
