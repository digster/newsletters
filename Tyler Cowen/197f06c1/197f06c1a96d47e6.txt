Post       : What should I ask Anne Appelbaum?
URL        : https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/07/what-should-i-ask-anne-appelbaum.html
Posted     : July 9, 2025 at 2:20 pm
Author     : Tyler Cowen
Categories : Books, Current Affairs, History, Political Science

Yes, I will be doing a Conversation with her.  From Wikipedia ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anne_Applebaum ) :

> Anne Elizabeth Applebaum...is an American journalist and historian. She has written about the history of Communism ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/History_of_Communism )  and the development of civil society ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Civil_society )  in Central and Eastern Europe ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_and_Eastern_Europe ) . She became a Polish ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poland )  citizen in 2013.
>
> Applebaum has worked at The Economist ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Economist )  and The Spectator ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Spectator )  magazines, and she was a member of the editorial board ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Editorial_board )  of The Washington Post ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Washington_Post )  (2002–2006). She won the Pulitzer Prize for General Nonfiction ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pulitzer_Prize_for_General_Nonfiction )  in 2004 for Gulag: A History ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulag:_A_History ) . She is a staff writer for The Atlantic ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Atlantic )  magazine, as well as a senior fellow of the Agora Institute and the School of Advanced International Studies ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_H._Nitze_School_of_Advanced_International_Studies )  at Johns Hopkins University ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johns_Hopkins_University )  .

But she has done more yet, including work on a Polish cuisine cookbook.  So what should I ask her?
