Post       : Emergent Ventures India, 14th cohort
URL        : https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/12/emergent-ventures-india-14th-cohort.html
Posted     : December 16, 2025 at 12:30 am
Author     : Tyler Cowen
Categories : Education

Avani Agarwal ( https://ohtherewegoagain.github.io/avani-agarwal-portfolio/ ) , 18, high school senior, received her grant for Synthera, to accelerate personalized medicine using AI-powered drug discovery.

Sushan Bhattarai received his grant to map archaeological sites linked to the Khasa-Malla kingdom across the Himalayas.

Utkrisht Singh Chauhan ( https://www.notion.so/InTacht-Labs-Team-23b0e993d0a84c9eb1f618d06977dbea?pvs=21 ) , 19, and Yash Chavan, 22, received their grant for InTacht ( https://www.intacht.com/ ) , to speed up and reduce costs for edge and private AI systems.

Tanuj Pandya ( https://x.com/tanuchpan?s=21 ) , 20, received his grant to build gloves bringing realistic touch to XR devices.

Dhanush Bakthavatchalam ( http://www.x.com/dhanuzch ) , 24, received his grant to build fully automated AI-driven factories for metal fabrication ( http://www.justmanufacture.com/manifesto ) .

Rashi Bhavsar ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/rashi-bhavsar-b052601b4/ )  received her grant for Algaevity, to develop an all-natural, zero-electricity mosquito-killing bio-device.

Rounak Banerjee received his grant to develop affordable technology for converting standard wheelchairs into electric wheelchairs.

Vasisht Dilip ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/vasishtdilip/ )  received his grant for Seric Steel, to turn iron ore mining waste and crop residue into steel without fossil fuels.

Mohammad Mahean Hasan ( https://devpost.com/mmaheanhasan ) , 22, studying at Minerva university, received his grant for travel and general career development.

Syed Irfan Ahmed received his grant to develop non-invasive devices monitoring posture in real time.

Kumari Anushka ( https://substack.com/@onarchetypes ) , 20, received her grant for RAD-Scan, to build a citizen-led biomarker testing system for radiation and heavy metal exposure.

Prince Rawat ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/mrprincerawat/ ) , 19, received his grant for Falken Aerospace, to build autonomous cargo UAVs for logistics.

Joy Agrawal ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/joyagrawalin/ ) , 19, sophomore at University of Chicago, received his grant for general career development.

Nikhil Kashyap ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/nikhil-kashyap-8996181a8/ ) , 20-year-old high school dropout, received his grant to build an affordable robotics kit and visual coding platform making STEM education accessible across India.

Ansh Saxena ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/anshss/?originalSubdomain=in ) , 21, received his grant for Aquanode, to build an AI-native cloud helping teams deploy and train models with lower costs.

Mateo Escalante ( https://www.mer.bio/ ) , 24, received his grant for Horus Prosthetics ( https://www.horusleg.com/en ) , to develop machine learning models generating perfectly fitting prosthetic leg sockets.

Dhruv Bathla ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/dhruv-bathla-84499b8b/ ) , founder of Ezbeat ( https://ezbeat.ai/ ) , received his grant to build a copilot preventing cardiovascular disease through early risk identification.

Rishi Rathi ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/rishi-rathi-iitb/ ) , 25, received his grant to develop a marine carbon dioxide removal solution.
