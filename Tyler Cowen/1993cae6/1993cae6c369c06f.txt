Author     : Tyler Cowen
Categories : Current Affairs, Uncategorized, Web/Tech

> Albania has become the first country in the world to have an AI minister — not a minister for AI, but a virtual minister made of pixels and code and powered by artificial intelligence.
>
> Her name is Diella, meaning sunshine in Albanian, and she will be responsible for all public procurement, Prime Minister Edi Rama said Thursday.
>
> During the summer, Rama mused ( https://www.politico.eu/article/albania-use-ai-artificial-intelligenve-join-eu-corruption/ )  that one day the country could have a digital minister and even an AI prime minister, but few thought that day would come around so quickly.

Here is the full story ( https://www.politico.eu/article/albania-apppoints-worlds-first-virtual-minister-edi-rama-diella/ ) , we will see how this develops.  Diella also is tokenized ( https://x.com/CousinGreg88/status/1966198113653452946 ) .  Via MN.
