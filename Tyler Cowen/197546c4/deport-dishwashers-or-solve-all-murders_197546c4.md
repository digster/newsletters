I understand being concerned about illegal immigration. I definitely understand being concerned about murder, rape, and robbery. What I don’t understand is being more concerned about the former than the latter.
Yet that’s exactly how the federal government allocates resources. The federal government spends far more on immigration enforcement than on preventing violent crime, terrorism, tax fraud or indeed all of these combined.
Moreover, if the BBB bill is passed the ratio will become even [more extreme.](http://marginalrevolution.com?action=user_content_redirect&uuid=d870e5049c5e86897d666a8c07316eda4922574cc1d22d1bb1ea29a57acfcc2b&blog_id=42693868&post_id=90831&user_id=262258391&subs_id=225460721&signature=dad8c8fee7e59cfdd418a2bbb2b5d6f9&email_name=new-post&user_email=ishan.mail@gmail.com&encoded_url=aHR0cHM6Ly94LmNvbS9EYXZpZF9KX0JpZXIvc3RhdHVzLzE5MzEwODIzOTQ5NDMwMjExOTI&email_id=278e75ba99e28baf1819559f704cdedb) (sere also [here](http://marginalrevolution.com?action=user_content_redirect&uuid=cdba724aa22475731946b33c934398f16d3ade26df7c6b573e99c7ce07f93738&blog_id=42693868&post_id=90831&user_id=262258391&subs_id=225460721&signature=9b950f1d870e8d5ae5c722b0445b06af&email_name=new-post&user_email=ishan.mail@gmail.com&encoded_url=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbW1pZ3JhdGlvbmltcGFjdC5jb20vMjAyNS8wMi8yMS9nb3AtYnVkZ2V0LXJlY29uY2lsaWF0aW9uLXBsYW4v&email_id=278e75ba99e28baf1819559f704cdedb)):
Don't make the mistake of thinking that immigration enforcement is about going after murderers, rapists and robbers. It isn't. Indeed, it's the opposite. ICE’s [“Operation At Large”](http://marginalrevolution.com?action=user_content_redirect&uuid=640f8ef5e76fa6f5b0af729d665d9d74a6ae021db7f55f5bd8f5ef4ef3456d9d&blog_id=42693868&post_id=90831&user_id=262258391&subs_id=225460721&signature=839454a79ec73f50c7951354af70ceee&email_name=new-post&user_email=ishan.mail@gmail.com&encoded_url=aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubmJjbmV3cy5jb20vcG9saXRpY3MvanVzdGljZS1kZXBhcnRtZW50L2ljZS1vcGVyYXRpb24tdHJ1bXAtZm9jdXMtaW1taWdyYXRpb24tcmVzaGFwZS1mZWRlcmFsLWxhdy1lbmZvcmNlbWVudC1yY25hMTkzNDk0&email_id=278e75ba99e28baf1819559f704cdedb) for example has moved thousands of law enforcement personnel at Homeland Security, the FBI, DEA, and the U.S. Marshals away from investigating violent crime and towards immigration enforcement.
I'm not arguing against border enforcement or deporting illegal immigrants but [rational people understand tradeoffs.](http://marginalrevolution.com?action=user_content_redirect&uuid=4d69adf8d85e8e7c267eb13f365bb656b7ac8115253cc2b73ddc3e318d497285&blog_id=42693868&post_id=90831&user_id=262258391&subs_id=225460721&signature=2044b279ae52f3b1d64dc0c7dd34ccb9&email_name=new-post&user_email=ishan.mail@gmail.com&encoded_url=aHR0cHM6Ly94LmNvbS9TdGVwaGVuTS9zdGF0dXMvMTkzMTQzNzA4NTk3OTczNDEwMA=&email_id=278e75ba99e28baf1819559f704cdedb) Do we really want to spend billions to deport dishwashers from Oaxaca while rapes in Ohio committed by US citizens go under-investigated?
[Almost half of the murders](http://marginalrevolution.com?action=user_content_redirect&uuid=4a29f29e0b88314ad911ff32fdf5b632496346274bd6b5a998ef99ee184b980a&blog_id=42693868&post_id=90831&user_id=262258391&subs_id=225460721&signature=89d34c21fa405801b6bf5dae7bb5c1c6&email_name=new-post&user_email=ishan.mail@gmail.com&encoded_url=aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubXVyZGVyZGF0YS5vcmcvcC9ibG9nLXBhZ2UuaHRtbA=&email_id=278e75ba99e28baf1819559f704cdedb) in the United States go unsolved (42.5% in 2023). So how about devoting some of the $167 billion extra in the BBB bill to hiring [more police, deterring more crime](http://marginalrevolution.com?action=user_content_redirect&uuid=75899b17c4a176789fcf36fa0c392cbb9c9a8a2614609aaa6e7b05eedd41fc85&blog_id=42693868&post_id=90831&user_id=262258391&subs_id=225460721&signature=758c54c860e52a27f20e6ee10d440ff8&email_name=new-post&user_email=ishan.mail@gmail.com&encoded_url=aHR0cHM6Ly9tYXJnaW5hbHJldm9sdXRpb24uY29tLz9zPW1vcmUrcG9saWNl&email_id=278e75ba99e28baf1819559f704cdedb) and to use Conor Friedersdorf's slogan, [solve all murders](http://marginalrevolution.com?action=user_content_redirect&uuid=d78239a1e84a05ebca486e5d6792a33f3c74d6dac82b7d8ca9765fccf598f669&blog_id=42693868&post_id=90831&user_id=262258391&subs_id=225460721&signature=258bdc7c7639483d9e6091c6808349e2&email_name=new-post&user_email=ishan.mail@gmail.com&encoded_url=aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhlYXRsYW50aWMuY29tL2lkZWFzL2FyY2hpdmUvMjAyMS8wOC9pbnN0ZWFkLW9mLWRlZnVuZC10aGUtcG9saWNlLXNvbHZlLWFsbC1tdXJkZXJzLzYxOTY3Mi8&email_id=278e75ba99e28baf1819559f704cdedb). [Back of the envelope calculations](http://marginalrevolution.com?action=user_content_redirect&uuid=66e3d65d72bf0d2e83a6d6aa970608f891e8a5803cf4f6b12d9a6e1e4eee1b13&blog_id=42693868&post_id=90831&user_id=262258391&subs_id=225460721&signature=c204dbb21d077e06073f2b79f4274644&email_name=new-post&user_email=ishan.mail@gmail.com&encoded_url=aHR0cHM6Ly9jaGF0Z3B0LmNvbS9zaGFyZS82ODQ2MGU3ZC1hMWJjLTgwMGYtYmMyNS01ZGMxNjZhNjIxMWM&email_id=278e75ba99e28baf1819559f704cdedb) suggest that $20 billion annually could fund roughly 150 k additional officers, a ~22 % increase, deterring some ~2 400 murders, ~90 k violent crimes, and ~260 k property crimes each year. Seems like a [better dea](http://marginalrevolution.com?action=user_content_redirect&uuid=66e3d65d72bf0d2e83a6d6aa970608f891e8a5803cf4f6b12d9a6e1e4eee1b13&blog_id=42693868&post_id=90831&user_id=262258391&subs_id=225460721&signature=c204dbb21d077e06073f2b79f4274644&email_name=new-post&user_email=ishan.mail@gmail.com&encoded_url=aHR0cHM6Ly9jaGF0Z3B0LmNvbS9zaGFyZS82ODQ2MGU3ZC1hMWJjLTgwMGYtYmMyNS01ZGMxNjZhNjIxMWM&email_id=278e75ba99e28baf1819559f704cdedb)l.
