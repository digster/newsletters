Post       : Resources for Teaching Tariffs
URL        : https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/08/resources-for-teaching-tariffs.html
Posted     : August 29, 2025 at 7:19 am
Author     : Alex Tabarrok
Categories : Current Affairs, Economics, Education

Trump has put tariffs on the economics agenda in a way that hasn't been true for decades. As a new semester of principles of economics ( https://marginalrevolution.com/our-textbook )  begins, here are some resources for teaching tariffs.

Comparative Advantage ( https://youtu.be/4rUfoU04QJM?si=q16R01l1yaGl8LlP )  (video)

The Microeconomics of Tariffs and Protectionism ( https://mru.org/courses/principles-economics-microeconomics/tariffs-quotas-protectionism-definition )  (video)

Why Do Domestic Prices Rise with Tariffs? ( https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/04/why-do-domestic-prices-rise-with-tarriffs.html )  (post)

Trade Diversion (Why Tariffs on More Countries Can Be Better) ( https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/08/why-tariffs-on-more-countries-can-be-better.html )  (post)

Manufacturing and Trade ( https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/04/manufacturing-and-trade.html )  (post), Manufacturing Went South ( https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/05/manufacturing-went-south.html )  (post) and Tariffs Hurt Manufacturing ( https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2024/10/tariffs-hurt-manufacturing.html )  (post)

Three Simple Rules of Trade Policy ( https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/02/three-simple-principles-of-trade-policy.html )  (Lerner symmetry, imports are inputs, trade balances and capital flows; post)

Tariffs and Taxes ( https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/08/taxes-and-tariffs.html )  (post), Tariffs are a Terrible Way to Raise Revenue ( https://www.economicforces.xyz/p/6-reasons-why-tariffs-are-a-terrible )  (Albrecht post) and Consistency on Tariffs and Taxes ( https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/03/consistency-on-taxes-and-tariffs.html )  (post)

Globalization: Economics, Culture and the Future ( https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PPZZQ0fmI2Y&t=556s )  (video)

The Tariff Tracker ( https://www.pricinglab.org/tariff-tracker/ ) , great source for real time tracking of prices such as below (the data can be downloaded):

https://marginalrevolution.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/tariff-tracker.png
