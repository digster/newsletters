Yes, I will be doing a Conversation with him.  He is a Professor of History at Northwestern ( https://history.northwestern.edu/people/faculty/core-faculty/paul-gillingham.html ) , specializing in Mexico and to some extent the Caribbean.  He has translated a Mexican book on Edgar Allan Poe ( https://www.amazon.com/Poe-Trauma-%C3%93scar-Xavier-Altamirano/dp/1787073327/ref=sr_1_1?crid=3HMEACTWFMZLT&dib=eyJ2IjoiMSJ9.m9Yt_WzZZgs2wGnSI30A1aox4JeL4qdYpokXMydXh2U.mG_4VmdO5hym-8nnqLccBl38emItFs4OWtRCjRpSZAI&dib_tag=se&keywords=Poe%3A+The+Trauma+of+an+Era&qid=1770129139&sprefix=poe+the+trauma+of+an+era%2Caps%2C110&sr=8-1 ) .  I am learning a good deal from his new 700 pp. book Mexico: A 500-Year History ( https://www.amazon.com/Mexico-500-Year-History-Paul-Gillingham/dp/0802164846/ref=sr_1_1?crid=2GL12LV8NB6B8&dib=eyJ2IjoiMSJ9.utLhtnsVCdKxy6gR2_JjeWvFeSSBn7LTznCA537r8muMAU-aF3owldo3lCbMDJYL3T1SwSQGRnAcFv3v5lFwlnBgp4pcOt-EpK88bSbw933EJkgA-RCV5EVgl-gYLzn7icu8lp0uddxv32I4IUIcI_D_YouHQfhE5lwSqWKhnPAKAiGxuy6vM25A5Cc6APmHK4l8jh1sCJcHBHNk3EBEirPPcinm1wkzgYhBtmPNvxI.briugTxiM2o49ZDyErq8CmOIwrqpJq7U_R0-V1in4og&dib_tag=se&keywords=paul+gillingham&qid=1770129411&sprefix=paul+gillin%2Caps%2C108&sr=8-1 ) , and I very much like his earlier work on Mexico and violence.  Here is an NYT review of the new book ( https://www.nytimes.com/2025/11/19/books/review/mexico-paul-gillingham.html ) .
