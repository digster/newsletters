Post       : Markets are forward-looking
URL        : https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/06/markets-are-forward-looking.html
Posted     : June 18, 2025 at 2:36 am
Author     : Tyler Cowen
Categories : Data Source, Economics, History, Uncategorized

> LPL Financial analyzed 25 major geopolitical episodes ( https://www.lpl.com/research/blog/israel-iran-conflict-stocks-face-another-geopolitical-test.html ) , dating back to Japan’s 1941 attack on Pearl Harbor. “Total drawdowns around these events have been fairly limited,” Jeff Buchbinder, LPL’s chief equity strategist, wrote in a research note on Monday. (Full recoveries often “take only a few weeks to a couple of months,” he added.)
>
> Deutsche Bank analysts drew a similar conclusion: “Geopolitics doesn’t normally matter much for long-run market performance,” Henry Allen, a markets strategist, wrote in a note on Monday.
>

Here is the NYT piece ( https://www.nytimes.com/2025/06/17/business/dealbook/markets-war-israel-iran.html ) , via the excellent Kevin Lewis ( https://www.nationalaffairs.com/blog/detail/findings-a-daily-roundup/immigration-numbers ) .
