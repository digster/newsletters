Post       : Emergent Ventures India, 12th cohort

Author     : Tyler Cowen

Harish Ashok ( https://www.harishashok.com/ ) , 16, received his grant to build a multi-purpose rover.

Dev Patel ( https://dev-patel.com/ ) , economist, received his grant to expand his method ( https://dev-patel.com/floods/floodmethodology.html )  combining machine learning and geophysics to detect and forecast floods across Indian villages.

Saurabh Chandra ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/saurabhchandra/ ) , Pranay Kotasthane ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/pranaykotas/ ) , and Khyati Pathak ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/khyatihp/ )  received their grant for Puliyabaazi Hindi Podcast ( https://www.puliyabaazi.in/ ) , to expand and develop articles ( https://www.puliyabaazi.in/s/tippani )  and video ( https://www.youtube.com/@puliyabaazi )  formats in simple, conversational Hindi.

Vishrant Dave ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/vishrant-dave-3107ba193/ ) , Ayush Ranjan ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/ayranjan?utm_source=share&utm_campaign=share_via&utm_content=profile&utm_medium=ios_app )  and Prateesh Awasthi ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/prateesh-awasthi-4a5215109/ )  received their grant for Armatrix, hyper-redundant robotic arms for inspection and maintenance in hard-to-reach and hazardous industrial environments.

Akhil Reddy K ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/akhil-reddy-44767719a/ )  received his grant for Livestockify ( https://livestockify.com/ ) , to develop solar-powered IoT sensors for real-time poultry disease and health monitoring.

Mohil Ahuja ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/mohil-ahuja-ba559722b/?originalSubdomain=in ) , 19, received his grant to develop a low-cost algae-based air purification system addressing indoor pollution.

Reivanth Kanagaraj ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/reivanth-kanagaraj-1a3976192/?originalSubdomain=in )  received his grant for ColourCryption, to create low-cost anti-counterfeiting solutions using fluorescent inks.

Kaviraj Prithvi ( https://in.linkedin.com/in/kaviraj-prithvi-800327192 ) , 23, received his grant for uDot ( https://www.linkedin.com/company/udot-braille-tech-pvt-ltd ) , to build a tactile display enabling blind students to study STEM.

Tawheed Rahman, highschooler, received his grant to build a low-cost prosthetic robotic arm.

Keya Shah ( https://x.com/thekeyashah ) , 22, received her grant to develop a prosthetics solution in Bangalore.

Sanjay Ganguli ( https://www.instagram.com/_sanprasad/ )  received his grant to acquire equipment for documenting wildlife stories ( https://www.youtube.com/@Sanjaywildlife/videos )  from India.

Avhijit Nair ( https://www.linkedin.com/company/hydroplas-tech/ ) , 26, received his grant for HydroPlas Tech ( https://hydroplastech.com/ ) , to produce graphene from waste plastic.

Rain Regious ( https://bevisioneers.world/profile/rain-rejius-m/ )  received his grant for TRIPd, to develop a wearable revolutionizing personal temperature control through thermoreceptors.

Pragyaan Gaur, 18, received his grant to build technology reducing industrial sulfur dioxide emissions.

Wajih ur Rehman ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/wajih-ur-rehman-rahman-881220143/ )  received his grant to develop an aerosol-technology-based solution to curb air pollution in Pakistan.

Rakshith Aloori ( https://rakshithxaloori.com/ ) , 25, received his grant to build desalination machines solving the water crisis.

Prashansa Tripathi ( https://www.linkedin.com/in/prashansatripathi/ ) , doctoral student at IIT Jodhpur, received travel and conference support to attend the cognitive neuroscience skills training program at Cambridge University.
