Post       : Privatize Federal Land!
URL        : https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/06/privatize-federal-land.html
Posted     : June 29, 2025 at 7:16 am
Author     : Alex Tabarrok
Categories : Current Affairs, Economics, Law

I’ve long advocated ( https://marginalrevolution.com/?s=sell+federal+land )  selling off some federal land—an idea that reliably causes mass fainting spells among the enlightened. How could we possibly part with our national patrimony, our land, our sacred wilderness? Calm down. Most of this “public land” is never used by the public. Selling some of it would actually make it more accessible and useful to real people.

Moreover, most of you wailing about selling some Federal land are probably very happy we sold the "public" airwaves for your private cell phone use. Privatizing the airwaves made them much more useful to the public. (Thank you Reed!).

AEI has an excellent map ( https://www.aei.org/research-products/report/senator-lee-bill-analysis/ )  of the lands that could be sold and developed in the Mike Lee bill ( https://www.energy.senate.gov/services/files/DF7B7FBE-9866-4B69-8ACA-C661A4F18096 ) . Here's their conclusion:

> The data show a significant opportunity. Our analysis finds that developing just 135-180 square miles of the most suitable BLM land, a minuscule fraction of the total, could yield approximately 1 million new homes over ten years. This would substantially address the West’s housing shortage while generating an estimated $15 billion for the U.S. Treasury from land sales.
