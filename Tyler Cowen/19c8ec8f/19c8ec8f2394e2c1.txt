Translated from the German by Cyril Welch.

Periodically I am asked if I have read Being and Time, and I always give the same response: "I have looked at every page."

I also have spent time with it in German, though not for every page.  But have I read it?  Read it properly?  Can anyone?

Is the book worth some study?  Yes.  But.

People, this volume ( https://www.amazon.com/Being-Time-Translation-Martin-Heidegger/dp/0300282729/ref=sr_1_1?crid=1UUYIRAX4WVSE&dib=eyJ2IjoiMSJ9.x3sT0czhlK5t9ZBmf4T4jm8Jd3YbwJ9oZH4DysqLZALGjHj071QN20LucGBJIEps.WbopT-IR7O9NXu-giZ1oCdOnDq_7eRhu4yZ7k8ipCmA&dib_tag=se&keywords=Being+and+Time%3A+An+Annotated+Translation&qid=1771806771&sprefix=being+and+time+an+annotated+translation%2Caps%2C123&sr=8-1 )  is the best chance you are going to get.
