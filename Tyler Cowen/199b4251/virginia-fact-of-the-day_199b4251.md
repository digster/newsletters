Virginia saw one of the smallest increases in electricity costs in the nation this past year, per new federal
[data], but it could get even higher as data centers proliferate statewide...By the numbers: Between May 2024 and May 2025, the average cost of electricity for residential customers in Virginia rose about 3%, from 14.95 cents to 15.41 cents, according to the U.S. Energy Information Administration.
The negative media bias aside, I find this encouraging. OK, "...could get even higher," but the nationwide average was 6.5%.
