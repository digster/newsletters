Instead of re-opening Alcatraz as super-max prison we should build a statute to America. I suggest “Lady Liberty of the Pacific". The spirit of Columbia ala John Gast’s American Progress carrying a welcoming beacon-lamp in her raised right hand and a coiled fibre-optic cable in her left representing Bay Area technology.
