Exit Interviews is a new podcast run by David Piegaro ( https://www.njexitinterviews.com/ ) .  I am honored to be one of the first few guests, along with Chris Christie.  Think of this session as "Tyler Cowen as regional thinker."  Almost 100% fresh material, not to mention some trolling directed at Central and South Jersey, Philly too.  Here is my episode ( https://www.njexitinterviews.com/episodes/tyler-cowen ) .

Definitely recommended, and let us hope that David Remnick gets on soon to defend the honor of River Vale vs. Hillsdale in Bergen County...
