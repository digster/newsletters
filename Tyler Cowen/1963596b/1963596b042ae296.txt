Yes, I will be doing a Conversation with him.  John might be the smartest person I know about the energy sector and also about philanthropy.  Here is the opening of his Wikipedia entry ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_D._Arnold ) :

> John Douglas Arnold (born 1974) is an American philanthropist, former Enron ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enron )  executive, and founder of Arnold Ventures LLC ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arnold_Ventures_LLC ) , formerly the Laura and John Arnold Foundation ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laura_and_John_Arnold_Foundation ) . In 2007, Arnold became the youngest billionaire in the U.S. His firm, Centaurus Advisors, LLC, was a Houston ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Houston ) -based hedge fund specializing in trading energy products that closed in 2012. He now focuses on philanthropy ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philanthropy )  through Arnold Ventures LLC. Arnold is a board member of Breakthrough Energy Ventures ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Breakthrough_Energy_Ventures )  and since February 2024, is a member of the board of directors of Meta ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meta_Platforms ) .

So what should I ask him?
