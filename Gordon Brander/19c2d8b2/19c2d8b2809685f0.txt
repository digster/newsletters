Sam Arbesman had me on the Orthogonal Bet podcast [ https://substack.com/redirect/b07f364f-8e87-4941-a6e9-cba31dd87fce?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ] recently. We chat about scenario planning, and my work on Deep Future [ https://substack.com/redirect/2dba5df8-d37b-44d2-9600-fdd181049122?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], an AI scenario engine that stress-tests your strategies against simulated futures.
You can listen to the episode on YouTube [ https://substack.com/redirect/cffc7a2a-65d8-4b66-ba62-1aee09d5379a?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], Spotify [ https://substack.com/redirect/cb8fb3b1-a653-4342-9e4c-5014da5d4f71?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], Apple Podcasts [ https://substack.com/redirect/a0a069d7-c6b2-4842-945d-3d9bc64914df?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], and anywhere else you might get podcasts [ https://substack.com/redirect/4f3e533a-2ceb-453c-85d1-928a025200d3?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ].
Our conversation covers a lot of ground: we discuss scenario planning, how it works, the difference between risk and uncertainty [ https://substack.com/redirect/d86d6071-c7f3-4c3a-a361-d6add3a14e1d?j=eyJ1IjoiOG81NG4ifQ.6oeetudtJEh-zO1onUJvtadWEdbodFIw0h4xbugrh1o ], why it can be useful to hold multiple futures in superposition, and how AI might improve our strategic thinking.
